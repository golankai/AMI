Popular British actress, most known for her breakout role as Hermione in the Harry Potter movie franchise as a child actress. She has continued to get notable roles after the end of Harry Potter, such as The Perks of Being a Wallflower, The Bling Ring and Beauty and the Beast.
She is an ambassador for the UN and for women rights, has done some modelling work for well known fashion labels, and is on the board of Kering, and luxury brand group.
Her birthday is on the 15th of April, 1990, and she is currently 31 years old. She has a bachelor's degree in English Literature from Brown University.
