Mick Jagger is an iconic British musician. Mick Jagger is best known around the world for his leading role as the front man of the British Rock band The Rolling Stones. He has received a very impressive amount of awards not only for his long spanning music career, but also for his humanitarian efforts. Mick Jagger also has famous children, including Bianca Jagger. Sir Mick Jagger is almost 80 years old. His music career has inspired millions of civilians and artists around the globe. He was the muse for the Adam Levine song moves like Jagger.
