Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor known for Sherlock in Sherlock Holmes. He was born in London on the 19th of July 1976. He studied at University of Manchester and London Academy or Music and Dramatic Act. He has won numerous awards such as ""Emmy Award"", ""BAFTA Award"" and ""Golden Globe Award"". His parents are both actors and his grandfather was a submarine commander. His full name is Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch. He was married in  2015 and has three children. He supports numerous charities.
