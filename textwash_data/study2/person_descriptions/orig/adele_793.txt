Adele Adkins was born on 5th May 1988 in London.  She has a English mother and a Welsh father.  She began singing when she was 4.  She was born in the Tottenham area of London and grew up in Brighton with her mum before moving back to London when she was 11.  She began her singing career aged 19 and has had massive success with her songs, such as Make You Feel My Love and Chasing Pavements.  

She is divorced from her husband Simon Konecki and has a son Angelo.  

She has released 3 albums;19,21 and 25 and has won various awards such as A Grammy and a Brit award for Best Album of the year for 21.
