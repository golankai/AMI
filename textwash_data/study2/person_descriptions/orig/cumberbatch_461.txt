Benedict Cumberbatch is an English Actor who has starred in many TV programmes and films.
He was born in England in 1976, and one of his greatest accomplishments is being awarded a CBE by the queen for his services to charities and the performing arts world.
Most known for playing Stephen Hawking in the 2004 film Hawking. He also played Sherlock Holmes in the BBC television series.
He is a husband and father of three children also.
Also recent involvement in the Marvel films playing DR STRANGE.
