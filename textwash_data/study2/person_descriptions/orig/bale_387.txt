Christian Bale is a British (Welsh) actor famous for playing Batman in the hit movies. He is well known to commit to his roles, both gaining and losing drastic amounts of weight for different roles. He has won multiple awards including an Academy Award and two Golden Globe Awards during his career.  He is married and has two children. His acting dreams started when he was quite young and he is very successful. He lives in Los Angeles, USA, and has been there since the 1990s.
Looks-wise he has dark hair and often has a beard and moustache.
