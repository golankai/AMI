Daniel Wroughton Craig is British Actor born in Chester on March 2nd 1968, he is the son of Carol Olivia (an Art Teacher) and Timothy John Wroughton Craig. Craig has an older Sister named Lea. He is best known for playing James Bond in the Eponymous film series. Beginning with Casino Royale in 2006 which brought him international fame. Casino Royale premiered on 14th November 2006 and grossed $594,239,066 worldwide. He was nominated for a Bafta award for Best Actor in Casino Royale. Craig first appeared on screen in the 1992 film The power of one playing Afrikaner.
