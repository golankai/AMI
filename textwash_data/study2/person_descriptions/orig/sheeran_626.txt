Ed Sheeran, (full name Edward Christopher Sheeran) is a singer-songwriter who comes from England
He is also a record producer, a musician and an actor. 
He has sold more than 150 million records worldwide and is one of the world's best selling music artists.
He has a distinctive look, sporting ginger hair, a cheeky smile and sometimes thick black glasses.
His debut album was called 'plus' and was released in September 2011 and his second album called multiply was released in June 2014.
Ed has also appeared on television and played a small part in the hit television series 'Game of Thrones'.
