Mock Jagger is also in fact a sir, and was born in 1943. He is a member of the Rolling Stones and has had a mammoth career, spanning six decades. He is well known for his energetic amazing live performances.  He was born in Kent and began studying at the London School of Economics at age 18, but left to be part of the Rolling Stones.  He has only been married and divorced once but has had many relationships, including to Marianne Faithfull and Marsha Hunt. He has eight children with a number of different women and in 2014 became a great grandfather.
