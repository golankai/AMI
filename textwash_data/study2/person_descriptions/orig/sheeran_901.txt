Ed  Sheeran is a young music musician, who writes his own songs, plays a guitar very well, is a record producer, and actor. He was born in 1991, on the 17th of February. He was born in Halifax, West Yorkshire.  His most successfull record to date is called, `Thinking out loud`, which sold over a million copies. His first single was released in the year, 2014, called, ` Sing`, which led Ed Sheeran into global stardom. Sheeran has sold over a 150 million records.  Ed Sheeran is married to his childhood sweetheart, Cherry.
