Christian Bale is an English actor who has starred in many films including Batman in which he played the title role. He made his acting debut at the age of 13 in Steven Spielberg's Empire of the Sun. Other films he has been in include American Psycho, Prestige and American Hustle. He has won two awards, best supporting actor for The Fighter in 2011 and a Golden Globe award in 2019 for best performance by an actor in a motion picture for his role in Vice. He is married with two children and currently lives in America.
