Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula1 driver for Mercedes. He is 36 years old and has won many racing trophies in his time racing. Hamilton has been interested in cars and driving since he was very young, at the age of 6 his father bought him a go-cart and this was the beginning of a lifelong passion and career for Lewis. Lewis was bullied throughout his childhood for various things, including his race, while this was an awfully traumatic experience for him it has made him who he is today- a widely successful, wealthy, talented, and handsome celebrity.
