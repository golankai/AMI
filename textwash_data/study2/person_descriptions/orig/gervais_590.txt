Ricky Gervais is stand-up comedian, actor, writer,  director and producer.  His most famous role is that of David Brent, the office manager in The Office tv series, and Andy Millman in the series Extras.  But, before he turned to comedy he wanted to be a pop star and had a group called Seona Dancing, who weren't that bad, then managed the group Suede for a while.
He's also made TV shows starring his friends Carl Pilkington and Stephen Merchant.  Most recently his show Afterlife on Netflix has become very popular.
He's starred in several films including both Night at the Museum films and Stardust.
