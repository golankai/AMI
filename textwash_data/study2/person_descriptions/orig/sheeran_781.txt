Ed Sheeran is an English singer-songwriter who is 30 years old, born on the 17th February 1991. His song which 'made him famous' was the single 'A Team' which was released In 2011. He started off playing in small venues and busking on the streets. He had always loved music from a very young age and started playing the guitar when he was just 11 years old. He has collaborated with many famous artists including Stormzy with their hit 'Shape of you'. He also has one child, his daughter, with his wife. He has also just released tickets to his next tour 'the mathematics tour' in 2022. He is one of the worlds best selling music artists !
