Adele is a British female singer. She has garnered international success, with her debut album 19 and follow-up 21 bringing financial and chart success. The latter is the best selling album of the century so far. Her other credits include writing the theme tune for the James Bond movie Skyfall. 
She was born in London, and has one son. Her personal life has informed her artistry, with her album titles reflecting her age at the time they were written. 21 was in part inspired by a break up with a former partner.
