Craig began acting in school plays at the age of six, making his debut in the Frodsham Primary School production of Oliver. Craig appeared in his first screen role in 1992, playing an Afrikaner in The Power of One. In 2005, Craig was contacted by Eon Productions to portray James Bond. He is a philantropist. He is also active in politics, he has been outspoken about his opposition to brexit. Craig married actress Fiona Loudon, the union gave birth to a daughter before they divorced. Craig and actress Rachel Weisz got married in 2011. Craig was appointed to the honorary rank of Commander in the Royal Navy.
