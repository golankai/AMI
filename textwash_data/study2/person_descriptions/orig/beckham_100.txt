David Beckham is a top English footballer who had a 20 year career which ended when he retired in May 2013.  He is now 46 years old and is still very much in the public eye due to his role as a UNICEF ambassador and also as one of the owners of Inter Miama CF.

He is married to Victoria Beckham who is more famously known as Posh Spice of The Spice Girls and are known as ""Posh and Becks"".  Victoria's nickname for David is ""Golden Balls"" - she named him this for his comeback after the 1998 World Cup. 
They have been married since 1999 and have four children.   

David is very fashion conscious and always wears stylish clothes.  He also has a large number of tattoos.
