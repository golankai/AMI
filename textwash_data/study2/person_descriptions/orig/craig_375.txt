Chester (UK) born Daniel Wroughton Craig was born in 1968. He best known as playing James Bond in the James Bond films. Firstly he was in Casino Royale in 2006, and since then he has been in three more instalments, with the next one to be released later this year. 

He once attended the National Youth Theatre and graduated the Guildhall School of Music and Drama in 1991. He has been involved in multiple charities, one uses street theatre to address social issues in Kenya (S.A.F.E), another is the opportunity network in New York which provides access to education for low-income students.
