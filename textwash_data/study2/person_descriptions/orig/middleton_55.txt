Kate Middleton, also known as Duchess of Cambridge, was born on January 9th 1982. She is married to Prince Williams, the oldest son of Prince Charles and Princess Diana, he is second in line to the throne after his father Charles. Together Kate and William have 3 children: Prince George who is eight years old and third in line for the throne, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis. She graduated from St. Andrews University (where she met Prince William) in 2005 with an Art History degree. She has 2 siblings: Pippa Middleton and James William Middleton. Kate and William started dating in 2003, but in 2007 after some difficulties, they broke up but shortly after they got together. The pair announced engagement in 2010 and they got married in 2011.
