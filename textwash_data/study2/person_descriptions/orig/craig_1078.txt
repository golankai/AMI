Born in Chester in England Daniel Craig is an 53 year old well-known British actor who played the role of James Bond in the latest James Bond movies. Has had roles in many other movies too. He starred in the film the girl with the dragon tattoo as well as the film knives out. Most of his films focus on action and crime. He is currently married to Rachel Wiesz and has a son with her as well as a daughter from his first marriage. He began acting in school plays from the age of 6 carrying on taking part in plays and theatre productions during his time at school. His first role was in the film The power of one in 1992.
