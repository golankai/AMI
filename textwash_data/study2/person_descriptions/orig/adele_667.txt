Adele is 33 years old from London and rose to fame at 19. 
Adele is a world wide celebrity and a celebrity in her own right, selling over 120 million records. She is recognised for her quirky personality, and her beautiful voice. Adele has received many awards and has even been published in 'The Guinness Book of Records'. 
Adele often speaks of the Spice Girls as her influence 'making her what she is today'. 
 Adele had many tours, but sadly the tours were cut short in London due to Vocal Injuries... She also stated that it would be the last ever tour.
