Her given name is Joanne Rowling and she is 56 years old. She was born in Yale, Gloucestershire in England. She is a British author and best known for her work writing Harry Potter. She has won multiple awards and sold more than 500 million copies. She has three children. Rowling also writes crime fiction under the name Robert Galbraith. 
Her idea for Harry Potter was conceived in 1990 on a train from Manchester to London. She lived in relative poverty until the first novel of the series which was published in 1997.
