Judi Dench is an english actress, best known for her role as 'M' in the James Bond movies. She's a Dame, awarded by the British Empire. She is thought to be one of the best actresses in Britain. She was born on the 9th of December 1934, aged 86 and still acting to this day. During her years of acting she has received multiple awards such as Best Supporting Actress. She's been awarded multiple honorary degrees throughout Britain and even getting fellowships such as one from the British Film Institute.
