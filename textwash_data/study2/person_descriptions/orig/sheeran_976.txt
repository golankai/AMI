Ed Sheeren is an English singer and songwriter, also a record producer. He has sold more than 150 million records worldwide. He is considered one of the best selling artists of our time. He was born in 1991; in Halifax, West Yorkshire in England. He has been active since 2004 in the music industry. He has no child. Sheeren began when he sang at a local church choir aged 4. By the time he was 11 he had learnt to play guitar and began writing his own music. He was considered a natural performer at school.
