Dame Judith Olivia Dench CH, DBE, FRSA is an English actress. Regarded as one of the best British actresses, she is thought to be a ""peerless performer"" with roles ranging from James Bond films to Shakespearean dramas. She made her professional debut in 1957 with the Old Vic Company.
She was born in York on the 9th of December 1934 and is married to Micheal wwilliams.
They have one daughter named finty and one grandson named Sam.
Her film appearances were infrequent, and included supporting roles in major films, such as James Ivory's A Room with a View (1985), before she rose to international fame as M in GoldenEye (1995), a role she continued to play in James Bond films until her final appearance in Spectre (2015).
