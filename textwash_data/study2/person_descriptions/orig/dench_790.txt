Judi Dench is a British actress. She is born the 9th of December 1934 in York. She is short and has short blond hair. She studied at the Central School of Speech and Drama. Judi Dench has performed with the Royal Shakespeare Company, the National Théâtre and at Old Vic Theatre.  She is a world known actress. She played in many movies including many James Bond. She won an Oscar for best second role, two Golden Globes. Judi Dench is a ten-time BAFTA winner. She is known to play serious characters. Her most recent film is Six Minutes to Midnight by Andy Goddard. She has a daughter named Tara. She has an eye disease and she needs an assistant on set.
