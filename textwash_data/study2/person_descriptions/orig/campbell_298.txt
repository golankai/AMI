Naomi Campbell is an English model, noted as being one of six supermodels of her generation. By the turn of the 1990s, Campbell’s reputation was solidified as one of international stardom. 
Campbell has also released a musical album, and has several acting credits, as well as having appeared in several music videos (including Freedom! 90 and In the Closet).
Her previous relationships include Flávio Briatore (an Italian businessman and former Formula One team manager), and  Vladislav Doronin (a Russian businessman and art collector).
