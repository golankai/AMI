Lewis Hamilton is a very successful Black British formula 1 racer who drives for Mercedes. He was born in 1985 making him 36). His career began with the McLaren young driver programme in 1998. Hamilton then went on to drive for McLaren between the years of 2007 and 2012. He is probably smaller than the average male heigh and has a slim build and brown hair. 

Overall Hamilton holds a joint record with another racer named Michael Schumacher who both have seven World Drivers' Championship titles each.
