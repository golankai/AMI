Mick Jagger is an English singer, songwriter, actor and film producer who was born on 26/07/1943 and is now 78 years old. Mick Jagger is the lead singer of rock band, The Rolling Stones. Mick Jagger is known as a rock legend and for his charismatic stage presence and dancing. So much so, that Maroon 5 released a song after his dancing, called 'Moves like Jagger'. Mick Jager has 8 children, and has had multiple partners, and one spouse. Mick Jagger has been with his current partner Melanie Hamrick since 2014.
