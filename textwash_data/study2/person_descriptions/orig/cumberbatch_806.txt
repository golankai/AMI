.Benedict Cumberbatch was born on a farm in the early 2000s, when owning a farm was seen as old fashioned and outdated since the majority of the public purchased their foods from the supermarkets. Benedict is known for producing the most user friendly economic and organic food in the world. His supermarket is only local, so people around the world travel to India where he was born, just to taste his crops. He developed his own technique to grow crops, allowing for mass production and allowing costs to be low for the average consumer to purchase and gain health from.
