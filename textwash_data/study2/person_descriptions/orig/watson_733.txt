Emma Watson is an English actress who starred in the Harry Potter film franchise alongside Daniel Radcliffe and Rupert Grint for the whole seven-film saga.  She is also an activist for women's rights and was a loud voice in the #METOO movement.  While working on the Harry Potter films she also continued her education which led her to graduate from the prestigious Brown university later on in 2014 with a bachelor's degree in English litriture.  In the same year, she also got a job in the UN and was appointed the Woman's Goodwill Ambassador and continues the fight for equal rights for women.  She also helped to launch the campaign HeForShe which also continues this worthy fight. Along with films and campaigns, Emma has also had a career in modeling with many fashion houses wanting to acquire her for their campaigns.
