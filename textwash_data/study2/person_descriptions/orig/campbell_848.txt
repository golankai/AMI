Naomi Campbell is known her work as a supermodel, actor and businesswoman. British born, Naomi was discovered at the age of 15 whilst window shopping in Covent Garden. Naomi was one of 6 models of her generation recognised with the title of supermodel by the fashion industry and press. Naomi was the first black model to grace the front of the American Vogue September magazine, traditionally the year's biggest and most important issue.

As well as her work as a model, Naomi has also ventured into reality Tv; as a judge on The Face, she also released an RnB album in 1994 titled ‘Baby Woman’.
