David Beckham is a London Born, English world renowned footballer, most known for his number 7 position at Manchester United. Also playing for other teams such as Real Madrid and LA galaxy just to name a couple. He is now however now retired, this is since 2013 after playing professionally for just over 20 years.
David is married to Victoria Beckham most known from being in the group spice girls. They have 4 children together.
He has been open about his struggles with OCD and how this has affected his football performance and personal life.
He has won many awards for his football performance over the years.
