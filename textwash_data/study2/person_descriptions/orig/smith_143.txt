Samuel Frederick Smith is an English singer and songwriter, his birthday is 19 May 1992, He has had 7 UK number ones and has featured on one of the James Bond theme songs for Spectre with writings on the wall, He has made 3 studio albums which have all charted on the UK top 5, his first song in the charts he featured in Latch with Disclosure, He now identifies as Non binary, he studied musical theatre.
