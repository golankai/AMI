Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor who rose to fame in the early 2000s as a child actor in his role as the lead character in the film adaptations of J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter franchise. He reprised the role for all the films, attaining international superstardom that has followed him long past the conclusion of the films several years ago. Since wrapping Harry Potter, Daniel Radcliffe has performed on Broadway in both plays and musicals and has acted in several indie films and television shows. He lives in New York City with his long-time girlfriend, also an actress.
