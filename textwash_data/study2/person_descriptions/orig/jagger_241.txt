Mick Jagger is a singer and song writer who is most famous as the lead singer of The Rolling Stones, a rock and roll band he started over 6 decades ago. He was born in 1943 in Dartford, England. He has 8 children and has been married once but had various long term partners including Jerry Hall. 
In 1989 he was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame. His band have reached the top 40 singles charts with 70 singles.
In 2003 he became Sir Mick Jagger for his services to popular music. His net worth is around $360
