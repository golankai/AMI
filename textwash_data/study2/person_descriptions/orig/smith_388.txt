Sam Smith is an English singer songwriter. They are a feminist and identify as genderqueer having come out in 2017.  They were initially brought onto the music scene through featuring on Latch by Disclosure. He is currently signed to the UK record label Capitol.

Sam smith has have had three studio albums which have all done very well selling over thirty million copies worldwide. Namely In the Lonely Hour (2014); The Thrill of It All (2017); and Love Goes (2020). The most well known song from these albums is probably Stay With Me which has had 1.288 Million streams on Spotify.
