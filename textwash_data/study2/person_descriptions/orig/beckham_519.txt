David Beckham is a famous former Football (Soccer) player who has played in the English Premier league, American MLS and nationally for England including in world cups. he is regarded as one of the highest earning football players. He Retired in 2013 after many trophy wins and now co-owns Inter Miami and Salford City.

He is also known as a model and for doing high profile advertising campaigns for brands such as Pepsi. he has also had several movie cameos and a film dedicated to his name called Bend it like Beckham in 2002 starring world famous actress Keira Knightley

He is Married to former Spice Girl Victoria Beckham (Posh Spice) and they have 4 children together.
