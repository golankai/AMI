Daniel Jacob Radcliffe, born 23rd July 1989 in Hammersmith, came to fame with the role of Harry, in the Harry potter film series, which consists of 8 films. He began acting at the age of 10 in David Copperfield and then gained the part of Harry in 2001. After he finished his very successful role as Harry, he then took the lead role in The Woman in Black film in 2012, he has also worked in the West end and Broadway. He also does alot of work for Childrens and aids charities, as well as being a big supporter for LGTBQ rights.
