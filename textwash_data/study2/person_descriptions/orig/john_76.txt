Elton John aka Reginald Dwight is a British singer and composer. He was born in 1947 in Middlesex England. At a young age he won a scholarship at the Royal Academy of music in London. His parents divorced when he was a teenager. His first break came in 1969 with his debut album Empty Sky.
Elton John teamed up with Tim Rice to work on the soundtrack to the 1994 animated feature film The Lion king. He win an Academy Award for the song ' can you feel the love tonight'. Elton John's and Tim Rice also won a Tony Award for the best musical score for Aida.
