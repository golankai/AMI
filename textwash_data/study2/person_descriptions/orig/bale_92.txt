Christian bale is a British actor who was born in Wales in 1974. He has been awarded various accolades but is probably best known for playing the multi millionaire bruce Wayne and his super hero alias batman in movies batman, batman begins, the dark Knight and the dark knight rises. He has built a reputation for his commitment and dedication to his roles where he transforms his physical appearance and for playing such versatile characters.

In his private life he lives in the United States and is married to a model who he has two children with, a boy and a girl.
