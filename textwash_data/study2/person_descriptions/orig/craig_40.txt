Daniel Craig is a leading British actor. He is best known for his portrayal of the eponymous James Bond. To date he has starred in 4 Bonds films, with the 5th to be released in the coming days. His Bond was known for showing a grittier side to the character lending a more nuanced take on the films. He had intended to stop being Bond after 3 or 4 films but was brought back both times and his films have gone onto to gross incredible sums. His other recent films have included Knives Out and Logan Lucky. He is married to Rachel Weisz another English actor.
