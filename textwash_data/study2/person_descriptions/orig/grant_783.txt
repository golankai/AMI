Hugh Grant is an English actor who is 61 years old and born on the 9th of September 1960. He is best known for his lead roles in romantic comedies. the most popular starring movies being 'Four Weddings' and 'Funeral'. He has five children with 2 different women. The films which made him well known also got him a golden globe award which would follow with many more awards to come in his career. His most recent appearance is in the film 'The undoing' in 2020 with Nicole Kidman. He has upcoming movies to be released in the next few years which everyone will look forward to see him star in.
