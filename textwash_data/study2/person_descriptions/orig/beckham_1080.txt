Born in 1975, David Beckham is an English former football player.He is married to Victoria Beckham who is now a fashion designer and used to be part of the spice girls. They have four children together, Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruise and Harper seven. Dvid Beckham is a very well-known footballer and although now retired played for the English national team over many years. He was also a footballer for Manchester United, Real Madrid and LA Galaxy amongst others. He now is co-owner and president of Inter Miami CF and co-owner of Salford City. He is often seen on the cover of magazines and perfume ads. His eldest son is engaged to Nicola Peltz.
