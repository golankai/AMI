Naomi Campbell is a former supermodel who gained a lot of success right from her teenage years as being highly in-demand for by the fashion industry. She has since made other ventures into different industries such as music where she had released her own album in the middle of the 1990s and also was a judge on the tv show ""The Face"". She appeared in Playboy magazine during her modelling years along with becoming a business entrepenuer with several beauty product lines and is also considered to be the most famous black model of all time. These days she's mostly known for her activism and charity work often being the voice of reason for the black community, LGBT community and founded the charity We Love Brasil with aims of improving lives and decreasing poverty.
