Sam Smith is a British singer and songwriter born in London in 1992. He first broke onto the music scene on 2012 on the vocals for disclosures track latch. His international success has won him various acolades including 4 grammys and brit awards. Such success seen him asked to write and appear on the theme song for 007 movie spectre. He has sold 30 million albums.

Sam Smith is genderqueer meaning he is homosexual and neither considers himself male or female but rather refers to himself as they/ them.
