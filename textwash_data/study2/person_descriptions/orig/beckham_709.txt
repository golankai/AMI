David Beckman was born in London in 1975, to a hairdresser and a kitchen fitter. He currently is co-owner and president of the Inter Miami CF and co-owner of Salford City. He also holds and Order of the British Empire. He started his career as a footballer in the 80s, but reached international fame with the Manchester United. He then moved to Real Madri in Spain, later on to Los Angeles Galaxy (where he was loaned to the AC Milan) and finally to Paris Saint-Germain. He also played for the England national team, and is the only professional player that hold national titles in four countries.
He married former Spice Girl Victoria Adams in 1999, and they have four children, Brooklyn, Romeo, Harper Seven and Cruz. He and his wife are also a fashion and lifestyle icons
