Ricky Gervais is a very funny comedian from Britain who is best known for starring and creating the mockumentary series The Office. He won 7 BAFTA awards, 5 British comedy awards, 2 Emmys, 3 Golden Globes and the Rose D'Or 2 Times. In 2007 he was number 11 on Channel 4's greatest stand ups and came number 3 on their 2010 list. In 2010 he was named as one of the world's 100 most influential people. He is now 60 years old and has become a very successful comedian after trying his luck initially in the music industry as a pop star in the 1080s. He achieved fame after the office was released as it was such a popular series.
