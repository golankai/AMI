Catherine Middleton, The Duchess of Cambridge, is Married to Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, 2nd in line to the Throne behind Prince Charles, the Prince of Wales.  Mother to 3 children, Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis making her children, 3rd, 4th and 5th in line to the English throne.  Catherine or Kate as she is known, studied History as St Andrews University, where she met Prince William.  She is a senior member of the Royal family and therefore attends Royal engagement on behalf of the Queen.  Kate has become somewhat of a fashion icon, closely watched by fashionistas across the globe.
