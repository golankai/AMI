Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor known for his TV lead role of Sherlock Holmes, his film roles as Khan in Star Trek and Dr Stephen Strange in The Marvel Cinematic Universe. He has also lent his acting skills to a variety of other shows and films, many of which are set in the past and this has led to him being well-recieved for his classical-style performances. It is universally agreed that Benedict Cumberbatch has a strange look for a leading man but while he may not possess the typical features for Hollywood's main actors, he is still considered attractive, making him an interesting addition to many films.
