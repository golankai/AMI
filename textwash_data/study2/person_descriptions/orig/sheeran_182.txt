Ed Sheeran is a singer-songwriter, producer, musician, actor, all around musical prodigy. He is one of the world's best selling artists. Ed was born in West Yorkshire and from a very young age showed great musical potential. He became famous when posting covers and original songs on YouTube until his song A-team where his fame exploded. Since then, Ed has made album after album of record-breaking and global number ones and is today one of Spotify's most streamed artists. Sheeran has had several arena tours, with another upcoming in 2022, and has collaborated with worldwide talent such as Taylor Swift, Stormzy, Eminem, BTS, Camila Cabello, and Benny Blanco among others.
