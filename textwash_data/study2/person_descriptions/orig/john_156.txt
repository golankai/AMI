Elton John is a British singer and songwriter who has had a career which has spanned nearly five decades. He is one of the biggest selling musical artists in history.

He is famous for playing the piano and wearing extravagant costumes whilst performing.

He has recorded more than 30 albums and has helped write soundtracks for musicals and films such as The Lion King. A film about his life was made in 2019 which starred Taron Egerton as John. John has also starred in other films and made cameos in movies such as Kingsman.

Elton John is openly gay and is married to his partner David Furnish. Together they have two adopted children.
