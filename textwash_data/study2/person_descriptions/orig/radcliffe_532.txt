Daniel Radcliffe is an English actor who is most known for playing Harry Potter in the Harry Potter series. He started playing the role at the age of 12 and played the character up until he was around 23 years old. After playing Harry, Daniel tried to get away from the role and has acted in a variety of different films such as The Woman in Black, Kill Your Darlings, Victor Frankenstein, Swiss Army Man and Now You See ME 2. in 2017 Daniel moved onto stage acting and starred in Equus, How to Succeed In Business Without Really Trying, The Cripple of Inishmaan and The Lifespan of a Fact. Daniel is known for his charity work and has contributed to the Demelza Hospice Care for children and the Trevor Project
