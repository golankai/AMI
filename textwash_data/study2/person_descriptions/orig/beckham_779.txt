David Beckham is an ex English professional footballer. He played for Manchester United, Real Madrid, AC Milan, Paris Saint-Germain and others. He was the captain of the England national team. David Beckham won titles in four countries: Spain, the United States, France and England. He retired in 2013. He is also famous for his family. His wife is Victoria Beckham, who is a former member of the British girl group Spice Girls which was formed in the 90'. David and Victoria have four children, three boys and one girl. David Beckham is also recognised for his tattoos. He has more than 65 tattoos, which cover almost all of his body.
