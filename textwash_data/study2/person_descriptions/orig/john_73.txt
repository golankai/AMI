Sir Elton Hercules John CH CBE  (birthname Reginald Kenneth Dwight) is a legendary English musician whose career spans over nearly 60 years.

John has received multiple awards for his many songs, including, 5 Grammy Awards, 5 Brit Awards and Two Academy Awards. John was inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame (1992) as well as the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame (1994).

John came out as bisexual in 1976 and has been openly gay since 1988. John is often praised for his work on behalf of the LGBT community, and thanked for shining light on societal issues around these subjects.
