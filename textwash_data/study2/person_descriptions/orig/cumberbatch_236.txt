Benedict Cumberbatch is a high end British actor with a wide range of roles, all of which he portrays well. From starring in television series such a Sherlock Holmes to his roles in several Marvel  films as Doctor Strange, he is definitely never short of offers of work. He has received many awards including an Emmy and a British Academy Television Award. 
In his younger years Benedict Cumberbatch attended boarding schools from the age of 8 and ended up graduating from the Victoria University of Manchester before continuing on to the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.
