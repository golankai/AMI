Sam Smith is a 29 year old British soul singer from London, England. Raised in Cambridgeshire, Smith's parents encouraged Smith’s singing passion at a young age. Smith teamed up with house duo Disclosure on the track “Latch,” in 2012 which quickly became his first major hit. At the 2015 Grammy Awards, his album ""In the Lonely Hour"" was named best pop vocal album, and “Stay with Me” won record of the year and song of the year, and was also named best new artist. As of 2021, Smith's net worth is estimated to be in the region of $40 million.
