Lewis Hamilton is an F1 racing driver from the UK. He has driven for Mercedes and McLaren in F1 and currently holds the record for most podium finishes. He is often regarded as the first black Formula One driver. He has previously been in a relationship with Nicole Scherzinger. When he was just 13 years old, told McLaren’s boss to sign him. He has a deep interest in music and once collaborated with Christina Aguilera on her song ""Pipe"" in 2018.  In June 2020, he launched The Hamilton Commission, which has the mission of ‘improving the representation of Black people in UK motorsport’.
