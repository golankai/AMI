Kate Moss is a British model who began her career at the age of just 14, when she was discovered and given her first cover shot. After being featured in Calvin Klein's Obsession advertisement campaign in the early 1990s, her career was catapulted onto the world stage, and quickly gaine a large following, During her career, she has appeared on the cover of over 300 magazines, and also starred in advertising campaigns for many of the top fashion houses. She continues to be a model and cultural icon around the world, sporting a chic, thin stature. She has been under much scrutiny and media rumours that she was too skinny and suffered from an eating disorder.
