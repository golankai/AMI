David Beckham (born in 1975) is a former football player retired in 2013 after 20 years of career. He played for Manchester United, Paris Saint Germain and Real Madrid amongst other. With Manchester United alone he won 6 times the premier league. He's married with the ex spice girl (posh spice) Victoria Beckham and they four kids. He played in three world cups and two euro cups. He's been an ambassador for Unicef for many years and also a patron on Elton John aids campaign. Despite the movie title, he didn't star in the movie ""Bend it like Beckham"" but he did appear in the movie ""Goal"" alongside with Zidane, and in the sequels ""Goal II"" and ""Goal III"". He received dozens of honours during his football career.
