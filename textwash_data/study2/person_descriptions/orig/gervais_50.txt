Ricky Gervais is an English much beloved comedian from Reading, Berkshire. Considered by many to be a national treasure, Gervais is known for his comedy, which is both satirical and intelligent. He is also an actor, director, and producer, having starred in more serious roles in many Hollywood and home-grown films, as well as in domestic TV shows. He is a popular and recognisable celebrity worldwide, getting his big break in The Office. He also attempted a pop career in the 1980's - while this flopped, he has now found his spot in the stage light and will for years to come. Born 25 June 1961, he spends his time living between the UK and USA.
