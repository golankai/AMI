Where do I begin with Benedict? Or better known by his adoring fans - Ben! He is your typical tall, dark and handsome English actor. Born in Chelsea, Benedict, first begin his acting career on a TV movie almost 20 years ago. Since then he has risen to fame and his popularity has grown exponentially with no signs of stopping. He has over 100 award nominations for his incredibly varied filmography. With iconic puns and a dazzling smile, Benedict is an incredibly witty and intelligent man that can simply charm everyone he meets.
