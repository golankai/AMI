Dame Judith Olivia Dench is an English Actress born 9th December 1984 in Heworth York. She is regarded one the best British actresses she is thought to be a 'peerless performer' with roles ranging from James Bond Films to Shakespearean dramas. she starred in Romeo and Juliet and Lady Macbeth. Although most of her work during this time was in theatre she also branched into film work and won a BAFTA Award as 'Most Promising Newcomer' a 7 time academy award nominee Dench won the academy award for Best supporting Actress for her performance as Queen Elizabeth the 1st in Shakespeare in Love in 1988.
