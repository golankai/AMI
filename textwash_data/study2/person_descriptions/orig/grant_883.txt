Hugh Grant (born in 1960) is a British actor from London. He graduated at Oxford in drama and worked in theatres for many years. In 1994 he had his cinema breakthrough with ""4 weddings and a funeral"" and had a brilliant career since then that granted him emmys and baftas awards. Among his most famous work we can annoverate Notting Hill, Bridget Jones Diary, Cloud Atlas and many more. He worked alongside some brilliant actresses like Meryl Streep and Renee Zellweger. He gained the fame of sweet and romantic actor as he mostly starred in rom-coms. He's a father of 5 and an ambassador for Marie Curie since 2001
