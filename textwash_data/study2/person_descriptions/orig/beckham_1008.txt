David Beckham's full name is David Robert Joseph Beckham OBE and he is a former English football player. He has played for various football teams in his time such as Preston North end, MAdrid, AC Milan, LA Galaxy and the English National team. He has won league title in 4 countries, England, France, United States and Spain, he is the first English player to have achieved this. He has a 20 year career and retired in 2013, during his career he won 19 football trophies. He began his professional football career with Manchester United at age 17 in 1992. He is married to Victoria Beckham. He had 2 football academy's in LA and London, unfortunately these both closed in 2009. He plans to open a travelling company that travel around the UK and other countries.
