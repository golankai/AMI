Daniel Wroughton Craig was born in Chester on 2 March 1968 currently 53. 
Craig began acting in school plays at the age of six, making his debut in the Frodsham Primary School production of Oliver. 
Daniel is an English actor. He is best known for playing James Bond in the eponymous film series, beginning with Casino Royale, which brought him international fame. As of 2015, he has starred in three more instalments, with a fifth set to be released in late 2021. Famous for his looks and charm he continues to fill out screens and is arguably the best bond to date.
