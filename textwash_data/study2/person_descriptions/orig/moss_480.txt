Kate Moss is a British supermodel who became famous during the 1990s when she largely modelled for calvin Klein. 

She has been heavily criticised for her drug use and has often appeared on the cover of British newspapers with controversy. She has also received much speculation about her weight and size zero status. She was dropped by h and m due to the drug allegations. She also lost contracts with Burberry and Chanel. She was however cleared of all drug allegations made against her .

Kate Moss designed her own clothing range which appeared in the high street store Topshop. 

She has one daughter and has appeared on the Sunday times rich list.
