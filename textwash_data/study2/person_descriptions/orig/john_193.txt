Elton John is a singer-songwriter from England. His 'real' name is Reginald Dwight. He is from south-east England and is known for his love of Watford football club. He is 74 years old. He has had countless hit singles and albums over his long career and has huge fanbases across the world. He has received hundreds of awards, including Brits, Grammys, and Golden Globes. His piano playing is legendary and he is known for great live performances. Elton was the subject of the Hollywood film Rocketman where he was played by Taron Egerton.
