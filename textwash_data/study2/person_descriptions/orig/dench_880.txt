National treasure. Esteemed and popular British actress of TV, stage and film. Is now an older actress with short grey hair.
Played M in James Bond. Is a Dame. Has won an academy award for playing Queen Elizabeth in Shakespeare in love. Best friends with Maggie Smith and have worked together in many films including Room with a View, Ladies in Lavender, Tea with Mussolini, The Best Exotic Marigold hotel. Married to fellow actor Michael Williams. Judy Dench has a visual Impairment, macular degeneration.
