Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch CBE is an English actor well known for his roles as Sherlock Holmes and Alan Turing in The Imitation Game for which he received an academy award nomination.
He studied at the University of Manchester before moving to the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art to study classical acting.
Not only is a he a star of the screen but stage as well and in 2005 he made his west end debut in Richard Eyres revival of Hedda Gabler.
He is married to Sophie Hunter whom he met on the set of Burleque Fairytales.  The couple have 3 children together.
