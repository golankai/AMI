Naomi Campbell is a 51 year old British model, actress, singer, and businesswoman from Streatham, South London. 

She studied at Italia Conti Academy stage school and appeared in music videos for Bob Marley and Culture Club before signing with Synchro modeling agency at age 15.

She became the first Black woman to appear on the cover of French 'Vogue' en route to iconic status in the modeling industry. After beginning her modeling career at age 15, Naomi Campbell became the first Black woman to appear on the cover of French Vogue and the first Black model on the cover of Time.
