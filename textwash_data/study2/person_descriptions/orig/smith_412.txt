Samuel Frederick Smith was born on 19 May 1992 in London to Frederick Smith and broker Kate Cassidy. He is an English singer and songwriter. He is gender-queer and uses they/them pronouns. He is a recipient of many awards namely four Grammy Awards, three Brit Awards, three Billboard Music Awards, and an American Music Award, as well as a Golden Globe and an Academy Award. He also identifies as a feminist. n May 2014, Smith came out to the public as gay, and acknowledged a relationship with actor and model Jonathan Zeizel. The relationship has since ended.
