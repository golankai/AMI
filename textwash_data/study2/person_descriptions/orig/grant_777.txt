Hugh Grant is a British actor who mostly takes part in comedies, mostly romantic ones. He also started in some action films like The Gentlemen and The Man from U.N.C.L.E. Hugh Grant is a quite tall man with blond hair, light eyes and most usually with a smile on his face. Some of his most recognised roles were in the following films: Four Weddings and a Funeral, Notting Hill, Music and Lyrics, Love Actually. He is also famous for dating Elizabeth Hurley for a longer period of time. Hugh Grant has five children.
