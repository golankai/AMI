Christian Bale is a well known English actor of international renown. He has featured in many blockbusters and widely acclaimed films, and has won a number of awards for his performances on screen. He famously played Patrick Bateman in American Psycho, and played Bruce Wayne in the Christopher Nolan's popular Batman trilogy.
Other notable movies he has starred in are The Prestige, The Machinist, The Fighter, The Big Short, and American Hustle.
His birthday is on the 30th of January 1974, and he is currently 47 years old.
