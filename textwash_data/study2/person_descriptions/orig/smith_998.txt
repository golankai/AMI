Sam Smith is an English singer & songwriter. Smith was born in 1992 and grew up in Cambridgeshire. Smith recorded a song for the 2015 James Bond film Spectre called Writing's on the Wall. It was the first ever James Bond theme song to reach number 1 in the UK singles chart. The song won several awards such as Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song at the 73rd Golden Globe Awards and the Academy Award for Best Original Song at the 88th Academy Awards,
 In 2014 Smith came out as gay and revealed they were in a relationship with model Jonathan Zeizel. They later dated 13 Reasons Why actor Brandon Flynn. In 2019, Smith came out as non-binary and changed their pronouns to they/them.
