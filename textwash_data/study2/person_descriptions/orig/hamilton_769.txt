Lewis Hamilton, born 7th January 1985, a British race car driver well known for his career in Formula One.
Started his career in go carting, his dream was supported by his father who worked multiple jobs to pay for his dream. It was a challenging time as they were very few ethnic racing drivers at that time. He joined the McLaren young drive programme in 1998 and eventually drove for them from 2007 to 2012, he then moved to Mercedes which is his current team. He is an incredibly successful driver, he holds the records for most wins, pole positions and podium finishes.
Away from racing he has given a lot of his time and money to charitable causes, he also has a clothing line with Tommy Hilfiger and has recently opened a vegan burger restaurant called Net burger.
