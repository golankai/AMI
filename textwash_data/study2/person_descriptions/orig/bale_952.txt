He is an actor from Wales known for multiple roles most famously the Christopher Nolan Batman series, he has worked as an actor for many years. He is also known to have a bad temper and a recording of him shouting at an assistant on set went viral in the late 2010s. More Recently he portrayed former vice president Dick Channey. He has won 2 best supporting actor Oscars one for ""The Fighter"" and one for ""American Hustle"". He has been married to model Sibi Blažić since January 2000. In 2008 he was arrested after an altercation with his mother and sister.
