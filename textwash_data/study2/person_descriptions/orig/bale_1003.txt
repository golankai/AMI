Christian Bale, born to entrepreneur David Bale and columnist Gloria Steinem, is a 47 year old Welsh actor. He is popular for his roles in 2000 comedy-horror film ""American Psycho"" as Patrick Bateman, and more significantly the Batman series playing Billionaire Bruce Wayne and his crime fighting hero alias The Batman. Bale has also been featured in the Terminator series as John Connor, however it was his role in the Batman movie series that has resulted in the majority of his 2021 net worth of $120 million.
