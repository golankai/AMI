Elton John is an English singer-songwriter and pianist. In collaboration with song-writer Bernie Taupin, John has made more than 30 albums and is one of the best-selling and most well-known music artists of all time. Some of John's most popular songs include: I'm Still Standing, Your Song, and Rocket Man. Rocket Man is the title of the biographical film of John's life, which explores his music career and personal life -- particularly with regards to his sexuality, relationship, and substance abuse.
