Judi Dench is a renowned English actress. Born and raised in North Yorkshire, she started acting from a very young age. She is now 86 years old. She has received Oscar, BAFTA, Golden Globe, and many other awards. Her most known roles include Skyfall and Pride and Prejudice. She has also received the 'dame' title from the royal family for her efforts of supporting the protection of the environment and other causes. She has one daughter, Finty, who is also an actress, and one grandchild. She has released an autobiography titled And Furthermore.
