JK Rowling, first name Joanne, is a British author most commonly known for her book series Harry Potter. The book series follows the story of a boy, aged 11 who is orphaned to live with his aunt and uncle and discovers he comes from a line of wizardry. JK was born on the 31st July 1965 in Yate Bristol. JK married Jorge in 1992 and then later married current husband Neil in 2001. JK has 3 children. JK went from living on benefits as a struggling parent to more recently being named first Billionaire by Forbes
