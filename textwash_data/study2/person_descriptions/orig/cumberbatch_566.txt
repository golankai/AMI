Benedict Cumberbatch is a famous British actor. He is well known all over the world for his portrayal of modern version of Sherlock Holmes. For Marvel fans he is well known for his portrayal of Dr. Steven Strange. He is tall, handsome and have trouble pronouncing the word penguin. He won multiple awards for his acting skills, including Emmy award and Golden globe. He studied at universities in London and Manchester. He was chosen by the Time magazine as one of the 100 most influential people in the world.
