Dame Judi Dench is a national treasure. She is an English actor who started her career in theatre but branched out to films to a great success. Her career spans over 60 years and includes many notable roles both on stage and in films. She has consistently been voted as one of Britain's best actors. She has been nominated for the Academy Awards seven times and won an Oscar for the Best Supporting Actress in 1998. She has also been awarded multiple other awards and honours. She was made a dame in 1988. Her career has gone from strength to strength, and she continues to act despite starting to lose sight in her eyes. She has been awarded 11 honorary degrees!
