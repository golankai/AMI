Judi Dench is a phenomenal English actress who has starred in many James Bond films and Shakespearean dramas. She was born in 1934 in York and married Micheal Williams in 1971 who sadly died in 2001 from lung cancer. She has had regular contact with theatre through her parents which led her to an interest in acting as well as many opportunities. She joined the Royal Shakespeare company in 1961 and played Anya in The Cherry Orchard. She also played Queen Victoria in 1997 in the film Mrs Brown. During her career she has starred in so many films and thatre productions and is seen as one of the best english actors.
