Judi Dench is regarded as one of the greatest actors in the UK. Naturally she has a title. Moreover she has won many awards over the years including an academy award. Her back catalogue of films are extensive. She has appeared in range of genres from historical to action. Including James Bond Movies and Historical drams such as Shakespeare in love. More recently she has appeared in the rather embarrassing ""Cats"". She has dabbled in charity work but this should be expected considering that she is a very wealthy high profile celebrity.
