Ricky Gervais is an English stand up comedian. Born in Reading on the 25th June 1961. He is best known for co creating, co writing and acting in the British sitcom The Office. This aired from 2001-2003.  He has won numerous awards which include seven BAFTAS, five British comedy awards, two Emmy awards and three golden globe awards. In 2010 he was named in the ‘time 100’ list of the worlds most influential people. Ricky Gervais has appeared in a number of Hollywood films such as ‘Ghost town’, ‘the night in the museum’, ‘for your consideration’ and ‘muppets most wanted’. He also wrote, directed and starred in ‘The invention of lying’. He is also the creator, executive producer, director and the writer of ‘After life’. He plays the lead role of Tony Johnson.
