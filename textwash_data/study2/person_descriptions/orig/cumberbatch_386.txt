Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor who has starred in many hit dramas and films including playing Sherlock Holmes in Sherlock, also starring in The Imitation Game, Dr Strange and The Courier, 12 Years a Slave and the Fifth Estate. he also does a lot of theatre work. His father was an actor. Benedict trained at the University of Manchester and the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.

He has three children and was married in 2015. 

Benedict is an ambassador for The Prince's Trust and aims to help support disadvantaged young people. He is also a founding member of the ""Save Soho"" group which aims to protect theatres and venues in London's Soho area.
