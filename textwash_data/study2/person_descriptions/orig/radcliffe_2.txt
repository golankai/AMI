Daniel Radcliffe is a 32 year old British actor who currently lives in America. He is most famous for his role as the main character in the 'Harry Potter' film franchise and has acted in multiple other films and featured in Broadway plays. His role as Harry Potter earned him the most fame: he started acting in the films when he was 11 and finished the series at the age of 22. His other films include Escape from Pretoria, The Woman in Black, Kill Your Darlings and Guns Akimbo. His first acting role was as David Copperfield in a film. Radcliffe was 10 years old at the time.
