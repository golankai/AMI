Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor. He is best known for his childhood performance as the eponymous hero in the Harry Potter franchise, over the decade 2001-2011, though he has since worked in more serious roles on stage in plays such as Equus. He has also undertaken charitable work and has expressed some political and social views. He endorsed Jeremy Corbyn's candidature for the leadership of the Labour party, which he supports. He had admitted to problems with alcohol and dyspraxia in his teenage years.
