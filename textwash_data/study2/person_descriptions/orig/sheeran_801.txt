Ed Sheeran was born on the 17th of February 1991 in Halifax, West Yorkshire in the United Kingdom. His full name is Edward Christopher Sheeran. Ed is a musician, singer and song writer and started writing at playing the guitar at a very young age. Ed has has many incredible hit songs such as castle on the hill, shape of you, galway girl, perfect and many more. Ed has also collaborations with other artists such as Justin Bieber, Camila Cabello, Eminem, Lewis Capaldi plus more. Ed Sheeran was awarded a MBE by prince Phillip on the 7th of December 2017 for his musical career and his charity work
