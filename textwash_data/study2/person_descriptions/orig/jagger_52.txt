Sir Michael Phillip Jagger, also known as Mick Jagger was born on 26 July of 1943 in Dartford, Kent. He is a British rock singer, songwriter, actor, movie producer and businessman. Mick is most commonly known as the lead singer and one of the founders of the British rock band called The Rolling Stones. He studied at the London School of Economics, but he did not get a degree from it because he dropped out and started The Rolling Stones. He got married to Nicaraguan actress and human rights advocate Bianca in 1971 but the couple split in 1978.
