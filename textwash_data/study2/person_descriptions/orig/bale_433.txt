Christian Charles Philip Bale was in Wales on 30th January 1974 and is an English actor. He began his acting career at the young age of 13 in Steven Spielberg's film Empire of the Sun. He is the recipient of various awards including an Academy Award and two Golden Globe Awards with time magazine including him on its list of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2011. Bale currently resides in LA and has US citizenship. He married Sandra  Blažić (former model) in Las Vegas on 29 January 2000 and they have a daughter and a son.
