Emma Watson is an English actress best known for her portrayal of Hermione Grainger in the Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. Since finding fame and acclaim as Hermione, Watson has gone on to lead in a number of blockbusters and more independent films. Her role in The Perks of being a Wallflower drew particular praise. She has taken time out of her career to finish her education, studying at Browne with time spent at Oxford. She has since cemented her place as a dedicated and powerful activist focused on gender equality and women's' issues.
