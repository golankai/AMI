Judi Dench is a dame. Judi is one of the best British actresses who has been in hundreds of productions. Judi is now eighty six years old! Judi had been married since 1971 to her husband Michael Williams, but sadly divorced in 2001. Judi worked for the royal Shakespeare company. Judi has been nominated for the academy award seven times! Judi did win an academy award for the best supporting actress, she won this award for her performance in Shakespeare in love, where she played queen Elizabeth 1.
