Elton John is one the greatest British pop stars of all time. He has numerous hit records which include Rocket Man, I'm Still Standing and Tiny Dancer.
Elton John is also well known for his flamboyant and colorful appearance. Throughout his career he has dressed up in various out of this world costumes. Out of everything that Elton John is known for in terms of his appearance, his iconic glasses would be the most recognizable thing. 
Elton John has managed to stay relevant across many decades. In recent years the film ""Rocket man"" starring Taron Egerton put Elton John back into the spotlight. Following the release of ""Rocket Man"", many of Elton John's big hits made it back into the UK top 40.
