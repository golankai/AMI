The queen in waiting. Kate Middleton met Prince William, our future king, at university and their up and down romance eventually ended in a marriage. Whilst she keeps a clean image in the press she is not a “queen of our hearts” as her mother in law was (and still is today). Rumours of her uninviting nature toward her new sister in law Meghan Markle and her seemingly calculated pr moves have left a bitter taste in the mouths of some of the British public. Despite the firms hard work, there are also many whispers about her husbands alleged affairs. It feels as though the curtain may be slipping in the “perfect” marriage that they show in the press
