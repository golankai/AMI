Daniel Jacob Radcliffe born 23 July 1989 is an English actor, best known for playing Harry Potter. He was very young when he first started in Harry Potter and throughout the film series we saw him grow up. In his GCSE exams he got seven B's, a couple of A's and an A*and in his A levels he got A grades but decided to take a break from education and did not go to college or university. 
In 2008, he revealed that he has a mild form of the neurological disorder developmental coordination disorder (dyspraxia). The motor skill disorder sometimes prevents him from doing simple activities. He has been in a long term relationship since 2012, with Erin drake.
