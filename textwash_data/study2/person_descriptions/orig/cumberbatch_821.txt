Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor, born in London. He was born on 19th July 1976, making him 45 years of age.
His full  name is Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch.
Benedict was awarded a CBE in 2015 for services to the performing arts. The Queen presented him with his CBE on the  10th November 2015, at Buckingham Palace.
Benedict is married to Sophie Hunter. They married in 2015 on Valentine's Day.
One of his most famous acting roles was as Sherlock Holmes in the TV series, Sherlock. This ran from 2010 ubtil 2017.
He plays Doctor Strange in the Marvel movies franchise, also starring in his own Doctor Strange film in 2016.
