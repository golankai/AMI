Daniel Radcliffe is a 32 year old English actor who is best known for playing Harry Potter in all of the Harry Potter films over a ten year period.  He has gone on to start in a number of other film including the 2012 horror the Woman in Black,  2013 Kill your Darlings where he starred as poet Alan Ginsburg.  His varied roles has led him to receive thirty nominations and sixteen award wins including a 2001 MTV movie award for best breakthrough male performance for his role as Harry Potter in Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone.  The followed in 2005 with the best young actor award Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.
