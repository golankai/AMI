Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula One driver who has won seven world titles since he started competing in 2007. He currently drives in Formula One for Mercedes, previously with Maclaren.

He is the first black person to drive in Formula One. He is also the youngest person to have won a championship. He is currently tied with Michael Schumacher for number of titles won in the sport.

Hamilton has a number of rivalries in the sport, mainly with Fernando Alonso and Sebastian Vettel. he is considered a very competitive driver with the desire to win all the time.

Hamilton previously dated former Pussycat Doll Nicole Scherzinger.
