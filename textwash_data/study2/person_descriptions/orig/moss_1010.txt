Kate moss, full name Katherine Ann Moss was born on January 16 1974, she currently age 47. She is a supermodel and also a business woman.  Kate Moss was in the 'supermodel era' and become famous in the mid 1990s. She had collaborations with Calvin Klein that  got her fashion icon status. She is known for her size zero fasion. She won an award in 2013 that acknowledged her contribution to fashion over the past 25 years!! 

She was also named one of the worlds 100 most influential people. She was accused of taking drugs in the early 2000s and this caused fashions brands to drop  her from campaigns.
