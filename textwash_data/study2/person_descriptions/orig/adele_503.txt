Adele is a singer, who is 33 years-old and has been preforming for the last decade. She naturally has ginger hair, however, over her carer, she has adapted a brownie-blond hair style. She has been married to Simon Konecki since 2016 and they have a son together, who is one and half years old.

Her songs consist of high pitched volumes with her using her highest vocals. She has recently gained publicity by her losing a large amount of weight after her breakup with her partner and has made a big comeback after years of silence by going on SNL and announcing her return.
