Ricky Gervais is a British actor, comedian and writer, known for The Office (UK), Extras and Afterlife. He also hosted 'An Idiot Abroad. His first appearance on TV was in 1998. Before comedy and acting, Ricky Gervais worked on radio and podcasts. Ricky Gervais has also done stand up comedy since the 1990's, with his first appearance at the Edinburgh Fringe in 2001. His sense of humour can be quite controversial or dark. Gervais has a degree in Philosophy from UCL, and is an atheist, humanist and vegetarian. He is in a long term relationship but does not wish to marry and is against the idea of having children.
