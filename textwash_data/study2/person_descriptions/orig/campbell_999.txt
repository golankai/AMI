Naomi Campbell is a British model born in 1970. She was one of the most in-demand models of the 1980s and 1990s. In August 1988, she made history and become the first black model to appear on the cover of French Vogue. A year later she graced the cover of American Vogue. Campbell was considered one of the first supermodels, along with Christy Turlington, Linda Evangelista, Claudia Schiffer, Kate Moss and Cindy Crawford. They were known together as the Big Six of the fashion industry. 
In May 2021, aged 51, Naomi Campbell announced the birth of her first child.
