David Robert Joseph Beckham was born on the 2nd May 1975.  The son of Sandra (a hairdresser) and David (a kitchen fitter).  Siblings include an older sister Lynne and a younger sister Joanne. He went to Chingford County High School.  A keen footballer her played for a local team then went on to have trials at various clubs.  Before signing for Manchester United at age 14 becoming one of the clubs most successful signings, finishing his career at LA Galaxy in America. He went on to marry Victoria from the Spice Girls and have 4 children. Nicknamed Golden Balls. He has more than 65 tattoos.  Has been a fashion icon
