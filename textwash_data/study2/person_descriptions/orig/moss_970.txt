Kate Moss is a 47 year-old British supermodel, fashion designer, and business-woman. A household name, Moss' work on ad campaigns with fashion brands such as Calvin Klein in the 1990's brought her to top-earning status. Moss is known for her thin figure, blonde hair, and adherence to the 90's fashion trend of 'heroin chic'. Beyond her modelling career, Moss found prominence in the media during the 1990's and 2000's due to drug use and other personal life scandals. Moss has participated in philanthropic work with charities such as War Child, Cancer Research UK, Comic Relief, and the Elton John AIDS Foundation.
