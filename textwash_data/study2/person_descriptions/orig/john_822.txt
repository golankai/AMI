Elton John was born with the original name of Reginald Kenneth Dwight. He was born in Middlesex on the 25th March 1947. His parent were Sheila and Stanley.
He is a singer, songwriter, pianist and composer. His first hit single was Your Song, in 1970.
Elton is married to David Furnish and they have two children together.
A film of Elton's life was released in 2019, called Rocketman. It told the story of his early life and career and also his battle with alcohol and drugs. He was played by Taron Egerton and he and Elton became good friends during the making of the film.
