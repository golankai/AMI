Emma Watson is an actress, born in Paris, but better known as being from England. She is 31 years old. She became famous as a result of playing Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter films. Since then she has had a very successful acting career and is also known for her activism and modelling. She has appeared in lots of films since Harry Potter, including Little Women, Beauty and the Beast, and The Bling Ring. In terms of activism, she was appointed to a G7 advisory board for women's rights and also cares a lot about ethical consumption. She was named in Time's 100 most influential people in 2015.
