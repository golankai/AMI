Emma Watson owns a florist shop with her husband in a small village. She has regular customers every week, and she loves doing new flower arrangements for them. Her and her husband have plans for children, but not for a few years. There main plan is to set up their florist in Paris, where they will get the shop established and hire staff to look after it, while they come back and run this one and settle down and have children. They visit their in laws every week, and all get together for a family meal. She always makes sure she takes a fresh bouquet of flowers.
