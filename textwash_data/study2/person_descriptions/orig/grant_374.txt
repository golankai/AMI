Another white older male. Hugh John Mungo Grant was born in 1960 and is an English actor who has been highly successful.  

He has been in films such as Four weddings and a funeral, Notting Hill to Bridget Jones diary. All being very similar roles as a vulnerable and witty romantic lead.  

He is a patron of a charity called DIPEx, which operates Healthtalkonline and the Fynvola foundation, which is named after his late mother. The foundation is a home for adults with learning disabilities. He has also worked as a fundraiser and ambassador for Marie Curie Cancer Care.
