English actor, extremely well known for playing charming, witty, posh romantic lead. Well known for playing opposite Andie McDowell in ‘Four Weddings and a Funeral’ and Julia Roberts in ‘Notting Hill’. Other notable films include:- Love Actually, Bridget Jones Diary; Sense and Sensibility; about a boy. 
Good looking and funny made him a popular British actor, he has managed to get through scandal and still keep his career endearing him to the public even more. He was in a relationship with Elisabeth Hurley for many years.
