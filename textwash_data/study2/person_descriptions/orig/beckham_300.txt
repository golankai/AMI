David Beckham is an English former professional soccer player. In his career, he won major titles in England (with Manchester United), Spain (with Real Madrid), Italy (with AC Milan), the USA (with LA Galaxy), and France (with PSG). He was also part of the Golden Generation of the England national team. 
He has been married to Victoria Beckham (Adams), a member of the pop group the Spice Girls, since 1999. He has four children, three boys (Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz) and one girl (Harper). Harper’s middle name, Seven, is in reference to Beckham’s shirt number throughout his career. 
He currently owns the MLS team Inter Miami, and has ownership stakes in the English club Salford City.
