Samuel Frederick Smith is an English singer and songwriter. Sam rose to fame in October 2012 after being featured on Disclosure's breakthrough single ""Latch"", going to number eleven in the UK Singles Chart. 
Famous for his looks and charisma Sam (Samuel) 
Sams third single, ""Stay with Me"", was an international success and reached number one in the UK and number two in the US. Sams singles ""I'm Not the Only One"" and ""Like I Can"" reached the top ten in the UK and won  four awards, at the 57th Annual Grammy Awards. This included Best Pop Vocal Album, Best New Artist, Record of the Year, Song of the Year, and nominations for Album of the Year.
