Christian Bale is a handsome British actor most well known for his roles in the Batman films. He won an Oscar in 2011 for best performance for an actor in a supporting role in the film The Fighter. He was born in Haverfordwest in Wales, UK and his birth sign is Aquarius. Christian is one point eight three metres in height. He apparently was compared to a young Steve McQueen by Steven Spielberg while filming Empire of the Sun back in 1987. Christian has also won two Golden Globe awards. He has been married since 2000 and has a son and a daughter.
