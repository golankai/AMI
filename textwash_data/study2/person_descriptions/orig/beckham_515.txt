David Beckham is a previous English football player who now owns Inter Miami CF and is co founder of Salford FC. David Beckham is married to precious Spice Girl Victoria Beckham and they have 4 children together Romeo, Brooklyn, Cruise and Harper. The 46 year old ex English player spent most of his professional career playing for Manchester United FC. David is the first English player to win league cups whilst playing for four different nations. He has become one of the most famous midfielders from his generation of football. David Beckham has been famously known for being one of the highest paid premier league footballers.
