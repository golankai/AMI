Naomi Elaine Campbell is an english model, actress, singer and businesswoman born on 22nd May 1970. Naomi was born in South London to Valerie Morris. Valarie states Naomi has never met her father who abandoned her when she was just four months pregnant. She was very young when she was declared as a supermodel. Naomi’s last name comes from her mother’s second marriage. She is of Afro-jamaican  descent and chinese-jamaican from her paternal grandmother. She grew up in Rome as this is where her mother was a dancer. Her mother travelled a lot so Naomi went to London to stay with relatives.
