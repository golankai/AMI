Award winning hugh grant was born in 1960 and has starred in close to 30 films released globally Including such hits as notting Hill,  Bridget Jones and four weddings are funeral. 

Hugh had a high profile relationship with Liz Hurley and was photographed with her in the famous safety pin dress. 

Hugh has 5 children, his youngest born in 2018 with his now wife Anna Eberstein. 

Hugh has won a BAFTA and a golden globe but so far the Oscar has eluded him. He has achieved 15 nominations for the work in his career.
