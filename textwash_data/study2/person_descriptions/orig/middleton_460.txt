Kate Middleton is the Duchess of Cambridge and is married to HRH Prince William the Duke of Cambridge. Her husband is second in line to the throne and she is therefore likely to be queen consort in the future. Kate grew up in Berkshire and attended St Andrews university in Edinburgh where she met William. They dated on and off over the course of almost 10 years before getting engaged in 2010. They married in 2011 at Westminster Abbey, watched by millions of people all over the globe. Kate's lace Alexander McQueen wedding dress inspired the fashion for wedding dresses for years to come, and she is also well known for her classy and fashionable sense of style. Kate has 3 children with William called George, Charlotte and Louis and they live on the Sandringham estate. Kate, along with her husband William does lots of work with charities and is patron to a number of them.
