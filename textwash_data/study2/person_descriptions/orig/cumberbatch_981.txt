Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor who has distinct features and look, as a tall man with dark curly hair. Due to his name, in recent years he has been subject to memes and jokes. He is arguably most known for his acting roles within the marvel universe as of recent, as a character called Dr Strange who came to help during the Avengers: End Game. He was appointed as a CBE in 2015 by the Queen of England for his contributions to art and charity. He was born in 1976 in London, and studied at the University of Manchester.
