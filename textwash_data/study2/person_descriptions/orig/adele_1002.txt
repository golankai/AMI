Londoner Adele Adkins is a 33 year old pop-soul artist from London, England. Adele began her love of voices and singing at just 4 years old, while being raised by her single mother. She first topped the charts in 2011 with her song, ""Someone Like You"". Since then she has become the UK's best-selling female album artist of the 21st century, even creating the theme song ""Skyfall"", featured on the 23rd film in the popular spy movie franchise ""James Bond"". In 2019, her net worth was valued at $150 million, making her the 22nd richest musician in her home country.
