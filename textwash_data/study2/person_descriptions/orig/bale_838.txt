He is British, born in 1974 in Wales to English parents. His mother was a circus performer and his father an activist. His grandfather was a stand in actor for John Wayne. Bale’s parents divorced in the 90’s, and he moved to the US with his father and sister. He actually has 2 older sisters and a half sister. He appeared in his first film in 1987 but didn’t get wider recognition until 2000. Bale holds US citizenship and has 2 children with his wife. He first played Batman in 2005 and went on to star in 2 further Batman films. This trilogy was the largest grossing of its kind.
