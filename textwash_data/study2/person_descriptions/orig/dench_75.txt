Dame Judi Dench CH, DBE, FRSA (Birthname Judith Olivia Dench) is a legendary English actress from York. She is currently 86 years old. She is regarded as one of the best British actresses of all time.

Dench has had a long career spanning over 60 years (64 years as of 2021), and has established herself as one of Britain's most prominent performers. This can be seen by her 7 Academy Award nominations. She may be known to many by her numerous appearances within the ""James Bond"" franchise as ""M"", a character she played for 20 years.
