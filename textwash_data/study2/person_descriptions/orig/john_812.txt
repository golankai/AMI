Elton John is a English singer songwriter pianist and composer. He was born on the 25th March 1947. Elton John was born Reginald Kenneth Dwight and was born in Pinner ,Middlesex. Elton John was raised in a council house in Pinner by his maternal grandparents. When Elton John decided he wanted a career in music his father tried to persuade to do something more conventional like banking. John used his wild stage costumes and performances as a way of letting go of his restrictive childhood. Both johns parents were musically talented his father was a trumpet player with the Bob millar band.
