Christian Bale is a British actor born in 1974 in Pembrokeshire, Wales to English parents Jenny and David. He also holds US citizenship and has lived in Los Angeles since the 1990s. He has starred in films such as American Hustle, American Psycho and The Big Short. Bale was cast as Bruce Wayne/Batman and starred in 3 Christopher Nolan Batman films. 
He married Sandra Blazic in 2000. The couple have two children, a son and a daughter. In 2008 he was arrested in London for an alleged assault and released on bail. Charges against him were later dropped due to insufficient evidence.
