Famously known for his role of Harry Potter, Daniel Radcliffe is an English actor born on the 23rd of July 1989, making him 32 years old. He become one of the highest paid actors in the world due to the success of the Harry Potter franchise. After this success, Daniel branched out into other movies and even as far as stage acting. He is also known for singing 'The Elements' song on the Graham Norton show and rapping Blackalicious 'Alphabet Aerobics' on the Jimmy Fallon show. His go-to karaoke song is 'The Real Slim Shady' by Eminem. He met his girlfriend, Erin Darke, on the set of 'Kill Your Darlings' in 2013.
