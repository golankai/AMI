Daniel Craig is an English actor.  He is best known for playing James Bond. Born on the 2nd March 1968 the son of Carol Olivia an art teacher and Timothy a member of the merchant Navy. He has an older sister.  He began acting at the age of 6 in his schools production of Oliver before taking roles at High School. He was accepted into drama school - Guidhall School of Music and Drama where he  Graduated from the in 1991.  He started his career on the stage going on to appear on TV then on to movies.  He has appeared in many movies which include Elizabeth, Lara Croft and Layer Cake.  

  Married to Rachel Weisz and he has one daughter named Ella.
