Former pro-footballer, metrosexual fashion icon, and wife of the ever-glamorous Posh Spice, David Beckham has it all. As a family man with four children, three sons and one daughter, Beckham lives a happy retirement in his countryside dwelling, ""Beckingham Palace"". He is a big supporter of the LGBTQ+ community, actively partaking in advocacy and acceptance and has a track record of philanthropy. He boasts many impressive friends and has been invited to several royal weddings. He began his famed football career in the early 90s and quickly became the man to watch playing for Manchester, England, Madrid, and LA Galaxy to name a few. He retired in 2013 but remains a constant in UK tabloids and Magazines.
