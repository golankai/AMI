Sam Smith born Samuel Frederick Smith is an English singer and songwriter who rose to prominence in October 2012. Sam's Debut studio album 'In the Lonely Hour' was released in May 2014 on Capitol Records UK. He was born on the 19th May 1992 in London to Frederick Smith and Broker Kate Cassidy. They grew up in Great Chishill, Cambridgeshire, Smith was bullied for having breasts as a child and had liposuction aged 12. In May 2014 Sam came out to the public as Gay and acknowledged a relationship since ended with actor and model Jonathon Zeizel.
