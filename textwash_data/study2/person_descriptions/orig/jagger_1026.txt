Mick Jagger’s real name is Sir Michael Philip Jagger. He was born on 26th July, 1943 in Dartford Kent. Mick is the lead vocalist for the Rolling Stones. He is an english songwriter, singer, actor and film producer. Mick’s networth is estimated at $360 million. Keith Richards, is his songwriter partner and they are the most successful in history. Mick went to London school of Economics however he left these to join the Rolling Stones. In 1985 he went solo and released She’s the Boss album. Mick has 8 children with five different women. As a solo artist, Mick was in the top 40 charts for US and UK with 70 singles. Finally, in 2003 he was knighted for his services to popular music.
