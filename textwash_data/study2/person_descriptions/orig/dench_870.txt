Dame Judi Dench is a legend of an actress who made her acting debut way back in 1957. She was born in York in 1934 and has had a wide and varied acting career ranging from classic Shakespearean plays, tv series and films to more recently being well known as playing tne character ""M"" in seven of the James Bond/007 movies, the last one being Skyfall where her character was killed of. She is a very classy lady with a fabulous sense of style. Despite suffering with macular degeneration, she continues to work relying on friends to help her learn her lines.
