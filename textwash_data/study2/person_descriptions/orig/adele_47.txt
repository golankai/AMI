Adele Atkins, known as Adele, is an English singer songwriter from London, England. Born 5 May 1988, she has been active in the music industry since 2006 when she released her debut album, 19. Her second album, 21, built on the success of 19 and surpassed it in sales and popularity. Her third studio album, 25, was also a best selling album and continued on the success of her previous offerings. She is a self confessed Labour Party supporter and is known for her philanthropy.
