Lewis Hamilton (born in 1085) is an English formula 1 driver from Stevenage. Hamilton showed his passion for driving when he was only 5, and by the age of 6 he was already competing on go-karts. He won several junior tournaments and was so talented that Ladbroke took a bet on him in 1998 that he would have won his first championship by the age of 23. Hamilton raced for Formula A for a few years and then joined Formula 3 with Renault in 2003. He debuted in formula 1 in 2007 finishing on the podium on his debut, giving hard time to the back then champion Fernando Alonso. From there, his career has been more and more remarkable and to this days he holds the records of victories in formula 1 (over 100)
