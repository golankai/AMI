Christian Bale is an English actor well known for his method acting. He is well known for his role as Batman, his depiction of the violent killer in American psycho as well as starring in The Machinist in which he lost a huge amount of weight 
Footage was leaked online on line of him losing his temper on set. He was born in 1974 and his first film saw him taking a lead role in Empire of the Sun at just thirteen years old.  
He has several awards including an Oscar for best supporting actor, a golden globe and screen actors guild.
