Christian bale is an English actor age 47 years,  born on the 30th January 1974, well known for his versatility. Christians breakthrough role was at age 13 in the war film empire of the sun.  He trained in ballet as child. His first acting role was at 8 years old for an advert for fabric conditioner lemon. Even though he was born in Wales he has lived in Los Angeles since 1990s and hold a US citizenship. He married Sandra ""Sibi"" Blažić, on the 29th January 2000, they now have a daughter and a son.
