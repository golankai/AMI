Daniel Craig is a British actor best known for his role as James Bond from 2006 in Casino Royale to his most recent role in No Time To Die which has not yet been released. Daniel Craig is also known for several other roles on television and movies including Munich and The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, having made his first appearance in 1992. Daniel Craig does not like politics, and is an advocate for a programme with the UN to eliminate mines. 
Daniel Craig is married and divorced with actress Fiona Loudon with whom he has one daughter called Ella, and has since remarried to another actress, Rachel Weisz.
