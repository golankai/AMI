Jagger is an English singer, songwriter, actor, and film producer who has achieved international fame as the lead vocalist and one of the founder members of the Rolling Stones. His songwriting partnership with Keith Richards remains one of the most successful in history. He was an absolute icon in the 70’s and was looked up to by many people. Everyone enjoyed his music back in the day and still do now. He is an icon looked upto by the older generation. He is now 78 years old and even today’s youth make songs about him- that’s how popular he is.
