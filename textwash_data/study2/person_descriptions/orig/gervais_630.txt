Ricky Gervais is a very funny comedian from England.
He shot to fame in 2001 when he released the television series 'the office' which he also co-wrote and starred in. The office was a 'mockumentary' type program based around a paper supply office in Slough.
He also released another television series called 'an idiot abroad' in which he sets his friend (carl pilkington)up travelling around the world into some circumstances in which is is completely uncomfortable and Gervais gets lots of laughs by setting him tasks to do.
Ricky Gervais comes from Reading , in Berkshire which is a county in England in the UK
