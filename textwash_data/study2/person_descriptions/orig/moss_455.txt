Kate Moss is from Croydon and was born in 1974. She rose to fame in the 1990s as one of the Supermodels.  Her look was described as 'heroin chic'.  She has a daughter named Lila Rose, who's father is the publisher Jefferson Hack.  Other famous relationships that Moss has had include Babyshambles singer Pete Docherty, and Johnny Depp from A Nightmare on Elm Street. She has been featured on the cover of Vogue magazine over 65 times.  She has sung onstage with the Kills whilst dating Jamie Hince, whom she later married - she wore a bespoke Galliano dress for the occasion.  She was filmed snorting cocaine with Pete Doherty, and lost several of her modelling contracts due to the controversy generated by these actions.
