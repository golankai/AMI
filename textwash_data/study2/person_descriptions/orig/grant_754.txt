Hugh John Mungo Grant was born in 1960 in White city, London.  
He is a much loved English actor and has starred in some of the biggest British films of all time.  Such films as Four Weddings and a Funeral, Notting Hill, Love Actually and Bridget Jones Diary have made him one of our biggest stars.
In 2019 Grant took on the role of Fletcher in Guy Ritchies The Gentleman in which he played a very different role from previous charactors we have seen him as.
He has been romantically linked with several high profile women most notable Elizabeth Hurley.  It was whilst in a relationship with Hurley that Hugh Grant was arrested on Sunset Boulevard for seeking the services of sex worker Divine Brown.
