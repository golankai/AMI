Ed sheeran was born in 1991. He has had worldwide success as a singer/songwriter selling over 150 million records. He has collaborated with some of the best known artists in the world including Beyoncé and Taylor Swift.
Ed sheeran fan base and popularity grew through him releasing music on YouTube.  He was one of the first people to top the iTunes Charts without being signed to a record label. 
He has released 4 solo albums to date. 
His career hasn’t been without some controversy with several lawsuits being settled out of court for plagiarism claims.
