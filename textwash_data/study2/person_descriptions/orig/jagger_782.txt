An English singer who's one of the members of the Rolling Stones. He is 78 years old, born on the 26th July 1943. One of his biggest hits in the band was ""I can't get no satisfaction"". He has worked with many other famous individuals such as Keith Richards which still is one of the most successful partnerships in history. He has a total of eight children the youngest being just 4 years old. He has also been in multiple movies/films the most latest date being 2019. He is a very popular singer and very well known to so many people. He was most popular in the 70's and early 80's where him and the band created several hit albums.
