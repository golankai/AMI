David Beckham is a former footballer. He played for Manchester United, and famously wore the number 7 shirt. He spent 6 years as the England captain, earning 56 caps throughout this time. He currently co-owns Inter Miami CF and Salford City football clubs, and still takes an active interest in football. He has an OBE is married to Victoria Beckham. They have 3 children: two boys, Romeo and Brooklyn, and a girl Harper(whose middle name is 'Seven' and represents the number that Beckham wore at Manchester United). David Beckham had a footballing career that spanned 20 years and spent many of these years at American football club LA Galaxy, something other top retired players have done.
