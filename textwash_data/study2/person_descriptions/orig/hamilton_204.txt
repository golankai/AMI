Lewis Hamilton is an F1 racing car driver, he has received a Knighthood for his contributions to Sport. He has 7 world championship titles and holds records for the most pole positions, wins and podium finishes. He was born and raised in Hertfordshire. He started with a learner F1 driving program in 1998 at the age of 13, this eventually led to him being the first black driver to drive in the f1 and then him being the youngest person to ever become F1 world champion.
Hamilton currently drives with Mercedes and will continue his contract into 2023.
Hamilton is often cited as being the best F1 driver in history. 
Hamilton has also suffered a lot of racist abuse in his time in the F1 despite the fact that he does a lot of charity work with his free time and is a very charitable person.
