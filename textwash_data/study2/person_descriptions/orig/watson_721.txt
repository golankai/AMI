Emma Watson is a 31 year old, white, female actress most well known for her role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter films. The casting of the first film, Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone, she was only 9 years old and had previously only acted in school plays, but had attended drama clubs and schools in Oxfordshire. She has since starred in a number of other well known films including starring as Belle in the live action adaptation of Beauty and the Beast. Emma is notably recognised as an activist for womens rights, a UN spokesperson and a model for luxury and sustainable brands.
