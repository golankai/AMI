Adele Laurie Blue Adkins MBE is an English singer and songwriter. She is one of the worlds best selling music artists, with sales of over 120 million records. After graduating in arts from the BRIT School in 2006, Adele signed a record deal with XL recordings. She was 33 years old, born in May. She was divorced from Simon Konecki in twenty twenty one after five years married. She has one child Angelo Adkins whose name she kept a secret for many years. Adele suffered vocal chord damage that forced her to cancel two of her four Wembley Stadium shows in twenty seventeen.
