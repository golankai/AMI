Cara Delevigne is an English model and actress born on 12th August 1992. She has two sisters, Poppy and Chloe, and a half brother called Alex. Cara has worked for many fashion designers such as Marc Jacobs, Dolce & Gabbana and Louis Vuitton. In 2012 she walked in the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show. She has starred in films such as Paper Towns, Suicide Squad and Pan. 
Cara has revealed she is bisexual and identified as pansexual. Her previous relationships include St Vincent and Ashley Benson. In 2018 Delevigne revealed she is gender fluid.
