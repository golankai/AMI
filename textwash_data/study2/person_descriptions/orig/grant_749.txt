Hugh Grant is a British actor, born on the 9th September 1960.  He is best known for his roles in romantic comedies such as Love Actually, Bridget Jones's Diary and Four Weddings and a Funeral. 

Hugh Grant was born at Hammersmith Hospital in White City, London. He went to Hogarth Primary School in Chiswick, West London and then went to St Peter's Primary School in Hammersmith, also in West London. He then attended Wetherby School, an independent prepatory school in Notting Hill, before going to Latymer Upper School in the same area. 

He has received numerous awards during the course of his career. In 1994, he received a BAFTA for the Best Actor in a Leading Role in Four Weddings and a Funeral, and a Golden Globe for the same role in the same year.
