Hugh Grant is an English actor who came to light particularly for his role in 4 Weddings and a Funeral. He is now in his sixties, has made nearly 30 films, and has won  a Golden Globe award. 
He is one of the UK's most famous acting faces, despite often portraying the same, bumbling englishman as a character, His acting range seems limited. He has short hair, which is now grey and is clean-shaven. He dated Liz Hurley for a considerable period of time, which further thrust him into the media spotlight, particularly in the early stages of the relationship. Hugh has 3 children and is married to a Swede.
