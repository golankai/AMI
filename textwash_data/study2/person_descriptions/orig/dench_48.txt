Dame Judi Dench is a beloved English actress born on 9 December 1934 in York, England. She studied at the Central School of Speech and Drama and is a respected actress in theatre and in film. She had one child with former spouse Michael Williams, Flinty Williams, and also had a grandchild, Sam Williams, She had been active in film and stage since 1957. Her most notable roles have been playing 'M' in the James Bond films, Evelyn Greenslade in Best Exotic Marigold Hotel, and as Queen Victoria in Vicotria and Abdul. She currently resides in Outwood, Surrey.
