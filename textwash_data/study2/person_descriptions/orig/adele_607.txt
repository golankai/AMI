Adele, Adele, Adele. The living legend, a actual icon. Adele is a thirty-three year old record breaking British pop singer song writer known for her incredibly emotional and heart - felt music which is typically based on her heartbreak at the time. Adele is a beautiful, confident, loud and bubbly character well known in the industry with lots of A-list friends. She has made hundreds of TV appearances as she has a great sense of humour and even appeared on the infamous SNL! Over the last few years she has taken a step back from the lime light to focus on herself, her young son and her personal life.
