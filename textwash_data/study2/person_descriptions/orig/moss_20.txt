Kate Moss is a British supermodel with blonde hair. She wears high fashion and is one of the world's most famous supermodels and is also a lesser known businesswoman. She arrived at the end of the supermodel era and rose to fame in the mid 1990s. Her collaborations with Calvin Klein brought her to fashion icon status. She is in her late 40s born in Croydon in London. She also edits British Vogue magazine. She has her own clothing range and been involved in music projects also. She has won many awards for her modelling. In 2007 she was one of the world's 100 most influential people. She also received media scrutiny for her party lifestyle and drug use. Drug allegations in 2005 led her to be dropped from fashion campaigns.
