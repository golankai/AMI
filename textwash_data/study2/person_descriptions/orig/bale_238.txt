Christian Bale is an English actor. He is best known for his roles in several Batman films. He has won several awards for his work including an academy award and a golden globe. He has undergone several physically transformations for his work and is very dedicated to his profession. He is regarded as one of the most influential actors of our time. 
Aside from his professional life he is married with two children and lives in LA, where he has lived since the 1990s. He holds a full US citizenship.
