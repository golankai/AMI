Daniel Craig, born in 1968 in Cheshire, England, is best known for his role as James Bond from the year 2006. He has also starred in numerous other films including Knives Out, a murder mystery, The girl with the dragon tattoo, and Munich. He has earned a golden globe award nomination for best actor in Knives Out. He is heavily involved in charity and has worked with various charities including comic relief, aids charities, or is international. He was appointed the first global advocate for the elimination of mines and explosive hazards by the United Nations in April  2015.
