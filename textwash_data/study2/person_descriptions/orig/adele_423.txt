Adele is a well known British singer who has won multiple awards for her albums. Her usual genre is pop and power ballads. She went to the BRIT school. Adele is known for her bubbly and funny personality along with her London accent. She has children and took a career break during these times as she struggled with postpartum depression. She has become good friends with Alan carr over the years. 
Popular songs include someone like you and rolling in the deep. 
She is won of the richest females in the world due to her success. Particularly with her song, skyfall which featured in a James Bond movie.
