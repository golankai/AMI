Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch is a British actor. He is best known for starring in Sherlock, Which is a British Television series. He was born in London. He is currently 45 years of age. The Imitation game, in which he played the main role, earned him nominations for several awards, all for best actor in the leading role. In 2014, he was in the Times magazine, on the list of top 100 influential people in the world.  He was awarded a CBE by The Queen in 2015. 
He is a graduate of Victoria University of Manchester in which he obtained a masters of Art in classical Acting.
