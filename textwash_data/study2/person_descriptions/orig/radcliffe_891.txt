Daniel Radcliffe is a child actor who rose to fame in the lead role in Harry Potter film franchise. He quickly became one of the most recognisable faces in film. He has scruffy brown hair, is pale and isn't particularly tall. Since Harry Potter he has appeared on stage and in a number of comedy films. He has been known to openly make fun of himself and the fact he isn't the greatest actor of all time. Most recently he starred in an Amazon Prime film where he gets a gun attached to each of his hands.
