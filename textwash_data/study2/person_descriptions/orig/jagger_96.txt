Mick Jagger is the lead singer with world famous 1960s band, The Rolling Stones.  

He was born in England and is 78 years old.  Despite thinking retirement would be calling Mick is still currently touring with The Stones.  

Mick is one of the founding members of The Stones and is known for his unique voice and distinctive dance style.  Together with Keith Richards he has written most of The Stones' songs.  

Mick has been married once, to Bianca Jagger in 1971 but they divorced in 1978.  Since then Mick has had many relationships and has eight children with five women.
