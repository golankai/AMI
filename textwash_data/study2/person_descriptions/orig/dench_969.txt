Dame Judi Dench is a well-known English actress, whose work in theatre and film has received numerous accolades. Dench has received multiple Oscar nominations, including for her roles in the films Philomena, Chocolat, Mrs Brown, and Iris. Indeed, Dench has developed a reputation as one of the greatest actresses in Britain, and is a household name. Outside of her acting career, for decades Dench has been closely involved in charity and advocacy work. This work has largely focused on either theatre or medicine.
