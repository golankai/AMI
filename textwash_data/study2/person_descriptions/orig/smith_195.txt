Sam Smith is an English singer-songwriter. They are 29-years old and were born in London, England. They are one of many artists to have sung a 'James Bond' theme tune. They've been around for about ten years and won lots of awards, like Grammys, Brits and Golden Globes. They like stating which other musical stars have influenced them, including Adele, Amy Winehouse, Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey, Beyonce, Christina Aguilera, Robyn, and Ed Sheeran. Sam has produced three main albums - In the Lonely Hour, The Thrill of it All, and Love Goes. They're all pretty good!
