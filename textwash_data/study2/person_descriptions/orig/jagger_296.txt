Mick Jagger is an English musician, most famous for being the front man of the rock group Rolling Stones. After dropping out of the London School of Economics to pursue his career, the band became one of the most successful in history. Despite pursuing a solo career, his biggest musical successes remain as part of the Rolling Stones.
Jagger has been inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, and was knighted for his services to music in 2003. 
He has eight children, from various relationships. He has had several long-term partners, typically with women much younger than himself.
