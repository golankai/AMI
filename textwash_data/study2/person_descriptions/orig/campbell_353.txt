Naomi Campbell is a supermodel from the 1990s. She is 51 years old and has one child. She was born in England and raised in Italy. She initially was in music videos for Bob Marley and Culture Club before she was scouted by a modelling agency. Naomi did catwalk modelling for designer brands such as Versace and Docle & Gabanna. She has also tried her hand at being a singer and an author, launching a beauty cosmetics range and presenting on her own You Tube channel. Naomi has been convicted of four counts of assault over the years.
