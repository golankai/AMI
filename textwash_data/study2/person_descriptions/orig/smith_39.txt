Sam Smith is an English singer songwriter. He came to prominence by featuring on Disclosure's hit 'Latch'. He has gone onto independent success with multiple hit singles and albums and wrote the intro for the Bond film Spectre. He is multi Grammy award winner and has sold over 30 million records world wide. Smith cites Adele and Amy Winehouse as his greatest influences. Smith is non binary and identifies with the pronouns they/them and came out as gay in 2014. He is a leading figure in the queer community.
