Sam Smith is an English singer songwriter who is arguably most known for their single on the James Bond Soundtrack, they have a unique high voice for singing which is noted to be of ‘diva style’. Their main inspiration is said to have not come from any male artists. They first came to limelight after featuring on Disclosures album in a song called Latch and then NaughtyBoys LaLaLa, before releasing their own music. Smith is influenced by the likes of Adele and Amy Whinehouse in their music. They have been nominated and won numerous numerous awards and continue to top the charts in the UK and beyond.
