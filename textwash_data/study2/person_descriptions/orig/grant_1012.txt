Hugh Grant was born  Hugh John Mungo Grant 9th Soptember 1960 in Hamersmith, White City London. His father was an officer in the Gighlanders and also ran a carpet firm, his mother was a school teacher for over 30 yrs, teaching Latin and french. He spent a lot f time with his grandfather during the holidays in Scotland shooting and fishing.He joined the Oxford university drama society and started acting playing the part of Fabian in the production of Twelfth night. He done a variety of jobs including a groundsman job at a football club. He was soon acting with the likes if Anthony Hopkins and Mel Gibson. At the age of 32 he was about to give up acting when he landed the role in ""Four weddings and a funeral"" annd shot to stardom.
