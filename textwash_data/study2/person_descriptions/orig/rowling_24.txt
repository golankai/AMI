Sam Smith is an English singer and songwriter who was born in London, England. Smith rose to fame when he was featured on Disclosure's breakthrough single ""Latch"". Sam Smith has won four Grammy Awards, three Brit Awards, three Billboard Music Awards, an American Music Award as well as a Golden Globe and an Academy Award through his career. He's best known for his hit's 'Stay', 'Too Good At Goodbye's' and 'Lay Me Down' which got him to where he is today. Sam came out to the public as gay, and acknowledged a relationship with model Jonathan Zeizel.
