Kate Middleton is the wife of Prince William of England. She is also known as the Duchess of Cambridge. She has been in a relationship with William since they both attended university at St. Andrews in the early 2000s. Kate has always been adored by the public all over the world, and is seen as being incredibly down to Earth and very likeable. She and Prince William have three children together: Prince George, Princess Charlotte, and Prince Louis. She performs many public duties for the British Royal Family, including a variety of philanthropic work. In recent times, she has been pitted against Meghan Markle, the Duchess of Sussex due to the scandal surrounding Meghan and Prince Harry's departure from the Royal Family.
