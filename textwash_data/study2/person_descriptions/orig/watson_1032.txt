Emma Watson is a British actress who came to prominence for her role as Hermione Granger in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone. As one of the most widely recieved film franchises of the time, spanning 8 films across 10 years, and with Hermione as one of the three lead roles, Watson was able to put on full display her talents for the entertainment industry. She was noted for having many characteristics in common with her character, writing pages of notes for her audition. She has since gone on to continue her acting work in films, becoming one of the highest earning female actresses, as well as being applauded for her activism efforts for equal rights.
