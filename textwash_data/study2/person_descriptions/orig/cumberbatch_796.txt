Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor possibly best known for his portrayal of Sherlock Holmes as well as his role as Doctor Strange in the Marvel cinematic universe. He is married with three children. His acting career spans across films, theatre, radio, television, narration, and music. Cumberbatch has often featured in ‘Most Desirable Male’ lists, and his fans are sometimes dubbed as ‘Cumberbitches’. In 2005, he and two friends were briefly abducted in South Africa but were later released without explanation.
