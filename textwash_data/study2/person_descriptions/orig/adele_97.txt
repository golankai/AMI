Adele Adkins is a 33 year old extremely successful British singer/songwriter. 

She has an extremely powerful voice and has won numerous awards, including Grammys, a Golden Globe and Brit Awards.

Adele was married to Simon Konecki, a charity entreprenuer who is probably best know for Smart Water.  They have a son, Angelo James, together who is nearly 9 years old.  Unfortunately their marriage didn't last and their divorce was finalised this year.  

Adele has recently undergone an amazing transformation having lost a large amount of weight.  She achieved this by following the Sirtfood diet and also working out with a personal trainer.
