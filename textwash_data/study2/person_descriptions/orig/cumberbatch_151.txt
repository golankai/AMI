Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor, best known for his roles in many on screen and stage roles. His successful career has seen him win a Primetime Emmy award, a British Academy Television Award, and a Laurence Olivier Award. Some of his best known roles include Sherlock Holmes, and characters in Spiderman, the Hobbit, the Imitation Game and Doctor Strange. Of which the latter has a new film being released in 2022. 
Benedict Cumberbatch grew up in London to a family of actors and actresses, and was destined for the career path when he joined the prestigious arts school LAMDA
