Daniel Radcliffe is a 32 year old British actor. Radcliffe first appeared on the big screen at the young age of ten, debuting in a BBC One film called David Copperfield. He is best known for starring as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter franchise, consisting of eight movies, over a period of ten years. Radcliffe is also a very versatile actor with roles in genres including horror, thriller, fantasy and more. Radcliffe lives in both London and New York City, splitting his time between the two cities. He has also been in a steady relationship with Erin Darke, a fellow cast member of the movie Kill Your Darlings in 2012.
