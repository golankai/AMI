Emma Watson is an English actress, best known for her role as Hermione Granger in Harry Potter. Born in Paris to British parents in 1990, she then grew up in Oxfordshire. She went to a theatre art school, and landed the Harry Potter role as her very first professional role at only 9 years old. The Harry Potter movies brought her world fame and success. She then chose to go on to pursue a university degree at Brown and became an activist for women's rights. She was also appointed UN Women Goodwill Ambassador in 2014.
