Ricky Gervais is an English stand-up comedian and actor, gaining wide recognition for his work in the sit-com The Office. His career spans across multitude of medias, such as television, film, radio, music, podcasts, and books. He has won a wealth of awards and honours, such as seven BAFTAs, five British Comedy awards, and three Golden Globes. In 2010 he was included in the Time magazine's 100 most influential people. Before beginning his career in comedy, he attempted to become a pop star! His comedic genres include black comedy, observational comedy, satire, and cringe comedy. He has a reputation of being controversial and blunt.
