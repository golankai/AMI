Adele is a british singer/songwriter. Born on the 5th of May 1988 (aged 33), Adele is one of the best selling artists of the world. She is known for breaking records such as achieving 6 Grammy awards in one night. She is also known for co writing the theme song for James Bond's Skyfall. She went to college with Jessie J and Leona Lewis. Adele has broken 3 World Guinness Records. In 2020, she stunned her fans by sharing a photo on her 32nd birthday, having lost a huge amount of weight. In 2012, she was awarded an MBE by the British Empire.
