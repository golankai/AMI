Naomi Campbell is an international supermodel. She is British and is currently 51 years old. She is also known for acting and singing. Naomi was the first black woman to appear on the cover of french vogue. Naomi featured on modelling reality show ‘the face’ and also created an r&b music album. Naomi has one child and is not currently married. Naomi has never met her biological father. Campbell spent her early years in Rome, Italy, where her mother worked as a modern dancer. She took the surname ""Campbell"" from her mother's second marriage.
