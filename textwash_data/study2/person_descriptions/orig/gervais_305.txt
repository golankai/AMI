Ricky Gervais is a popular English comedian, actor, director, producer and writer, and is most well known for co-creating and starring in the hit British comedy series, The Office. He has won many awards for his many creative roles, and has international success with a lot of his work.
He has managed to successfully transition over stateside, and in Hollywood, featuring in movies and hosting numerous prestigious award shows.
He has had a successful working relationship with Stephen Merchant and Karl Pilkington, two other well known British comedians.
His birthday is on the 25th of June, 1961, and he is currently 60 years old.
