Mick Jagger is the lead singer for the famous British rock band The Rolling Stones, who are one of the most popular rock bands every. Mick Jagger is one of the most well-known lead singers in the entirety of rock history, and has won multiple awards as part of the Rollings Stones and also as a solo act. He famously had a love/hate relationship with Keith Richards, who was also in the Rolling Stones. He has 8 children with multiple different women and has been married once and in a long term relationship with other girlfriends.
