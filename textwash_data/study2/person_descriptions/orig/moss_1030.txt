Katherine Anne Moss was born 16th January 1974. She is an english supermodel and businesswoman. She grew up in Croydon. She had a younger brother called Nick, a half sister called Lottie. Her parent’s names are Peter and Linda however they split up when Kate was only 13. Kate went to Ridgeway Primary School and then Riddlesdown High School. She was scouted as a model at just aged 14. In 2005 she was accused of taking drugs and then in 2006 she then had to return to the UK from USA to answer some questions regarding the drug allegations. 8 months later the police dropped the charges against her as they didn’t have enough evidence to prove she was in the wrong.
