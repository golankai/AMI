Ed Sheeran MBE (Birthname Edward Christopher Sheeran) is an English musician from Halifax, West Yorkshire. He is one of the worlds best-selling artists at the current time.

Sheeran has been active within the music industry since 2004, even managing to earn 4 Grammy awards. Sheeran currently has the most streamed song (""Shape of you"") on Spotify (One of the biggest music streaming platforms) with over 2.9 billion streams worldwide. He was even named as the second most streamed artist of the decade by Spotify.
