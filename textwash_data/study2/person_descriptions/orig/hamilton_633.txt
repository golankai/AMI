Lewis Hamilton is a formula one British racing car driver. He currently competes for Mercedes-Benz, however has historically also driven for McLaren. He has won 100 races throughout his career and has been awarded as a Member of the British Empire. Despite being respected as one of the best,  Lewis Hamilton has been a victim of racial abuse over the years, even by the British media. He has since been determined to promote equality and anti racism within the sport of motor racing and supporting the black lives matter campaign.
