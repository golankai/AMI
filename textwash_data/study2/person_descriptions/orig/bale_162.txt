He has won a Oscar and two golden globe awards. His first role was in Stephen Spielberg's film Empire of the sun at aged 13. He plays Batman in the trilogy of films which is the highest grossing franchise. He is married to Sandra and has 2 children, 1 son and 1 daughter, they live together in Los Angeles.
