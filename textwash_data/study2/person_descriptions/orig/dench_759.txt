Judi Dench is an 86 year old woman. She went to private school, and has a university degree. She has three children, two boys and one girl. She is married to her childhood sweetheart, they got married when they were 16. She has five grandchildren who she adores, and see's every Sunday. She always gives them sweets even though their parents tell her not to. She goes to a book club every Tuesday with her neighbours, while the husbands meet to watch tv and have a beer. She has a trip to disneyland planned for her, her children and grandchilden, but they do not know about it yet.
