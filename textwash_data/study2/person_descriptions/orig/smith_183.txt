Sam Smith is a singer and songwriter from the UK and rose to fame in 2013. They were born in London but grew up in Cambridgeshire where they received an education in musical theatre, among other things. Smith's breakthrough song was Lay Me Down which allowed them to create a name for themselves as a constant on the UK charts. From then on, Smith released several award-winning albums such as The Thrill of It and Love Goes. 
Smith came out as gay in 2014, genderqueer in 2017, and non-binary in 2019, making the choice that they be known with they'them pronouns.
