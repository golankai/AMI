Hugh Grant is a 61 year-old English actor. He is best known for his portrayal of 'proper' upper-class English characters, such as his roles in Bridget Jones' Diary, Love Actually, Notting Hill, and Maurice. However, Grant has also explored roles outside of this typing, such as his character in Guy Ritchie's The Gentlemen. Grant has received critical acclaim for his work on stage and screen. Grant's personal life has landed him in the tabloid's, from which libel lawsuits and legal issues have spawned.
