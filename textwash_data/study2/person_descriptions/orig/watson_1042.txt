Emma Watson is perhaps most notable for her role as Hermione Granger in the 'Harry Potter' film series, in which has a strong and ever-growing fan base. She has diversified her repertoire with , including 'Bling Ring' and 'Perks of Being a Wallflower'. Watson, alongside film-making has also earned herself a Bachelors degree of English from Brown University. Of late however, she has flourished in the women's rights movement, facilitating this by acquiring the role of UN Women Goodwill ambassador.
