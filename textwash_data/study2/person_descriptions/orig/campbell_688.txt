Naomi Campbell is a well-known English supermodel. She was born in 1970 in London. She is known for being the first black woman to be featured on the cover of the French Vogue. Her career began at 15 years old. She has been featured in the campaigns of the biggest fashion houses. She also pursued acting and singing once her model career had set off, as well as developing her own model competition series. She was also involved in a lot of scandals, and even got charged with physical assault multiple times. Apart from that, she has been very active in helping African children and other disadvantaged groups and has raised millions of dollars through her own charity. She has welcomed her first daugther in 2021.
