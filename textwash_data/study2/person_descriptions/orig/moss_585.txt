Kate Moss is one of the most famous British supermodels. She is 47 years old and has one daughter. She was born in Croydon, London. Kate Moss was once accused of drug abuse in 2005 there was a big scandal. Due to this, she lost many campaigns such as with H&M, Chanel and Burberry. Moss did later apologise but never admitted using drugs. 
She has been an ambassador for Dior. Ultimately after a trial Kate Moss was cleared of all the accuses due to lack of evidence.
Kate Moss then resumed her modelling career and has worked with all the most known brands such as Versace, Bugatti, Balmais, Luis Vittion, YSL, Calvin Klein, Bulgari and Rimmel. She is definitely a living legend for the modelling industry. She has a daughter Lila
