Benedict Cumberbatch is a charismatic, talented and well-liked British actor, both in the UK and overseas.
He may be best known for his role as Sherlock Holmes in the series with the same name from the BBC. He has a classic drama upgringing and has been on stage with many theatre productions. He is also known for his roles in the Imitation Game and as part of the Marvel Universe as Doctor Strange.
He has a quirky yet respectable demeanor. He is known for having quite a legion of fans, which, when interviewed, he is thankful for but also causes shyness.
He is married, has three children and is 45 years old.
