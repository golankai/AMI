Daniel Radcliffe, most known for playing the  star character Harry Potter in the film series of Harry Potter, is an English actor born and raised in London. Currently aged thirty two.
He started acting at a young age and has continued this all the way into his adult career, starring in many other films such as The Woman In Black. 
He has also done some stage work on Broadway and The West End throughout his whole career.
He currently lives in New York, America with his long term partner Erin, splitting his time between America and England.
