Lewis Hamilton was born on the 7th January 1985 in Stevenage England. He is a highly talented Formula 1 racing driver. His parents as Carmen Larbalestier and Anthony Hamilton. Lewis began his career at the young age of eight years old. He won the british kart championship at the age of 10 years old. In 2008 Lewis became the first black driver to win the F1 world drivers championship. He owns the F1 record for career race victories and is tied with Micheal Schmacher for the most drivers championship
