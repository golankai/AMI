Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor most famously know for playing Harry Potter in the globally recognizable film franchise of the same name. He played Harry Potter in all eight of the films. The first being in 2001 and the last being in 2011.
Since starring in the Harry Potter franchise he has played parts in many films such as Horns, Train Wreck and Lost in London. Although none of these films have reached the levels of the Harry Potter films. 
Daniel Radcliffe has been with his partner Erin Darke since 2012 who he met on the set of Kill Your Darlings.
As well as starring in many films, Daniel Radcliffe has also become well know for playing many roles in theatre and plays.
