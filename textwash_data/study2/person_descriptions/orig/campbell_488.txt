Naomi Campbell is one of if not the most instantly recognisable and well known supermodels for the world. She featured in music videos from a very early childhood age, and rose to fame in the modelling industry. She has walked in fashion shows around the world for numerous fashion hauses, including Alexander McQueen, Gucci, Versace and Dolce and Gabbana. Naomi Campbell became notorious not only for her rivalry with Tyra Banks, but also because of her alleged physical altercation with an assistant.
