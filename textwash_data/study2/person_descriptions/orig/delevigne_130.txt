Cara Delevingne is a British solo artist who has had a number of hits in her new found career as a singer. 

Prior to singing she was a model and won several awards in the late naughties (2009). 

Cara took to acting and has been in a number of adverts, films and tv shows. 

She has the stereotypical characteristics of a model in that she is tall, slim and glamorous. Her look often changes but she is instantly recognisable due to her piercing eyes. 

Cara was born in London and grew up in Belgravia.
