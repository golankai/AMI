Emma Watson was born in Paris in 1990 to British parents. She reached early stardom as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter Films. She studies English Literature at Brown University, and is also a goodwill ambassador for the UN. She also supports sustainable fashion, and its a fashion reference for the youth. 
In more recent years, she played the role of Belle on the real life adaptation of Disney's The Beauty and The Beast, and played Meg March on the latest Little Women movie adaptation.
A self-declared feminist, she refuses to discuss her relationships with the press, but is rumored she has been in a relationship with  American businessman Leo Robinton since 2019.
