Catherine Elizabeth Middleton , The Duchess of Cambridge, was born on the 9th January 1982. She was born in Reading to parents Michael and Carole Middleton. She was brought up in Bucklebury, Berkshire.
She attended St Andrews university in Scotland, where she met Prince William. 
Catherine worked in retail and marketing, also getting involved in charity work.
The engagement of Catherine and Prince William was announced in November 2010.
They married on the 29th April 2011 and have three children, Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis.
The Duchess of Cambridge is involved with many charities to do with art, sport, early years  and mental health. 
The Duchess of Cambridge is the likely future queen consort if her husband takes the royal throne.
