Christian Bale is an English actor and has played the film lead in many award winning Hollywood films. One of his most notable early roles was as Phillip Bateman in 'American Psycho', where he gained a lot of popularity as an actor. He was cast in Batman Begins, and lead on to play the character Batman in The Dark Knight and it's sequel: the most successful Batman film franchise ever created. He has been acting since 1982 and has announced no plans to retire. At the age of 47, he has two children.
