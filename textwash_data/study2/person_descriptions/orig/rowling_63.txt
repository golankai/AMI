An English singer song writer who identifies as non-binary. His rise in popularity came from when he got awarded with the Brits critics choice award in 2014. He has also won a golden globe and Oscar for his song writings on the wall which featured in the bond movie spectre.  Sam was born in London in 1992 and was bullied in school for being overweight, he had liposuction surgery in order to lessen the bullying. Sam smith has had great success with 4 Grammy awards, 3 Brits, 3 billboard music awards, golden globe and an Oscar.
