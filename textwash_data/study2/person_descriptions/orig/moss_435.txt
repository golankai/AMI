Katherine Ann Moss was born on 16th January 1974 in London and is a British supermodel and businesswoman.
She rose to fame in the 1990s in which she collaborated with Calvin Klein. Kate received an award at the 2013 British Fashion Awards to acknowledge her contribution to fashion and she is also a contributing fashion editor for British Vogue. She has had drug allegations reported over the years which lost her some big contracts; to which she has recovered.
