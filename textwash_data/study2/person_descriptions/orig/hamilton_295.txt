Lewis Hamilton is a very famous formula one race car driver. Lewis Hamilton is British, and is 36 years old. He has been awarded an MBE and has the title of 'Sir' after receiving a knighthood by Queen Elizabeth II as part of a New Years honours list which recognised British individuals who worked to defeat coronavirus. Since 2014, Hamilton has won 78 races out of 153. Lewis Hamilton was previously in a long-term relationship with Pussy Cat Dolls lead singer Nicole Scherzinger. The pair were on and off dating for seven years, and split in 2015.
