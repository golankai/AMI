Daniel Craig was born on the 2nd March 1968 in Chester and is an English Actor. He is most commonly known for his roles in James Bond. Daniel was married to first wife Fiona in 1992 and has since married his second wife Rachel in 2011, who he is still married to. Daniel has 2 children. Daniel trained at the national youth theatre and graduated form the Guildhall school of Music and Drama in 1991. Daniel has received 77 nominations and won 12 awards. Daniels earliest film was the Power of One with his most recent being the James Bond film due to be released in 2021
