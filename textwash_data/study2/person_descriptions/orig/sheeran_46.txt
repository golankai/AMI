Edward Sheeran, or Ed Sheerman, is an English singer and songwriter. Born 17 February 1991, he is a best selling music artist. He has quite a distingushing appearance, including ginger hair. He has dated notable Hollywood actresses and singer-songwriters, perhaps most famously Taylor Swift. He remains a popular music artist and recently released his fifth studio album.  In 2017 and 2018, lawsuits were brough against him for plagiarism. He is from Halifax, Yorkshire, and currently resides in South London. He is considered by The Sunday Times in the Rich List as the 17th richest muscian in the UK (2019) with a net worth of $200,000,000 (2020).
