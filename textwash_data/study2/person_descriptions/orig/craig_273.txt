Daniel Craig is best known for playing James Bond. The first James Bond film he starred in was Casino Royale and he has since been in three more. These being Quantum of Solace, Sky fall and Spectre. His latest film No Time to Die has finally been released in 2021 after a delay caused by the Covid pandemic. For his role in Casino Royale he was nominated for a BAFTA award. He is an English actor and is 53 years old. Daniel Craig was born in Chester. He has been married twice and has two children.
