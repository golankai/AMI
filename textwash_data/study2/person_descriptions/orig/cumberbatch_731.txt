Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor that has starred in many films and Tv shows.  His most notable TV show is Sherlock in which he started alongside Martin Freeman playing the lovable detective and sidekick Sherlock Holmes and John Watson.  The pair ran around London and solved crimes that would eventually bring them face to face with a mortal enemy.  Benedict has had a glorious career so far and started in many films including The Hobbit, Four Lions, The Current War and has starred in the Marvel franchise as Dr. Stephen Strange.  Away from the screen, he enjoys a private life and rarely appears in public, although he does make it out for his friends, most notably Judge Rinder.
