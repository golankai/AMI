Ricky Gervais is an outspoken British actor, stand up comedian, writer and director. He is best known for his creation of the tv series 'The Office' and has had a number of nominations and won a number of awards for this sitcom. In later years he has created a comic travel documentary 'An Idiot Abroad'.  He has hosted the golden globes awards ceremony on a number of occasions. As the host of the golden globes in 2020, he used his comedy and his dry humour to create controversy and highlight the issues that had recently plagued the Hollywood elite, such as Harvey Weinstein's sexual abuse of women that he accused Hollywood of turning a blind eye towards.
