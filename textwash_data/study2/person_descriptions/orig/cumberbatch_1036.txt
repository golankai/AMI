Benedict Cumberbatch is a 25 year old English actor, best known for his role as Sherlock Holmes. He has received numerous awards for his acting, including an Emmy, BAFTA, and Olivier Award. He studied at Harrow school before pursuing his career in the arts. After studying at LAMDA, he then started his professional career in the theatre before then taking on screen roles too. He is famously known for not being able to say the word 'penguins' correctly, as mentioned in an episode of Graham Norton. He is an ambassador for The Princes Trust, and partakes in many charitable endeavours. Benedict is a buddhist and vegan, subscribing to a meditative lifestyle. He is married to theatre director, Sophie Hunter.
