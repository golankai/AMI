Catherine, duchess of Cambridge, original name in full Catherine Elizabeth Middleton, known as Kate was born January 9, 1982 in Reading, Berkshire, England), and is married to Prince William, Duke of Cambridge who is second in line to the British throne

She married Prince William in 2011 at Westminster Abbey. She is the mother to Prince George, the third in line to the throne, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis.

Catherine was the eldest of three children of Michael and Carole Middleton who were both British Airways employees who then set up their own party business; her siblings are Philippa (Pippa) and James.
