J.K. Rowling, aged 56, is an English author. She is best known for her penmanship in writing the Harry Potter series. J.K. Rowling experienced a period of misfortunes after originally having the idea for Harry Potter. She experienced the death of a parent, divorce from her first husband as well as relative poverty in which she was living on benefits. Rowling also had an overall approval for the scripts of the Harry Potter movie franchise. J.K. Rowling also uses the pen name ‘Robert Galbraith’ for many of her crime fiction stories.
