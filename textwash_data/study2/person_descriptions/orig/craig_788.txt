Daniel Craig is a British actor. He is born on the 2nd of March 1968 in Chester. He is a world known actor mostly for his role in James Bond where he plays the protagonist. He is the sixth James Bond behind Pierce Brosnan. He plays mostly action movies but also in drama and polars. He went to the Guildhall School of Music and Drama and graduated in 1991. His first big role was for the mini série Our Fiends in the North. His last film is Knives Out by Rian Johnson but we’ll see him in theatre for the last James Bond « No Time to Die » in 2021. He married Rachel Weisz the 22th of June 2011, and together they have a girl.
