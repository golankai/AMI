Christian Charles Phillip Bale is a famous English actor. He was born in Wales to parents Jenny James and David Bale. His father David was an entrepreneur. Christian has two sisters. Christian although born in wales, identifies as English not Welsh. Christian trained in ballet when he was a child and then he starred in adverts. Christian bale is most famous for his role in Batman, the dark knight rises. Christian decided very early on in life that he wanted to be an actor, In fact by the time he was ten he had decided his career.
