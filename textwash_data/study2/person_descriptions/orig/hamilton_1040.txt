Lewis Hamilton is a 31 year old British Racing Driver. He competes in the F1 championships, most recently for Mercedes but for other brands before that. Hamilton is known to be sporty, and as a keen Arsenal fan he says that if he did not have the career he does, he would be a footballer or sportsman instead. He has been subject to horrendous racial abuse throughout his career, and has been an advocate for anti-racism and increased diversity. He is also an advocate for environmental and animal rights, and donated to the Australian Bush Fires in 2020.
