Ed Sheeran is a much overrated singer songwriter from the UK. He is very popular with children and teens. He is a supporter and shirt sponsor of Ipswich football club. He has generated lots of controversy over his planning issues and plans for building various buildings on his farm property; including a pub. He was found to have copied a TLC track and was court ordered to credit them on his track. He is ginger and has made an advert for Heinz Tomato Ketchup. His girlfriend is a child-hood sweetheart. As well as singing, he also works as a music producer and owns a record company. He works internationally with many other performers and collaborators.
