Emma Watson is a British Actress who is most commonly known for her role in the film adaptations of J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter series as Hermione Granger. She is also a notable figure in the Women's Rights movement and a strong believer in gender equality as well as an outspoken feminist.

Emma Watson was born in 1990 in Paris, France and raised in Oxfordshire, and obtained her first acting role at the age of eleven as Hermione Granger after only previously acting in school plays. Watson also had a career in modelling which began in 2005 after starring in Teen Vogue, making her the youngest person to cover the magazine.
