Dame Judi Dench is an English actress.  She has starred on the stage and in films. She played Queen Elizabeth I in Shakespeare in Love in 1998 which won her an Academy Award for best supporting actress. Other films she has starred in include Mrs. Brown, Iris and Notes on a Scandal. Her theatre work  includes Cabaret, Macbeth and Hamlet. One of her most famous roles is playing M in James Bond film Goldeneye, she carried on this role until 2015 with the release of Spectre. Born in 1957, she married her husband Michael Williams in 1971, they have one daughter together who is also an actress.
