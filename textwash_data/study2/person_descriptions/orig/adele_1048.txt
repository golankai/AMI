Adele is an English singer. She also writes her own songs. She has made numerous hit songs such as  “Rolling in the Deep”, ”Skyfall” and “Rumour Has It”. She was born on the 5th of May 1988  in London. She studied at BRIT School for Performing Arts and Technology. At the school, she studied with other classmates who later became famous such as Leona Lewis and Jessie J. She has won numerous awards such as Brit Awards and Grammy Awards. She had a throat surgery in 2011 and made a comeback at Grammy Awards in 2012.
