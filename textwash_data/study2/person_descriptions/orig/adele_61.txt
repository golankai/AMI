Adele is a singer songwriter born in London in 1988, she is know for having attended London a famous arts school, Brit school. She is one of the best selling artists of all time having sold over 120 million copies of her albums. She rose in popularity by winning the Brit award rising star. Adele has only created 3 record breaking albums 19,21 and 25 all which have made her enter the Guinness book of world records for the longest time at number 1 and for having the most singles in the top 10 at once. She also recorded and sung a song for the bond movie sky fall which earned her a golden globe and Oscar.
