Edward Christopher Sheeran is an English singer-songwriter, record producer, musician, and actor. His lucious red hair, casual attire and of course catchy songs have lead him to become one of the best selling artists in the music industry. He is the proud owner of mnay awards, including Brit awards and Grammy awards for best solo artist and song of the year. Some of his best releases are 'Perfect', Castle on the hill' and 'Shape of you'. Born on 17 February 1991, he joined his local church choir at 4 years old  and leaned to play the guitar by 11.
