Ed Sheeran is known around the world mainly as a musician. He is a singer/songwriter and record producer and has also played small cameo roles in several films.
His genre of music would be classed as indie. He has had 11 number ones and his most famous song is 'Shape of you'.
Ed plays the guitar on all his music and has released 3 solo studio albums and 43 solo singles. He has also written songs for other artists.
Ed is from England and is married to Cherry Seaborn. He has one child, Lyra Antarctica Seaborn Sheeran.
