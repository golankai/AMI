Kate Moss is an international supermodel known for her waife like appearance. She's white, female, 47 years old, a mother of one and London based. She rose to fame during the 1990s when a fashion of very skinny, 'heroin chic', size zero models was in vogue. She later received a lot of criticism for drug use and was dropped from a number of campaigns. During her career she worked with the most famous and exclusive brands including Burberry, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Givenchy, YSL, Alexander Wang etc. She is now a designer in her own right. She's also had the largest gold statue since Ancient Egypt era exhibited in the British Museum.
