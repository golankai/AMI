Sam Smith is an English songwriter and singer. He is famous for his single Latch, which was very high on the UK Single Chart. His other famous singles, that number one singles are La La La and Money on My Mind. His international success has been recognised with the release of the single Stay with Me, which reached number one in the UK and number two in the US. Sam won the Golden Gold Award and the award for the best original song for the song Writing's on the Wall, which became a theme for the James Bond film Spectre from 2015.
