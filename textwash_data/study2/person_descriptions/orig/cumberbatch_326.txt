Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor and performer who has taken on many acclaimed roles and performances winning awards and accolades in the process. He has been best known on television for playing the updated version of Sherlock Holmes. He has also performed in theatres, playing a tour de force performance of Hamlet that was sold out and shown in cinemas. He has gone on to play various film characters notably Dr Strange and in Star Trek. Cumberbatch's fans call themselves Cumberbitches.
