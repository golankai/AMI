This British actor is best known for his role as007 James Bond in five of the Bond films. He has piercing blue eyes and blond hair (unlike his Bond predecessors) and a suave accent. He is aged in his early fifties and has proven to be somewhat of an accent chameleon, showcasing various accents in several of his films. His film breakthrough came about in the early noughties when he appeared in films such as Lara Croft: Tomb Raider and  Layer Cake. He is married to another famous female British actor with whom he has a young daughter.
