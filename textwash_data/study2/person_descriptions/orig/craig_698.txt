Daniel Craig is an English actor, that has become best-known for playing James Bond in several films of the film series. He was born in 1968 in Chester. He is married to the actress Rachel Weisz and they have a daughter. He has another daughter from a previous marriage. He is known for his dislike of politics and politicians. He has often been outspoken about them, especially Tony Blair, but he supported Barack Obama in the presidential elections of 2008. He is also a football and rugby fan and a well-known supporter of various charities.
