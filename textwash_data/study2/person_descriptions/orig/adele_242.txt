Adele is an English singer who has been famous since around 2006. 
She is 33 years old and was born in London in England. 
She was married to Simon Konecki in 2016 but they got divorced in 2021. She has a son with Simon. 
Her music is pop music and she has received many awards including an MBE, Brit award, Grammy award and American music award.
Her album names are all named after the year she did them and called 19,21 and 25. 21 is certified platinum17 times and is the second best selling album in the UK.
