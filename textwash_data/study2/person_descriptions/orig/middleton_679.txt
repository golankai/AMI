Kate or Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, is a member of the British royal family. Her husband, Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, is second in the line of succession to the British throne, making Catherine a likely future queen. Born in Reading, Middleton grew up in Bucklebury, Berkshire. 
Kate has 3 children, George, Charlotte and Louie. Kate will often be seen across our new spreads looking posed and elegant with the nation and press on her side.
