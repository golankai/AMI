Christian Bale is a 47 year old, white, male, Welsh/English actor known for a variety of starring roles in major films. His first breakthrough film was a Spielberg war film, Empire of the Sun. His first widely recognised role was American Psycho in 2000 where he played a serial killer Patrick Bateman. He is widely known for undergoing major transformations for roles and a regular award winner including an Academy Award, several BAFTAs and golden globes. Bale was cast as Batman in Batman Begins and went on to star in The Dark Knight trilogy where he became a household name.
