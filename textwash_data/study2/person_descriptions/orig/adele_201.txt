Adele is a british singer and songwriter, easily one of the worlds best known and well selling singers. She won her first award in 2007. She has currently released 3 albums and has a fourth album coming out soon. 
She was born in London before moving to Brighton. 
She gave birth to her first and only child in 2012 to her then partner Simon Konecki. 
She  is supportive of LGBT community even becoming ordained so she could minister at a close friends wedding. 
Adele is a major contributer to charity.
