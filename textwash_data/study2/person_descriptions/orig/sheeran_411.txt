Edward Christopher Sheeran MBE born 17 February 1991 is an English singer-songwriter, record producer, musician, and actor.  Edward Christopher Sheeran was born in Halifax, West Yorkshire, England on 17 February 1991. His early childhood home was on Birchcliffe Road in nearby Hebden Bridge. 
Sheeran began recording music in 2004 and independently released his first collection of work, tiled Spinning Man. He announced he was setting up a record label, Gingerbread Man Records. He is married to Cherry Seaborn.
