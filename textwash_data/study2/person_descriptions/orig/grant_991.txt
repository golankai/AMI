Hugh Grant is a British Actor, born 9th September 1960 in London to parents Susan and James. He made is debut in an Oxford-financed film named 'Privileged' having first received attention for his performances in costume dramas including Merchant-Ivory films Maurice and The Remains of the Day. 

International success came after he appeared in the romantic comedy film Four Weddings and a Funeral, for which he was awarded the Golden Globe and Academy Film Awards for Best Actor.Through this he cemented himself as the leading man in romantic comedies going on to star in many more; most notably Notting Hill and Bridget Jones's Diary.
