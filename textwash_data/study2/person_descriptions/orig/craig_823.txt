Daniel Craig is an English actor who was born in Chester. He was born on March 2nd, 1968, making him 53 years of age. His full name is Daniel Wroughton Craig. He is married to actress Rachel Weisz.
He plays the current James Bond and has made five of the films. The first film he starred in was The Power of One in 1992 and his first leading role was in Layer Cake in 2005.
Daniel Craig was paid around 24 million pounds for his role in Spectre. This makes him highest paid actor to have played Bond.
