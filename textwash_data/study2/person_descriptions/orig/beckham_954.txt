David Beckham is a former professional football player, most famously played for both Manchester United and Real Madrid. He was also the England football captain winning 100 caps for his country. He has been married to former spice girl Victoria Beckham since the 4th of July 1999. They have 4 children named Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper who is the only girl the couple have. David now owns a major league soccer team named Inter Miami and has since opened the ""David Beckham Acadamy"" in London and Los Angeles. In his playing career, he won 6 premier league titles with manchester united and 1 la liga title.
