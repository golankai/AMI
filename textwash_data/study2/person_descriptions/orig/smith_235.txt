Sam smith is a 29 year old singer from the UK. They rose to fame when they featured on Disclosure's song, 'Latch'. Since then, they have released 3 full length albums and many singles both independently and with other artists. Some of their most famous songs include, 'Money on my Mind', 'Pray' and 'Like I can'. They also performed a song for the James Bond film, Spectre titled 'Writing's on the Wall'. After coming out as gay and then gender-queer, they came out as non-binary, using they/them pronouns in September 2019, stating that 'after a lifetime of being at war with my gender, I've decided to embrace myself for who I am, inside and out...'
