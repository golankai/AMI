Ed Sheeran, born 17th February 1991 is an English singer song writer who has sold more than 150 million records worldwide, He was born in Halifax and grew up in Sussex. He sang and played guitar from a young age and had his first hit single ""A team"" reach number 1 in 2012, a year after he released his debut album(+)
He has gone on to release many successful singles and albums and has also won many awards including Brits and Grammys. He is also Spotify's second most streamed artist of the decade.
Although his escapades with his celebrity friends are widely talked about Ed does tend to keep his private life private, we do know that he married a childhood friend Cherry Seaborn in 2019 after 4 years of dating and they have a daughter - Lyra, born in 2020.
