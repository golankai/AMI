J. K. Rowling is a british writer / novelist, most widely known for having written the Harry Potter series of books, including also supporting the theatre adaptation and spin-offs such as Fantastic Beasts.
She lived in Portugal when she was younger and worked there as a teacher. She got married there and had a kid, though later got divorced.
She gives off a quite composed, maternal and wise impression. She also interacts with fans at times, e.g. to reveal secrets or nuggets to do with her previous works, on Twitter or on Pottermore.
There have been some controversies on some of her remarks around transsexuality, though the full story and context may not be fully clear.
She is the first billionaire author and one of the richest - if not the richest - in the UK
