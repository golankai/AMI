Lewis Hamilton is a successful formula one race car driver. He currently races for Mercedes after having a long career racing for McLaren. He is incredibly successful and holds records for most wins, most pole positions and podium finishes. 
He joined the McLaren hound driver program in 1998 and made his formula one debut with McLaren in 2007. This made him the first black driver to ever take part in the series. His his debut he has gone from strength to strength and is still one of the most well known formula one racers.
