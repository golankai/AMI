Cara Delevigne is a model actor and singer best known for her role in Paper Towns as Margo. 
She is of English birth born and raised in London, Delevingne is a depression and dyspraxia sufferer.
She began modelling at just 10 years old before being scouted by Burberry in 2012.
Since 2015 she has been on hiatus from the modelling world but continues to appear in advertising campaigns and some magazines. 
She also made her debut in 2017 as an author with a heavily LGBT themed book called Mirror, Mirror.
Delevinge openly identifies as pan sexual and bi sexual she is also genderfluid.
