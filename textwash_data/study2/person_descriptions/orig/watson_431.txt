Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson was born on 15th April 1990; she is an English actress and activist. She first became famous at the young age of 12 as Hermine Granger in JK Rowlings: Harry Potter. She completed her role in all the films.  She was recently ranked as one of Forbes's highest-paid actresses. Being such a young actress she did not complete her studies so reattended University between 2011-2014 to study her Bachelor's degree in English literature. she was appointed a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador and helped launch the UN Women campaign HeForShe, which advocates for gender equality, and in 2018 helped start the times up movement.
