Sam Smith is an English singer and songwriter, aged 29.   

He has won many awards including Grammys, Brit Awards and also a Golden Globe.  As of this year, he has sold over an amazing 30 million albums worldwide.  Some of Smith's best selling songs include Dancing with a Stranger, Stay with Me, I'm Ready (with Demi Lovato) and he also wrote the theme song  ""Writing's on the Wall"" with Jimmy Nape to Bond film Spectre in 2015. 

Smith came out as gay in 2014 and was in a relationship with model Jonathan Zeizel.  The relationship ended and it is not know if Smith is currently in a relationship.
