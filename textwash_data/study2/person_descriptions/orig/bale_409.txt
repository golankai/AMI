A serious actor with an unmistakeable gift for his craft. His talent is so dynamic that he can play Batman, an American psycho and a skin and bones insomniac all with equal conviction. He has been nominated for a golden globe on several occasions and is regarded as one of the most influential actors in Hollywood, a huge leap from his humble welsh beginnings. His personal life is a quiet one and he is a married father of two. He however found himself in the middle of a scandal when he was reported to the police by his family members for assault in a hotel room. No charges were pressed however the incident left a black cloud over the actor
