Daniel Craig is a British actor who is well known for playing James Bond, the latest incarnation of the secrete agent based on the novels of Ian Fleming. His other work includes appearances in Our Friends in the North. Munich, and the Philip Pullman adaptation The Golden Compass. He famously appeared with the Queen in the opening ceremony of the 2012 London Olympic Games.  Craig, being a sensible person, was staunchly opposed to Brexit. He has also engaged in charitable work including Comic Relief. He has been married twice and has two children. He holds the honorary rank of Commander in the Royal Navy, that being Bond's nominal rank.
