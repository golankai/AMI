Kate Moss is a well-known English supermodel. She was born in South London in 1974. She was discovered by an agency at the age of 14 at JFK airport. During her career, she has appeared on more than 300 magazine covers and got featured in the campaigns of the top fashion houses like Chanel and Dior. She was also controversial in the media, however, due to her very slim figure, which brought a new trend into the modelling industry, as well as due to her substance abuse rumours. Her romantic relationships were also highly publicised, she was engaged to Johnny Depp in the 90's, and she now has a daughter with an ex-boyfriend. She is now 47 years old.
