Adele is a popular British musician who is famous for her soulful and powerful voice and strong, emotional ballads. She has won many awards including several Brits, Grammys and an Academy Award for her bond theme 'Skyfall'. Famous hits of Adele's include 'Chasing Pavements', 'Rolling in the Deep' and 'Hello'. The song 'Someone like You' from Adele's second album became one of the biggest selling digital singles ever and it is the third most downloaded single of all time in the UK as well as the fourth best-selling single of the 21st Century.
