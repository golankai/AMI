Lewis Hamilton is a highly successful British Formula one car racing driver.

Hamilton is regarded as one of the best racing drivers the sport has seen and he has raced for high profile teams such as McLaren and his current team Mercedes. Starting his opening Season in F1 for McLaren in 2007 he now holds claim to 100 wins and over 100 pole positions.

Born in Stevenage, Hertfordshire, England he became part of the Mclaren young driver programme in 1998.

Hamilton has made history to become the first and only black driver in the series which he has spoken publicly about and is a prominent figure in the fight against racism and increased diversity in motorsport.
