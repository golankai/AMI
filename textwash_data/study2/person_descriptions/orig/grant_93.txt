High grant is a British actor born in 1960. Presently single but had a long term relationship with actress and model liz hurley which sadly ended in 2000. He has 5 children.

He developed a reputation for being a funny and spontaneous romantic lead hence the success of various romantic movies such as four weddings and a funeral, Bridget Jones diary, Nottingham Hill and love actually. 

He has an impressive collection of awards including a golden globe and a academy award.

He once stated that acting was not his true calling in life and rather happened by mistake but he now enjoys it.
