David Beckham is am English footballer, most well know for playing for Manchester United and England. He is also famously married to posh spice, Victoria Beckham. 

Beckham signed for Manchester United when he was fourteen, the club that he had supported throughout his childhood. He played midfield and was well known for his free kicks. 

He made his premier.league debut in 1995 under the then manager alex Ferguson. He quickly became a regular in the starting line up and played wearing the number seven shirt. 

David married Victoria Beckham in 1999. They have four children together, Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper.
