Elton John is a renowned singer/songwriter and pianist from the UK. Elton John is one of the best selling music artists of all time.
Elton John has released over 30 studio albums and has performed with many other world leading artists. 
His style of music is eclectic ranging from rock, to pop rock to glam rock. 
Aside from his music career, Elton John established the Foundation Elton John AIDS Foundation which has raised millions over the years. 
Elton John is known for his outlandish outfits when performing. Elton John is married to his partner and they have 2 children together.
