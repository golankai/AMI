Judi Dench was born in 1934, and is a well-established British Actress. She is best known to the younger generation for her role in James Bond films, whereas older people may know her from Shakespeare-enactments. She has short grey/white hair and a stern look on her face which could also be said to be pretty glum given that her husband passed away. She has one daughter with an unusual name and a lot of other relatives. She has been in acting her entire life and continues to do so and has had one of the most successful careers being awarded coveted titles.
