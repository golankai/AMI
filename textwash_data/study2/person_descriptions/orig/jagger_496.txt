Mick Jagger is a 78 year old English singer-songwriter known for being in the Rolling Stones band. 

He was born 26 July 1943.
He has eight children. 

In 1989 he was enrolled in the rock and roll hall of fame. In 2004 he was added to the UK Music Hall of Fame. He's had 10 number one charting songs and 32 in the top 10. 

In 2003 he was knighted for his services to popular music and is now known as Sir Mick Jagger. His net worth is supposedly 360million. 

Jagger has also appeared in more than 20 films during his career.
