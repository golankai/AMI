Sam Smith is a popular British musician known for his strong, sometimes feminine sounding vocals. Smith sites both Adele and Amy Winehouse as influences and many of Smiths hits can be classes as power ballads. Smith became famous around 2012-2013 after featuring on singles of other artists, particularly Naughty Boy's single 'La La La' which peaked at number 1 in the British charts. Smith's other well-known songs include bond theme 'Writing's on the Wall', ballad 'Stay with Me' and 'Too Good at Goodbyes'.
