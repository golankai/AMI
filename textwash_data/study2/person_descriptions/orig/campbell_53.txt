Naomi Elaine Campbell was born on the 22nd of May 1970 in London. She is a British supermodel, singer and actress. Naomi also goes by the nickname of Black Panther. In 1980 she finished an elite ballet school Italia Conti Stage School. She had an early start in her career when she was only seven years old. Her first role was in Bob Marleys video for Is This Love. Her modelling career started when she was only fifteen years old. She was walking around the mall when the modelling agent spot her and decided to offer her a contract.
