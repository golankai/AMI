Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch CBE is an English actor and was born in 1976, he is well known on screen and has numerous awards to name a few, prime time Emmy award, British academy television award and a Laurence Olivier award. Benedict won the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role in a Play for Frankenstein, his performance in The Imitation Game earned him nominations for an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Golden Globe Award. He won a BAFTA TV Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role. In 2014 Time magazine included him in its annual list of the 100 most influential people in the world, and in 2015 he was appointed a CBE by Queen Elizabeth II in the 2015 Birthday Honours for services to the performing arts and to charity.
