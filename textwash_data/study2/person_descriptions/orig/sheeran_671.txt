Ed Sheeran is a highly successful English solo artist, famous for hits such as ‘shape of you’, ‘perfect’ and ‘gallway girl’. Ed has had hits all over the world and has been honoured with an MBE. Most recently, Ed performed at the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France as part of the Global Citizen Live concerts, announcing at the same time that he would be undertaking a world tour in 2022. Ed is married to his childhood sweetheart and they have one child between them. Ed has a love of Heinz tomato ketchup and has a tattoo of a bottle of the sauce on his arm.
