Kate Moss is an English model. She is 47 years of age, 5 foot 7 and has blonde dyed hair. 

Kate rose to fame in the 1990s and is known for her size 0 figure. 

She has had many clothing ranges throughout the years and has influenced the wear of ugg boots, skint jeans, Balenciaga Handbags and waistcoats to list a few 

It is said the popular Oasis song “Sunday Morning Call” written by Noel Gallagher is about her. 

Kate has just 2 child and is divorced as of 2016. 

In 2009 Kate Moss was highly criticised when she was queried with the phrase “nothing taste as good as skinny feels” in an interview and was accused of sparking eating disorders.
