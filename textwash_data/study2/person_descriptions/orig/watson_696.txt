Emma Watson is an actress best-known for her playing of Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series. However, after the success of the film series, she participated in small, independent films rather than big-budget films. She is a feminist and is an UN Women Goodwill ambassador and delivered a speech in New York City that became famous for her defence and support of gender equality. She has been described as shy, intelligent and sensitive. She has definitely been a role model for many people, especially women and girls.
