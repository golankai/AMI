Ricky Gervais is a British comedian and actor. He is best known for his stand up comedy and acting in roles such as David Brent in the British version of The Office. He has also starred in a lot of shows with Stephen Merchant, who commonly collaborate together.

Gervais' stand up comedy is often controversial which has gotten him into trouble on many occasions. He considers himself an atheist.

He has been consistently voted as one of Britain's best comedians. One of his most famous moments is the dancing scene in an episode of The Office where he dances to himself and sings the music. He also appeared in a cameo role in an episode of The Office (US).
