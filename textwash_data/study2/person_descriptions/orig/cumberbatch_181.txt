Benedict Cumberbatch is one of the UK's greatest actors. He is best known for his role in BBC's Sherlock which allowed him to break in to Hollywood and achieve roles such as Doctor Strange in the Marvel Studios eponymous movie and Alan Turing in the Imitation Game. Prior to his breakthrough into on-screen acting Cumberbatch was a huge stage name with one of his greatest roles being that of both Victor Frankenstein and his monster in Danny Boyle's production of Frankenstein. He has won an Emmy, a BAFTA, an Olivier award among many other awards.
