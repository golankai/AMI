Elton rose to fame in the 1960s and quickly rose to being one of the most widely known singers in the world. Elton John is a song writer, piano player and music composer. He has sold more than 300 million records on the over 30 albums he has composed. Elton John has had more than 50 top 40 hits including 7 number ones in the U.K. charts. Elton has famously been known for many rehab admissions and struggles previous marriages. He recently had a film made about him and his life called The Rocket Man.
