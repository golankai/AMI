Benedict Cumberbatch(born in 1976) is a British actor. He started his career in theatre, and then rose to fame on the big screen thanks to his role in the beloved Sherlock Holmes series in the 2000, he then joined the MCU in the role of Doctor Strange. Both his parents are actors so it was only natural for him to be attracted to the industry since a very young age. Father of 2, he won several awards between 2012 and 2019 for his performances, and 6 Emmy nominations but without actually winning one
