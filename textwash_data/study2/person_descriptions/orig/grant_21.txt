Hugh Grant is an english actor best known for his roles in Love Actually and About A Boy. He's extremely successful having won multiple acting awards including a Golden Globe. He tends to appear in romantic comedies including Bridget Jone's diary and is known for his good looks and floppy brown hair. He has a very strong upper class British accent which he uses for many of the characters he plays. Now in his 60's he's settling down to spend time with his family and three children but is still heavily involved in films and acting.
