Cara Delevingne is an English model, actress, and singer. Mostly known for her strong fluffy brows she shot to fame modelling for top designer brands winning her 'Model of the Year' at the British Fashion Awards. Delevingne started her acting career with a minor role in the 2012 film adaptation of Anna Karenina by Joe Wright. Her most notable roles include Margo Roth Spiegelman in the romantic mystery film Paper Towns, the Enchantress in the comic book film Suicide Squad, and Laureline in Luc Besson's Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets.
