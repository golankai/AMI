Daniel Craig is a 53 year old British actor, known predominantly for his role as James Bond.  He is the sixth actor to play Bond and, as with all actors who play Bond, he is very handsome with blonde hair and piercing blue eyes.  

He has played Bond in 5 films. The fifth film will be his final and is yet to be released due to delays caused by the Covid pandemic.  

Daniel lives in New York with his wife of 10 years actress Rachel Weisz.  They share one daughter but Craig also has children from his previous relationships.
