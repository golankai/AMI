Kate Middleton, whose real name is Catherine, is the Duchess of Cambridge married to Prince William, the Duke of Cambridge, they have 3 children together, George, Charlotte and Louis. She is the daughter of Michael and Carole Middleton. She has a sister Pippa and a brother James. She is involved with many charities and has a lot of involvement with mental health charities. She comes across as a very approachable and down to earth person who is a very hands on parent. She is also a very keen and talented amateur photographer and takes many of the official photos of her children that are released for birthdays etc to the media. She designed a garden for the Chelsea Flower Show which then inspired part of the playground on the Sandringham Estate.
