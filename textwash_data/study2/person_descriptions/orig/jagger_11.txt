Mick Jagger is a singer songwriter, actor and producer. He was the lead vocalist and founder of the band the Rolling Stones. His career lasted for six decades and he had a big influence on the Rock and Roll scene. Jagger has 13 number ones and was knighted in 2003.
Jagger's relationship with his bandmate also caused a stir as he described it as a love/hate relationship. At one point the two refused to tour together and Jagger went on his own solo tour which included him singing some Rolling Stones songs. 
Jagger is also famous for having eight children with five different women. 
Jagger's song writing along with Keith Richards remains to be some of the most successful in history.
