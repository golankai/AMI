Ricky Gervais is an English comedian. He is white with brown hair and looks to be average height. He was born in 1961 in reading in Berkshire. His first big role in the uk was as David Brent in the office, this started in 2001 which was set in slough. This show was sold to the US and they have their own version of the office. He also made the programme extras where he played tv extras and there were many celeb guests. He is famous in the uk and USA and has presented many award shows such as the golden globes etc of which he has won many. He advocates for lots of charities for human rights and animal welfare.
