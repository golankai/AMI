Hugh Grant is a Britsh actor who has been acting since 1982. He was first noticthed whilst staring in costume dramas such as Maurice, The Remains of the Day and Sense and Sensibility. He gained more attention after staring in Four Weddings and a Funeral, for which he won a Golden Globe Award and a British Academy Award. Hugh has a reputation of playing the same type of character - typically as the lead charming , romantic character. Hugh has starred in many films such as Notting Hill, Bridget Jones' Diary, About A Boy, Two Weeks Notice, Love Actually and Paddington Bear. Hugh has had many relationships, one of which was with Liz Hurley. They were together for 13 years before they separated and he was made godfather to her son.
