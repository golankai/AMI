Ed Sheeran is an English singer and songwriter. He had released 4 studio albums which were massively popular with him touring for each album and having sold out shows. He also has released multiple singles from these albums and sold millions of copies, he is definitely one of the worlds best selling singers.
He was born in West Yorkshire but spent most of his time growing up in Suffolk, in the United Kingdom and is currently 30 years old.
He is also popular for his red hair colour. 
He also has an MBE awarded by the queen
