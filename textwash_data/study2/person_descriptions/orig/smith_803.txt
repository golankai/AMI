Sam Smith was born on the 19th of May 1993, in London. His full name is Samuel Frederick Smith. He grow up in Cambridgeshire. His parents are Frederick Smith and Kate Cassidy. Frederick was a truck driver and Kate was a Banker.  Sam Smith is a famous and talented singer and song writer, he has had many hit songs like ""like I can"" ""Stay with me"" ""latch"" plus many more. Sam has won many award like the ""academy award for best music"" also a Grammy award for ""song of the year"" ""record of the year"" ""best new artist""
