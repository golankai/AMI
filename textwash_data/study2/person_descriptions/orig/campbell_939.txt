Naomi Campbell was born on 22nd May 1970 in Streatham, South London.   Campbell attended the Italia Conti Academy of Theatre Arts as a child and into her teens and she wished to become a dancer.  But at age 16 Campbell was spotted by a model agency and her modelling career became very successful.  She appeared on the catwalk for Versace and by the end of the 1980s she had become one of the most recognised models of the decade.  Campbell is of Jamaican heritage and she became the first black model to appear on the covers of Vogue in both France and America.
