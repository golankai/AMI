Daniel Craig is a British actor, most well known for playing James Bond in four, soon to be five, James Bond movies. Daniel Craig is in his early 50s and is average height. As well as starring as James Bond he has also appeared in the films Munich, The girl with the dragon tattoo, and Knives out. Alongside the next James Bond movie (no time to die) he is also set to star in Knives out 2. Daniel Craig has had two romantic partners and has two children. He has been with his current partner for 10 years. He has been active in the film industry since 1992 and has been awarded many awards. He is for sure a well liked actor worldwide
