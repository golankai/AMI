Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver. Hamilton was born on 7 January 1985 and raised in Stevenage, England. Hamilton currently competes in Formula One for Mercedes, having previously driven for McLaren from 2007 to 2012. Hamilton clinched 176 podiums since his racing career started. He entered 281 Grand Prix races and is still one of the best drivers to date. He has the words ‘Still I Rise’ emblazoned across the back of his helmet and tattooed across his shoulders.

At the most recent Sochi Autodrom race track, Hamilton clinched the champion position, outperforming Lando Norris of McLaren at the last few laps. With this win, Hamilton has won his 100th Grand Prix.
