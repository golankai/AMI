Adele is a singer-songwriter and has found great universal fame due to her talents. She has achieved numerous accolades from the Brit Awards, Golden Globes etc. The lead single 'Hello' became the first song in the US to sell over one million digital copies within a week of its release. She furthered her repertoire by creating the single 'Skyfall' for the James Bond film of the same name. Her beginnings were in Tottenham, London, and she attended the BRIT school. Of recently, she has sparked discussions due her seemingly drastic weight-loss.
