Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch CBE is an English actor. Known for his performances on the stage and screen. He has played extremely famous roles including doctor strange in the Marvel films and his hit series Sherlock.  His performance in The Imitation Game (2014) earned him nominations for an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Golden Globe Award, all for Best Actor in a Leading Role.
