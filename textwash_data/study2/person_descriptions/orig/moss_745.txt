Kate Moss is a British model who was particularly prominent during the 1990s. Physically she is known for her very thin figure and long dark hair. She is 47 years of age and 5ft 5in tall. Some of her achievements include being on the cover of Vogue magazine, being named on of the world's top 100 most influential people by Time and receiving a special award from the British Fashion Awards in 2013 for her 25 years contribution to the fashion industry. She has been in several high profile relationships including with musicians Pete Doherty and Jamie Hince as well as actor Johnny Depp and has one daughter with magazine editor Jefferson Hack named Lila Grace.
