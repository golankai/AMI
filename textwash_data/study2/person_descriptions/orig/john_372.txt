Sir Elton Hercules John was born Reginald Kenneth Dwight in 1947. He is a very well known and highly regarded English musician, who is a singer, songwriter, pianist and composer. He has sold mover 300 million records and, therefore, one of the best-selling artists of all time.  
He was born in Greater London and has been involved in may movies, musicals and theatre, such as The Lion King and Billy Elliot. His life and career was dramatised in the 2019 biopic Rocketman. 
Since the late 1980s, Elton JOhn has been involved in the fight against AIDS and he established the Elton John AIDS foundation in 1992 and in 1993 he began hosting his annual Academy awards party.
