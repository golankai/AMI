Hugh grant is an English actor who has won many awards, to include a golden globe, BAFTA and Honary Cesar awards. He is 61 years old, born in London, has 1 sibling and 5 children. He first went to primary school in chiswick before moving on to a different school. And was educated on a scholarship, he received an offer to study a PhD in the history of art but didn’t take it as he didn’t have a grant. He also started dating Elizabeth Hurley in 1987 whilst filming and broke up in 2000 but he is the godfather of her child who was born in 2002, he went on to other relationships and one in particular with Tinglan Hong which was labelled as an affair.
