Lewis Hamilton is a Grand Prix Driver from Hertfordshire England,  he currently drives for the Mercedes Formula One Team having previously driven for the McLaren Formula one team.  Hamilton has won seven Formula One World Championships. Hamilton was encouraged to take up driving by his Father.  He famously dated Nicole Scherzinger, of Pussycat Dolls Fame, for a number of years.  Hamilton owns numerous properties in different countries, including The USA, Switzerland, and Monaco, where he currently resides.  He has been subjected to Racism since being a child and it has been said that he has been scarred for life over some the abuse he has been subjected to. He has been a prominent advocate against racism, not only in Motorsport but in general.
