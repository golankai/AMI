Judi Dench is an award winning British actor.  She has been on our screens for many years, in  TV, film and stage roles.  She is probably most well know these days for playing M in several James Bond films.  
She has an OBE and a DBE, several BAFTA's and two Golden Globes.
She is a very versatile performer who has acted in everything from Shakespeare to comedy. She appears on several cast recordings of musicals including Cabaret.
She was married to the actor Michael Williams and their daughter Finty is also an actor.
