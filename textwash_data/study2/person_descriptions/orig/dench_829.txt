Judith Olivia Dench is a well known female british actress born on the 9th December 1934. She has appeared in many award winning roles, theatre shows and films and most recently starred in the 2020 film release Six Minutes to Midnight. She received much recognition for her role in the James Bond movies, followed by many accredited awards including  six British Academy Film Awards, four BAFTA TV Awards, seven Olivier Awards, two Screen Actors Guild Awards, two Golden Globe Awards, and a Tony Award.
