As of 2015 he starred in 3 more installments with the 5th installment released this year. He originally began his career on the stage. He is now in his 50s and married to Rachel Weisz. He is quite a serious actor and has the smouldering look that James Bond always has. He is very swauve and convincing as the real James Bond. He was born in Chester and has blonde hair.
