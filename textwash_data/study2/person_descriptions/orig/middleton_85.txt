Kate Middleton, also known as Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge is a member of the British royal family. Middleton married into the British royal family in 2011 after years of an ""on again off again"" relationship with Prince William. The royal couple has 3 children together, Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis of Cambridge who are third, fourth and fifth in the line to the throne.

After marrying Prince William, the Duchess of Cambridge assumed royal duties and commitments in aid of the Queen. Middleton's charity work mainly focuses on issues surrounding, mental health, children, sport, art and addiction. One of the most notable campaigns the Duchess envisioned was the mental health campaign alongside the Duke and her brother-in-law Prince Harry. This campaign was coined Heads Together and called for conversations in around mental health to be more open to lessen the stigma.
