Kate Middleton is the duchess of Cambridge, born on 9th January 1982. She has brown hair. Kate married Prince William the Duke of Cambridge on 29th of April 2011, they now have three children, Prince George born 22nd July 2013,  Princess Charlotte born 2nd May 2015 and Prince Louis born 23rd April 2018. Kate lives with her family in Kensington Palace London. Her husband is in line to the throne meaning Kate will one day be the Queen of England. Kate has been put on many best dressed lists and always looks smart. Kates charity work focuses mainly on issues surrounding young children, mental health, sport, addiction, and art.
