Christian Bale is a 47 year old Academy Award-winning actor who has starred in such films as 'American Psycho,' 'The Fighter' and Christopher Nolan's 'Batman' trilogy. 

He was born in Haverfordwest, Pembrokeshire, Wales, and made his big-screen debut, with little formal training as an actor, at age 13 in Empire of the Sun (1987).

Most notably he lost over four stone for his role as an emaciated insomniac in The Machinist, and two to play Dicky Eklund, a real-life boxer and former drug addict, in The Fighter.
