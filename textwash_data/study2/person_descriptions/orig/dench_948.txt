Judi Dench was born in December 1934, and is one of England's most respected and well-known actresses. She has been nominated for several awards throughout her careers, and has been given four 'BAFTA's' and two Golden Globe's. She frequently appeared in the 'James Bond' film franchise, first appearing in 1995's GoldenEye, with her final appearance as the character 'M' in 2015's Spectre. Dench is a Quaker, and was married to the actor Michael Williams, who died in 2001. The couple have one child, Finty Williams, born in 1972.
