Ed Sheeran is a British singer and songwriter. He is on of the best selling artists and has sold over 150 million records. His first album + went to number one and the top hit was The A Team. Ed's next album was x and was the second best selling album of 2015. The song Thinking out loud earned Ed a grammy Award and was voted best solo performance. Ed did two other versions of the song and dueted with Andrea Boccelli and Beyonce. His third album as divide and the top singles were shape of you and castle on the hill. With these two songs Ed became the first artist to have 2 songs debut in the top 10 in the same week. Ed's fourth album was No 6 Collaborations Project and featured collaborations with many artists such as Stormzy and Camilla Cabello
