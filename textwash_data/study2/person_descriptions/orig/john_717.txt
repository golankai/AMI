Elton John is an English singer, songwriter, pianist and composer. He was born in 1947 and his official name is Reginald Kenneth Dwight. John has sold over 300 million records making him one of the best selling music artists of all time.

He has many great songs such as ‘Your song’ which aired on a movie called Moulin Rouge. Can you feel the love Is another great song which was on a 1994 cartoon movie called The Lion King.

Elton John has been fighting aids since the 1980s, to which he established the Elton John AIDS foundation which has since raised over £300million!

John has received 5 Grammy awards, five Brit awards, two academy awards, two golden globes, a tony award, a Disney legends award and the Kennedy center honor. 

He has been openly gay since 1988 and married partner David furnish in 2014 when same sex marriage became legal in the UK.
