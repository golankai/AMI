Dame Judy Dench is an English actress born in 1934. Is is regarded as one of the finest actresses in the world having been nominated 7 times for an academy award however only winning once (travesty),  six British academy film awards, 2 golden globes and 4 BAFTAS.
Dame Judy has starred in stage, tv and film spanning decades. In recent times she has played M in the James Bond films, the first time M has been played as a female part.
She has also depicted royalty several times - as Queen Elizabeth the first in Shakespear in love for which she won an Oscar and queen victoria in Mrs Brown.
