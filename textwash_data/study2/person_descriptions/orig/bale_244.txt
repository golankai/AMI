Christians full name is Christian Charles Philip Bale and he was born in Haverfordwest, Wales in 1974. He is 47 years old. 
He is an actor 
He is married to a lady called Sibi Blazic who he married in 2000. He has 2 children, Emmeline and Joseph. 
He has won and Oscar and 2 golden globe award for his acting. 
His first big role was when he was 13 in a Steven Spielberg film called Empire of the sun.
Other films he has starred in include : American Psycho, Batman Begins, The Dark Knight and The Dark Knight Rises, The Fighter, American Hustle.
He is an animal rights activist and does not eat red meat. He supports organisations such as green peace and the world wildlife fund. He regularly campaigns and fundraisers for these charities.
