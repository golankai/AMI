Ed Sheeran is a 30 year old singer-songwriter from the UK. He began his music career independently, playing in small venues and recording his own music. He eventually started uploading videos of his music onto Youtube where his fanbase rapidly started expanding. From then, he has released 4 full length albums entitled; '+' (2011), 'x' (2014), 'รท'(2017) and '=' (2021).

As well as his music career, Ed Sheeran is also a record producer and an actor. He has appeared in many films/tv shows, most noticeably; 'Game of Thrones', 'The Simpsons' and 'Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker'.
