Sam Smith is a British singer songwriter from London born to rich parents. His first track was alongside naughty of and has since gone gone to create the theme tune for the popular British film series james bond starring Daniel craig. He has appeared on many TV shows and has a lot of time in the press. He has recently come out as non binary and has received wide spread support for this. He sings pop soul and r&b

Sam Smith has received many awards such as album of the year, song of the year and many brit awards
