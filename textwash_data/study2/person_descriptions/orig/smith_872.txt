Sam Smith is an English singer and songwriter. Sam was born on 19 May 1992 and has openly come out as a proud homosexual man. Sam has a total of thirty-five million monthly listeners on Spotify and a growing global fan-base.

To date, Sam has released 21 albums globally. Sam is well-known for his songs Latch single in the Disclosure album, Stay With Me in the In the Lonely Hour album and the single Dancing with a Dancing With A Stranger.

Sam has won four Grammy Awards, three Brit Awards, three Billboard Music Awards, an American Music Award as well as a Golden Globe and an Academy Award through their career. His most recent 2021 award being the GLAAD Media Award for Outstanding Music Artist.
