Daniel Radcliffe was born in London in 1989, and became world famous when he played the role of Harry Potter in the movie version of JK Rowling magical universe. Since then he has taken several roles in both independen movies an theater plays without much success (as it often happens with child stars). 
He is engaged on several philanthropical and charitable projects.
Of Jewish ascendency, he owns places both in New York and London. He is dating actress Erin Darke, whom he met on the set of Kill Your Darlings, since 2012.
