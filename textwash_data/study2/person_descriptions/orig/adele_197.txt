Adele is an English singer best known for her her powerful voice and impressive ballads. When she was 18 years old, Adele wrote her debut hit ""Hometown Glory"", and has sales of over 120 million records. In 2011, Adele underwent throat surgery which her doctor blamed on her smoking habit. In 2015, she quit smoking, though she has frequent health issues, leading her to cancel a series of tour dates as she was at risk of never singing again. Despite experiencing world-wide fame and audiences of thousands, she still suffers from stage fright and has said in the past that she sometimes has panic attacks and throws up backstage.
