Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor, who is 45 years-old and was born in 1976. He has been involved in multiple different large movies series such as 'Sherlock Holmes' and Marvel. He is married  Sophie Hunter since 2015 and they have 3 children together. He has received multiple of Emmys and was knighted to commander by the Queen of England in 2015 for the performing arts and charity.

He has a condition called 'Heterochromia Iridis', which means his eyes have half green and blue in each eye, which has been sometimes to change colour in pictures.
