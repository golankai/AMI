Mick Jagger is an English singer, songwriter, actor, and film producer born on 1943 making him 78 years old. He was also born in Dartford, Kent.

His fame rose as the lead vocalist and one of the founder members of the famous Rolling Stones. His whole career spanned over six decades with most of his fame coming from the history of rock & roll.

Overall, Mick Jagger has released 15 solo singles and 5 solo albums. In 2003 he was rewarded for his contribution to music by being knighted Sir Michael Jagger.
