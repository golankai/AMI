Ricky Gervais is an English comedian, actor, director, producer and writer. He is mostly known for British comedy sitcom, The Office. Ricky Gervais was born in Whitley, Reading, Berkshire. He initially worked in the music industry. He attempted a career in the 1980's before turning to comedy. He began his stand up career in the 1990's. 
He is currently 60 years of age. He attended University College London intending to study Biology, However after two weeks, changed to philosophy. He is currently working on the latest season of After life, Which is being filmed in Beaconsfields, Buckinghamshire.
