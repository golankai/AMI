Daniel was born in Chester, England and gained notoriety by playing James bond since 2006.

Not wanting to be defined by his James bond role, Daniel branched out into other roles such as  the mystery thriller The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo (2011), and the mystery comedy Knives Out (2019).

Aside from acting, Daniel is passionate about his charity and humanitarian work for international womens day, Red nose day, S. A. F. E Kenya and opportunity network. 

Daniel is married to Rachel weiss and they have a child together with him also having a second child with a previous partner
