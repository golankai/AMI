David Beckham is a famous former professional English soccer player, and has been married to Victoria Beckham, a founding member of the famous English pop band The Spice Girls turned fashion icon, since 1999. He and Victoria have four children together. David is also known for being heavily tattooed. Additionally, he sponsored Samsung phones at one point. David Beckham now owns a few different soccer teams, and has been actively involved in philanthropy for nearly 20 years, particularly as an ambassador with UNICEF. David Beckham is also one of the highest paid and most decorated soccer players of our time.
