Daniel Craig is a 53 year old British actor most known for being the seventh man to portray secret agent 007 in the famed franchise ""James Bond"", replacing Pierce Brosnan. He began his role in the 2006 film ""Casino Royale"", which takes place at the beginning of Bond's career as Agent 007, falling in love with a lady while on a mission to bankrupt a terrorist financier. Since ""Casino Royale"" Craig has gone on to star in three more installments of the series, with an anticipated fourth expected to be released in winter 2021.
