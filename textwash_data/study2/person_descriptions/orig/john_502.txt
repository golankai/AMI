Elton John was born in 1947 and is a singer, composer, songwriter and sometimes actor. He is known for his hit songs such as 'Don't go breaking my heart' and 'Your song' and his humour on many topics such as politics and how other celebrities look. He has two adopted children, who are 8 and 10 years-old. 

Elton John has been married two times with Renate Blauel from 1984 to 1988 then David Furnish since 2006. He was awarded knighthood by the Queen of England in 1998, making him 'Sir Elton John' for his music and arts, which represented the culture and life of the English Country.
