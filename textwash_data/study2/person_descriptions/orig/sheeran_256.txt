Edward Christopher Sheeran is an English singer, song writer, musician and actor.  He was born on the 17th February 1991 in Halifax England.  His dad John was a curator and his mum Imogen worked in an art gallery.  He has an older brother called Matthew who is now a composer. He comes from a large Irish family. He sang at a local church from age 4, learning the guitar at 11.  He was a natural performer and accepted into the National Youth Theatre as a teenager. He began recording music  independently in 2004 gaining international success in 2011.  Now married to his childhood girlfriend and have one child together
