Lewis Hamilton is one of the most famous British racing drivers. He has won many world championships. He is regarded as one of the greatest racing drivers of his generation. He has engaged in philanthropy and activism to an extent but many regard this as  disingenuous. Furthermore as he has amassed greater wealth he has been portrayed as rather arrogant and pompous in the media. Moreover he has made numerous public comments that have been received negatively. For example criticizing his hometown a working class area, or reinforcing heteronormative gender roles in children. He publicly criticized a male child relative for wanting a princess dress. Lewis Hamilton has Swiss residency to avoid paying tax. He is also noted for his stereotypically Nouveau riche appearance.
