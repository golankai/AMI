Adele born May 1988 is an English singer and songwriter she recently divorced spouse Simon konecki after 5 years of marriage. They had one child together Angelo James who was born October 2012.

Adele released a song call ‘sky fall’ which she co wrote and recorded for the hit movie for James Bond which was also called Skyfall. 

She has released many famous hits over the years including rolling in the deep, someone like you and set fire to the rain.

She has so far released albums 19, 21 and 25. Adele has achieved 15 Grammy awards and 9 Brit awards. 

A fun fact is that Adele wrote her debit hit Hometown Glory in just ten minutes. Her album 21 is one of the best selling albums of all time in both the UK and the US.
