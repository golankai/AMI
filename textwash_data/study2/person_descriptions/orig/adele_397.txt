Adele is a UK born singer who has also made it big in America. She is one of the worlds best selling artists and has sold over 120 million records. She is currently 33 years old and has been married to her husband since 2016. Adele graduated from the BRITs school in 2006 and immediately got signed to XL recordings. In 2007, she received the Brit Award for Rising Star and won the BBC Sound of 2008 poll. Her debut album 19 went 8x platinum. She has won multiple Grammys over the years. Her second album 21 was released in 2011.
