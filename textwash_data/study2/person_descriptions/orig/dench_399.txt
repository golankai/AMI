Judi dench is an English actress, world renowned. She is currently 86 years old. She has starred in a large range of films from the James Bond franchise to Shakespearean dramas. Judi has one child - 
Finty Williams. Her husband died in 2001 and they were married for 30 years. The 1966 BAFTA Award for Most Promising Newcomer to Leading Film Roles was made to Dench for her performance in Four in the Morning and this was followed in 1968 by a BAFTA Best Actress Award for her role in John Hopkins' 1966 BBC drama Talking to a Stranger.
