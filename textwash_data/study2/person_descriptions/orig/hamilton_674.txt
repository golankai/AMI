Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula one driver. Lewis is well known for his life off the tracks and had a long term on and off relationship with the Pussycat Doll Nicole Scherzinger. Born in Stevenage, Hertfordshire, Lewis is a vegan by choice and was awarded an MBE by HRH Queen Elizabeth. Lewis is ranked as one of the richest sportsmen in the world and as such lives in Switzerland to avoid paying large amounts of tax to the British government. He currently drives for Mercedes but has previously driven for mclaren.
