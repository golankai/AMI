Hugh grant is a British actor most known for his role in Notting hill in which he starred alongside Julia Roberts. He is also very well known for starring in Bridget Jones diary. He is very educated having studied at the University of Oxford. He studied English literature but was part of the drama society and although acting was not his initial calling he really enjoys being an actor.  He has 5 children and is married to Anna Eberstein. He used to date Elizabeth Hurley but the pair broke up in the year 2000.
