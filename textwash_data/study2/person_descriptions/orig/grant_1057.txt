Hugh Grant is a 61 year old English Actor. He has been awarded a multitude of awards for the successes of his movies, including a BAFTA Award as well as a Golden Globe Award. He first came into the spotlight after his performance in Maurice in 1987, The Remains of the Day in 1993, Sense and Sensibility in 1995 and Restoration in 1995. He has since gone on to play a variety of different roles in a range of different genres. Grant is known for his meticulous preparations for his roles in order to make his characters effortless and realistic.
