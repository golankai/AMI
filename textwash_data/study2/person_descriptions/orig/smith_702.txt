Sam Smith is a British singer songwriter with many popular songs such as ""Like I can"", ""Too good at goodbyes"", ""Stay with me"", and ""Dancing with a stranger"". Sam Smith is 29 years old and has 2 siblings. Sam Smith has been active in the music industry since 2007 when they featured on a track called ""Latch"" by Disclosure. Sam Smith has received a fair few rewards for their singing and song writing abilities. Sam Smith also received masses of support after coming out as non-binary in 2019 and is a well known and supported member of the LGBT+ community
