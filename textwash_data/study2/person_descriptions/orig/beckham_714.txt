David Beckham is an English footballer who played most notably for Manchester United, Real Madrid, LA Galaxy and Paris Saint-Germain. He first played professionally for Manchester United at the age of 17 in 1992. He played for England internationally, competing in several world cups and Euros and was captain of the England team for several years. He was awarded an OBE for services to football in 2003. He is part of an iconic British family, married to Victoria Beckham, an ex-Spice Girl, and has several children - Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper. David Beckham is known for his style, hair and tattoos.
