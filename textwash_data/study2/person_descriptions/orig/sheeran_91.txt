Ed sheeran is an English singer/songwriter who also produces music. He is known as one of the best selling artists in the world following album sales of 150 million.

Ed sheeran was born in West Yorkshire in 1991. He is married to a childhood friend cherry and has one daughter, lyra.

His appearance is well known from his carrot top hair, baggy jeans and trainers. He also is recognisable by his tattoos one of which is a teddy bear. 

His story is magnificent how he went from homeless and busking on London streets to now a musician that has made history.

He has won far too many awards to possibly list but has also broke many world records. One being his album called divide released in 2017 which had the most top 10 singles to come off one album ever and was the top selling album in the whole world.
