Cara is a fun and energetic young actress and model. Her characteristic features such as eyes and voice make her an interesting character. She won the award for model of the year twice in the UK. She was born in 1992 an went to school in London.  She played in a couple of science fiction movies and even wrote one novel herself. She won several awards and even ranked nr 13 on the list of 99 most desirable women. She earned millions of dollars in the career and is now taking less work to have more time for herself.
