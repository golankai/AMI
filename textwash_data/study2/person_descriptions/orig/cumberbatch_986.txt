Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor who is most well known for starring in the hit TV show Sherlock. 
In recent years he has been known for playing Dr. Strange in numerous Marvel films. Benedict has been acting since the early 2000's but has only really reached global fame since the start of Sherlock in 2010.
Benedict is a very well spoken man and has a very typical English accent. He is one the most recognizable English celebrities around the world today.
Benedict has been married to his partner Sophie Hunter since 2015 and has since had three children his with her. The couple had known each for 17 years before deciding to tie the knot.
