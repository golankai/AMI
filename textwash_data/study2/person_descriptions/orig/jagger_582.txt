The Rolling Stones's Lead Vocalist Mick Jagger doesn't need any introduction. One living legend he definitely is. He is a singer, actor, songwriter and film producer. He is 78 years old. His current partner is Melanie Hamrick. In the past he was married once to Bianca Macias. He has eight children. He is the brother of Chris Jagger. He was born in Dartford. In 2003, he was honoured to be knighted for his service to the popular music. His career lasted 6 decades, and he is one of the most popular names in the rock and roll music industries.
