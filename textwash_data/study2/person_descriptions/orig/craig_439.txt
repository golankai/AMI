Daniel Craig was born in 1968 in Chester, England. He started acting in 1992. He is possibly best known for playing James Bond. His first movie as James Bond was Casino Royale. Although the most recent James Bond movie, No Time to Die, will be his last movie as James Bond. He played in American version of The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo based on Swedish novel from 2005. He did play a detective in Knives Out in 2019. 
He divorced Fiona Loudon in 1994. He is married to Rachel Weisz, also a British actress, since 2011.
