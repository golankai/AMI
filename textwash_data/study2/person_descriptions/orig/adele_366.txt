Adele is an English singer songwriter from Tottenham, London. She has had many big hits including Hello and Rolling in the Deep. She also sang the theme tune to the James Bond film Skyfall in 2012. She has one son with her ex husband who she divorced in 2021. She has received many nominations for various awards and has won 9 brit awards an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award, fifteen Grammy Awards, eighteen Billboard Music Awards, five American Music Awards, and two Ivor Novello Awards for Songwriter of the Year.
