Cara Delevingne is a British model and actress. She is 29 years old and is most widely known for her modeling career and her roles in ""Paper towns"" and ""suicide squad"". She began modeling when she was only ten years old, following in the footsteps of her sister. She is bisexual and genderfluid and has used her fame to raise money for some awesome charities. Since the age of 15, she has battled with depression and also has ADHD. She has been in a few public relationships, however she broke up with her latest girlfriend in 2020 after 2 years of dating. I think she is most well known for her iconic bushy eyebrows.
