Daniel Craig is a British actor best known for playing James Bond beginning with Casino Royale in 2006. Other Bond films include Quantum of Solace, skyfall, Spectre and more recently No Time to Die. He is the first Bond under 6 feet in height. He has been married to Rachel Weisz since 2011. 
Daniel Craig is muscular and sturdily built, he was a rugby player when he was younger. Daniel Craig began acting at age 14, starring in Shakespearean plays. In order to fund his acting training and career, he worked as a waiter.
