Mick Jagger is a British singer songwriter most well know for being the front man of the popular rock band The Rolling Stones. Mick Jagger is currently in his late 70s and has been present in the music industry since 1960, first as a solo artist and before later joining several bands including the rolling stones. Mick Jagger is well known in the rock scene and has made a lasting influence on new rock bands as they draw inspiration from his music and style. Mick Jagger has also won many awards along side his band the rolling stones for the music they created
