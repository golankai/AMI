Cara Delevingne is an English model, actress and singer. She was born 12 August 1992. She began modelling at the age of 10 after being signed to the Storm Management in 2012 she was the lead walk of the Chanel show in Paris Fashion Week. She has since gone on to do all the major fashion shows. She begun acting in 2012. Her first staring role was in an adaptation of John Greenes Paper Towns. In 2016 she played the Enchantress in the DC comic book adaptation of ""the Suicide Squad"", although it was a box office hit she was not invited back to reprise the role in the sequel.
