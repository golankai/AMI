Great comedian, writer and actor. Most well know from the series The Office which then went on to be commissioned in the States, where he has been living with his wife for many years after his initial success.
Dry sense of humour with witty comments.
Has appeared on many TV chat shows such as Graham Norton, Johnathon Ross. Now currently on a stand up tour of the UK and set to play at the London Palladium.
Multi award winning talent, Bafta's and Golden Globes. 
He has had roles in big blockbuster films as the height of his success such as Night at the Museum.
Physically,  not a model more like a cuddly beardy bear who definitely doesn't look 60.
