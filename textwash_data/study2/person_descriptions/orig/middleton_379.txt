Kate Middleton, full name, Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, is a British woman married to Prince William. They met at university, the university of St. Andrews . She was born in Reading, UK and grew up in Berkshire. She spent some of her childhood in Jordan due to her father being in the airline trade. She is 39 years old. She has three children, George, Charlotte and Louis. She has been involved in lots of charity work both before and after becoming a member of the royal family. Her husband William is second in line to the British throne, making Kate a likely future queen consort. She holds patronage with over 20 charities and is very involved in mental health charities.
