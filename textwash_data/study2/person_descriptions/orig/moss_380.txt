Kate moss, also known as Katherine Jane moss, is a British supermodel. She was born in 1974 in Croydon, London. She started to become famous in the 1990s and is still active today. She won an award in 2013 for her contribution to fashion over 25 years. She contributes to British Vogue, and has her own clothing range. She has been under fire for her drug use and wild lifestyle , but has since changed and recently was 2nd on the Forbes top earning models list. She has recently been in the news for being on the catwalk along with her 18 year old daughter Lila, who is her only child, in a Versace collaboration.
