Judi Dench was born in December 1934 making her 86 years old. She is also blonde and 1.55m tall.  

Her career included her being a successful actress with roles ranging from James Bond films to Shakespearean dramas. Some roles she played within Shakespearean dramas include Ophelia in Hamlet, Juliet in Romeo and Juliet and Lady Macbeth in Macbeth. Across her career she has worked for the National Theatre Company and the Royal Shakespeare Company.

Finally, Dench is also a seven-time Academy Award nominee.
