Kate (Catherine) Middleton is the wife of Prince William and the Duchess of Cambridge. She has three children: George, Charlotte, and Louis. Kate was born in Reading but was raised in Berkshire and first met her now husband at St. Andrews University where they were on and off again lovers for several years until Kate was finally forced into a marriage with William They were married in 2011 at Westminster Abbey in what was arguably one of the greatest weddings of the century and which attracted 17.6 million viewers. 
Nowadays, Kate works as a full time royal which involves a host of different types of work such as travelling to ex-colonies and walking in parades.
