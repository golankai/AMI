Ricky Gervais is a British stand-up comedian who is also known for his acting roles in Television comedy shows such as The Office which he also co-directed and Netlfix’s After Life.
Gervais was born on 25th June 1961 in Reading, Berkshire and was raised up in the Whitley area of Reading along with his sister Marsha and two brothers, Larry and Robert Gervais.
One of Gervais’s first notable appearances was in the Ricky Gervais show which consisted of an animated podcast type show where Gervais would discuss life with co-stars Karl Pilkington and Stephen Merchant in a comedic manner. This also brought Karl Pilkington to fame to later star in his own television shows such as An Idiot Abroad.
