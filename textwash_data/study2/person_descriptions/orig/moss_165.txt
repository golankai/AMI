She was spotted by a model agency at JFK airport at the age of 14 after a holiday in the Bahamas. in 2005 She was dropped by most of her labels as there were pictures in the headlines of her snorting drugs, whilst dating Pete Doherty. She had a daughter Lila Grace in 2002. She has played herself in several films such as Ab Fab and Oasis have writted a song about her. She came second of the forbes top earning list for models after earning 9.2m in one year. She has most recently walked the catwalk with her daughter at Milan fashion week, Kate also helps with Editing British Vogue magazine.
