Naomi Campbell is a British supermodel from London. She is now in her early fifties. She went on to become one of the worlds best well known models. She is of African Caribbean descent. 

Naomi Campbells first well known appearance was at the age of 7 in the video for Bob Marley's is this love. She first appeared on the cover of Elle magazine just before her 16th birthday. Her career quickly took off and she was soon modelling for well known designers such as Versace.

In 1998 she was the first black model to appear on the cover of French vogue
