Emma Watson is an English film actress most famously known for playing one of the leading roles as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter series of films. She was born in Paris in France on the 15th of April 1990. Since the end of Harry potter series, she continues to have a successful acting career and appeared in films such as ""Beauty and the Beast"" and ""Little Women"". She is also a feminist and an activist. She studied at Brown University and graduated with a bachelors degree in English literature.
