Ed Sheeran, born 17 February 1991 is an English singer-songwriter, musician, record producer and actor. He has so far sold more than 150 million records worldwide which makes him one of the worlds best selling music artists.

Ed Sheeran was born in Halifax, West Yorkshire, however in December 2005 Ed and his family moved from his childhood home in Hebden Bridge to Framingham in Suffolk. Ed Sheeran seems to have had an interest in music since he was a child, from singing in a choir since 4 years old, to learning how to play the guitar at 11, and writing songs since he was in school. In the early stages of his career Ed Sheeran would go busking out in his local community and play small venues before landing his first record deal.
