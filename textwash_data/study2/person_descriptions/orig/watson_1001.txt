Emma Watson is a 31 year old English actress born in Paris, France. Watson, made popular with her role as Hermione Granger in fantasy blockbuster series ""Harry Potter"" alongside Daniel Radcliffe, was named in 2015 as one of Time's 100 most influential people in the world due to her social efforts in support of feminine equity and equality. She started her acting career at just 10 years old in record breaking film ""Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone"". In the 21 years since, she has become remarkably wealthy, amounting a net worth of $85 million dollars.
