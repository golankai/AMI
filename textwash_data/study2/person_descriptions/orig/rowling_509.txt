JK Rowling is one of the most successful writes of her generation. In particular she is known for the Harry Potter series. At one point she was a Billionaire. However she has given much of her wealth to charity although she is still one of the richest people in Britain despite this. She has written in other genres after her initial success In her later years she has alienated many of her original fan base due to her rather outdated views on transgender rights. Many in the media regard her use of Twitter excessive.
