Judi was born in York. England and is considered one of the best British actresses due to her style being so adaptable to so many different genres. 

Judi Dench (dame) was honoured by the Queen in 1988 for her services to performance. Her acting ability has seen her in a wide range of roles including James Bond, period films such as Jane Eyre and Shakespearean classics such as Hamlet, Macbeth and Romeo and Juliet.

Judi Dench is also an advocate for charities and 
supports many organisations.  Dench is a president of a drama studio in London, President, a Patron of the British Shakespeare Association.
