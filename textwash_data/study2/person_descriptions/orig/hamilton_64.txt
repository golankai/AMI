A record breaking F1 driver, breaking rules by being the first black F1 driver along with the most wins. He currently drives from McLauren and has previously driven for Mercedes. A strong activists for black rights and campaigning for more black people to join the sport. He also campaigns regularly for animal, human and environmental rights, although owning his own private jet. Lewis is originally from England and he got into the sport by go-karting from a young age. He also has a half brother is a professional race car driver.
