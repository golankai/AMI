JK Rowling is a adult female (woman) writer who is most famous for the Harry Potter series which she thought up during a train journey. She lived in Exeter at some point and now lives in Edinburgh. She is also described as being a single parent at the time she wrote Harry Potter. 
She is a multi-millionaire and gives significant amounts to charity, 
She is left wing and has stated her support for Labour in previous elections. 
she is active on Twitter and is a feminist and has supported lots of feminist causes. 
Recently, she suffered online abuse from trans rights activists who claimed that she was transphobic and anti-feminist for asking questions about women-only spaces and rights.
