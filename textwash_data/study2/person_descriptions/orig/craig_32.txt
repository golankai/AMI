Daniel Craig is a British actor, most famous for the girl with the dragon tattoo and James bond films in which he plays the main character. Because of this he is an a list celebrity and a household name in the uk. 
He was involved with and trained at the national youth theater and then went on to drama school  starting his career on the stage before progressing to acting in film. He has been made an honorary commander in the British navy due to playing james bond, as the character also holds the title. 

Daniel Craig has been married a boucle of times and has a large expensive house in Brooklyn new York where he spends his time
