Elton John is a British singer and songwriter best known for hit songs such as ""I'm still standing"" and ""Candle in the WInd"" which he famously performed at Princess DIana's funeral. He has had more than 50 Top 40 UK hits and sold over 300 million records.

Elton John is an accomplished Pianist learning to play as a young child. His career started as a pub pianist before releasing his first album and rising to worldwide fame.

Elton John is well known for hosting an annual White Tie and Tiara Ball to raise money for his AIDS charity.
