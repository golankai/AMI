Daniel Radcliffe is a 32 year old actor from England. He is best known for his role as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter Franchise. Unlike Emma Watson however, this was not his first professional role, as he had performed in the 1999 David Copperfield film and The Tailor of Panama in 2001. 

During the 10 years playing Harry Potter in the Harry Potter franchise, Daniel gained worldwide fame and while it was difficult for audiences to see him as anyone but Harry, his acting career continued to grow after the franchise had ended.
