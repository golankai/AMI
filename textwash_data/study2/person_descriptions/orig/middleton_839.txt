Born Catherine Middleton in 1982 she is now a member of the royal family. She married Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, second in line to the throne, in 2011. They have three children together, Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis. She devotes her time to supporting a number of charitable causes and organisations, several of which are centred around providing children with the best possible start in life. She has set up a foundation to research young children, addiction, mental health and art. She also has a great interest in photography and has taken many official portraits of her children and other members of the royal family.
