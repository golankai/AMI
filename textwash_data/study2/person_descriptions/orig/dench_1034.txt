Judi Dench was made a Dame in 1970 for her contributions to acting and her charity efforts, and has since earned titles of similar merit for the same. She is known as one of the best british actors and has appeared in notable films such as James Bond (7 films) and Shakespeare in Love, where she won an Academy Award for best supporting actress. With an acting career spanning 65 years, she has become a standard for many aspiring actresses to live up to, particularly given the wide variety of roles and genres she has tackled both on and off-screen.
