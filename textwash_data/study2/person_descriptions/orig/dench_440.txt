She is Dame Judi Dench, but was born Judith Olivia Dench in York, England. She has been long time acting. Her first role was in TV series Hilda Lessways where she played the main character, that was in 1959. She is possibly most famous for playing in James Bond movies as M, James's boss. She is of British nationality. She has played in lots of Shakespeare dramas, received many awards. She is over 80 years old, with characteristic short haircut. She did portray Queen Elizabeth II in ""Shakespeare in Love"".
