Adele Adkins is an award winning English singer and songwriter of international fame. She is one of the worlds best-selling music artists and she famously began her career after graduating from BRIT school, like numerous other British music artists.
Her studio albums have a naming convention of being titled the age year she releases them i.e. 19, 21 and 25. 
She released the single ""Skyfall"" for the release of the popular and well received James Bond film, Skyfall. 
She has been covered a lot in the tabloid press over the years with news of splitting from her long term husband, the birth of her first child, her new prospective partners, and her weight loss journey.
