Sam Smith is an english singer and songwriter they released their first album in 2014 through capital records the album won 4 awards at the Grammys. 
As of 2021 they have sold over 30 million albums worldwide.
They were born in London and raised in Cambridgeshire. Sam Smith was bullied in school for ""having breasts"" which led to them having liposuction at 12.
They came out as gay in 2014 and then as genderqueer in 2017 and prefer to use they/them pronouns. 
Sam Smith identifies as a feminist and will dress as and how they feel the mood for in that day.
They are the third cousin of singer Lily Allen and actor Alfie Allen.
