Sam Smith is a famous musician from London, England. They have been a prominent figure in the international music scene for may years (breaking through in 2012), with hits such as; ""Latch"" (with Disclosure), ""La La La"" (with Naughty Boy), and ""Promises"" (with Calvin Harris).

Smith identifies as genderqueer and uses the they/them pronouns.

Smith has also won many awards for their music. Their achievements include 4 Grammy awards, 3 Brit awards and even an Academy award (for ""writing's on the wall"" from the film ""spectre""). These are just to name a few.
