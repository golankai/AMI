Elton John is an English singer, pianist, composer and songwriter, famous for rock music. His real name is Reginald Kenneth Dwight. There is also a film based on his life Elton John: Me, Myself & I, an autobiography. Elton John is one of the best selling authors of all time. He has received numerous awards for his work. Some of his famous songs included I'm Still Standing, Don't Go Breaking My Heart, Candle in the Wind, Sorry Seems to Be the Hardest Word, Tiny Dancer, Rocket Man, That's What Friends Are For. John Elton didn't have a good relationship with his family since they didn't support his music career. Today he has two sons.
