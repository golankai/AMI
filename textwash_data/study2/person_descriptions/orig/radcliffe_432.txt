Daniel Jacob Radcliffe English actor was born on the 23rd of July 1989, famously known as his character in JK Rowlings Harry Potter which he completed at the young age of 10. He was raised in London by his parents. He has stared In many successful films, acclaimed lots of awards, and even completed stage work.  He has contributed to many charities, including Demelza Hospice Care for Children and the Trevor Project; the latter awarded him its Hero Award in 2011. He owns a property in New York and goes back and forth from the USA to the UK.  He has been in a relationship with actress Erin Darke, whom he met on the set of Kill Your Darlings, since 2012.
