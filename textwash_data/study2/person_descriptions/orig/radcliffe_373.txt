Born in 1989, Daniel Jacob Radcliffe is an English actor best known as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter film series. He was born and raised in London and during his Harry Potter career he was one of the world's highest paid actors. Since then, he has played mainly unique roles, such as a lawyer in The woman in black, Igor in Victor Frankenstein to a sentient corpse named Manny.
Daniel has been part of many charitable organisations, such as designing Cu-Bed for habitats VIP range and all the royalties going to Demelza House CHildren's Hospice. He is a Labour supporter. He has also spoke out against J.K Rowlings Transphobic comments.
