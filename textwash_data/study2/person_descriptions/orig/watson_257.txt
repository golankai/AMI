Emma Charlotte Watson an English actress and activist was born on the 15th April 1990. She was born in Paris to English lawyers Chris and Jacqueline.  She lived in Paris until she was 5, then moving to England with her mother when her parents divorced. Started performing when she was very young in stage school and school shows.  Her first professional acting was Harry Potter, after 8 casting auditions she was cast as Hemione Granger in all 7 movies.  Has modelled and had a fashion career. Also an outspoken feminist and promoted education for girls. She divides her time between London and New York City.
