Sir Elton Hercules John, known as Elton John, original name Reginald Kenneth Dwight, was born March 25 1947 in Middlesex England. He is a British singer, composer, and pianist who was one of the most popular entertainers of the late 20th century. He has had a recording career that included the sale of hundreds of millions of records. In his youth he was a piano prodigy and earned a scholarship to the Royal Academy of Music at the age of just 11. He eventually gravitated towards pop after discovering rhythm and blues, and his writing and playing style greatly reflects this.
