Cara Delevigne is known for being a model. But she is also an actress and a singer.
She was born in 1992 in Hammersmith, London. 
She signed with storm management when she left school and has been lucky enough to win model of the year at The British Fashion Awards in 2012 and 2014.
She has been in the following films: Anna Karenina, Suicide Squad, Valerian and The City of a thousand planets. 
She had her first modelling job at 10 for vogue Italia. She was scouted by Burberry in 2012. Her first time on the catwalk was in 2011 at London Fashion week. Her big break was New York fashion week where she walked in 9 shows.
