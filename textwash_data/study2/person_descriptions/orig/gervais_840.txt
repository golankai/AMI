Ricky Gervais is an English stand up comedian, actor, director, producer and writer. He was born in 1961 in Reading and has 2 brothers and a sister. He has collaborated many times with Stephen Merchant, most namely for the mock documentary The Office and follow up, Extras. He has won seven BAFTA Awards, five British Comedy Awards, two Emmy Awards, three Golden Globe Awards, and the Rose d'Or twice (2006 and 2019), as well as a Screen Actors Guild Award nomination.
