Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor that is well known for many roles, such as playing Smaug the dragon in the Hobbit Trilogy by Peter Jackson, playing Sherlock Holmes in one of the many TV shows that the character is the star of, and his biggest commercial success is probably the role of Dr Steven Strange in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He is a very talented actor although a bit too typecast as the straight man. He doesn't have a very conventional physique because some would call his face quite unattractive yet he has a rich presence on camera which explains his success and good acting abilities.
