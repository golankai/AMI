Elton John is a singer/ songwriter he plays the piano and composes music, he is extremely talented. He has sold over 300 million records a huge achievement. Elton John was very close to the Princess of Wales Diana and when she died wrote the beautiful Candle in the wind dedicated to her life and their friendship. This song sold over 33 million copies worldwide.
In his Early life Elton had always had a love for music and he had started playing his grandmothers piano from a young age. By the age of 7 he had been playing the piano to family at gatherings and parties. He attended Saturday stage school classes in London. After same sex marriage became legal in 2014 Elton married his now husband and they have two children together.
