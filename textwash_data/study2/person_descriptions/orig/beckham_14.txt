David Beckham is a successful English football player. Beckham married Spice girl Victoria Beckham in 1999 and since then they have had four children: Brooklyn, Romeo James, Cruz and Harper seven - seven after the number he played as in matches.
During his 20 year long career, David Beckham won 19 major trophies. 
He began playing football for Manchester United where he won the Premier League six times and the FA cup twice. He then played for Real Madrid winning the major soccer league in his final season. 
Beckham was one of the most recognised midfielders of his time and awarded an OBE in 2003.
