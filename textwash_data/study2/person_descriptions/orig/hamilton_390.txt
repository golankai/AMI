Lewis Hamilton is a 35 year old British racing driver in Formula one. Yesterday he won the Russian Grand Prix. He is the current world champion having won the 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020 championships. He currently drives for Mercedes but before this he used to drive for Mclaren where he was for over five years. 

Hamilton is a vegan and raises the profile of veganism - in 2018 he was crowned PETA Person of the Year.
