Sir Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver perhaps most famous for his Formula One career. He finished on the podium in his debut race, going on to set numerous records. He’s widely credited with extending the audience of formula one and his highly competitive driving style and consistency makes him a viewer and fan favourite. He’s the only black driver to race in the series, and is an active advocate against racism - working for increased diversity in motorsport. He’s also spoken out against human rights abuses, and on environmental issues.
