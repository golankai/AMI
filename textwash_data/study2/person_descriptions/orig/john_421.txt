Elton John is a well known singer, composer and songwriter. He is a best-seller with many singles being in the top 40. His genre typically falls under rock. He is a Grammy award winner. He is an openly gay man and has done many things for AIDS charities. He did have a problem with substance abuse and bulimia but has now been sober for many years. He composed the music for the musical billy Elliot. 
Elton John has also had a movie made about him called rocket man. He has worked with many other artists including Eminem, Alice in chains and princess Diana.
