Benedict cumberbatch is another very well known English actor. He has been widely recognised for his work and has won various awards and has also been recognised her her majesty the queen and was awarded a CBE for his work within the performing arts and charity. He has worked on both movies and tv shows and is widely recognised for his role in Frankenstein and in Sherlock Holmes. He also has 3 children.
