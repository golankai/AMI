Successful prolific British female author, writer of the world famous Harry Potter series of books. Also writes the Strike detective novels under the pseudonym Robert Galbraith. Extremely wealthy, one of the highest paid authors in the world, her books have been translated into 76 languages. She used the initials J K to disguise the fact that she was female when submitting the Harry Potter books to publishers, as she believed this would increase her chances of being accepted for publication. Harry Potter was rejected 12 times before being accepted for publication.
