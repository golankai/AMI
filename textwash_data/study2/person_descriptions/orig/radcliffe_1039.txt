Daniel Radcliffe is best known for finding fame in the starring role of Harry Potter, but since then has gone on to partake in a number of other highly acclaimed movies, including The Woman In Black. He is 32 years of age, but started his career at just 10 years old in an adaptation of Charles Dickens' 'David Copperfield'. In 2007, he started to partake in stage acting as well as screen. In his personal life, he divides his time between New York and London, and is known to enjoy rap music. He does not have a spouse. He is reported to be a member of the Labour Party, and is known to be charitable in his actions.
