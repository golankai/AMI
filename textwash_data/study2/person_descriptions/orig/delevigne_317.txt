Cara Delevingne is an actress and model with a unique, eclectic, and at times eccentric sense of style. She has walked in major runway shows for top fashion brands. She is known for her striking physical features, namely her bold, dark eyebrows, as well her changing hairstyles (she has sported everything from a shaved head for a film role to long blonde hair). She has a sister, who is also in the fashion industry. She resides in Los Angeles, California in a large estate with a fully customised interior. She plays multiple musical instruments, but specialises in the drums.
