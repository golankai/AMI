 British actor Benedict Cumberbatch was born in 1975 in London. He is an accomplished actor starring in both stage, film and TV. He went to school at Harrow before moving on to the academy of music and dramatic art. 
Some of his most notable roles are as Sherlock Holmes in Sherlock which he starred alongside Martin Freeman and he played Alan Turing in The imitation game.
In 2016 he joined the MCU as Dr Strange where plays a surgeon who after a crash is unable to operate. 
He has a big female following who call themselves the cumberbitches.
In 2015 he received a CBE for services to performing arts and to charity
