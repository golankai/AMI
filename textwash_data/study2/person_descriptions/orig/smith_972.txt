Sam Smith is a British singer, songwriter. He was born in London, He is currently 29 years of age. Sam Smith featured on Naughty boy's 'La La La', Which become a number one single in may 2013. In the December of the same year, they were nominated for the 2014 Brit Critics Choice Awards and the BBC's Sound of 2014. In which they won both. 
He came out to the public as gay in 2014. In 2017, Smith came out as Genderqueer, stating, 'I feel just as much as a women as I am man'. 
In 2019, Smith then came out Non-binary and changed their gender pronouns to they/them.
Sam Smith is also third cousins to the famous , singer/songwriter Lily Allen.
