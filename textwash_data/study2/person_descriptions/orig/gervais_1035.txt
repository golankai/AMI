Ricky Gervais is a British comedian, actor and show-writer, known for his work on programs such as The Office, The Ricky Gervais Show, An Idiot Abroad and Extras. He has also worked on a number of projects inclusing podcasts with his friends Stephen merchant and Karl Pilkington. In his stand-up comedy he has tackled controversial topics such as religion and sex-abuse scandals within hollywood. He has won many awards celebrating his writing, directing, comedy and influence in the entertainment industry. Ricky Gervais has turned his hand to a number of additional projects including book-writing, music, fund-raising efforts and Film production. He is fond of animals and has pledged to leave his fortune to animal charities.
