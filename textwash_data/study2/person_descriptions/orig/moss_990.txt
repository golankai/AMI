Kate Moss is an iconic supermodel who has been the face of British fashion across the past two decades.
She is of slim build, typical of a supermodel. She also has a very chiseled face, again very typical of a supermodels appearance.
Kate Moss has blonde hair and is roughly 5 foot 7 inches tall. Unlike her psychical features, her height is not a feature that most people would associate with super models. Most supermodels would typically be 5 foot 9 and above.
Kate Moss has featured in countless campaigns with many well known designers and fashion houses. Examples would include Chanel, Louis Vuitton and Versace.
