Daniel Radcliffe is a 32 year-old English actor, who became a household name through his childhood role as the titular character in the Harry Potter film series. Radcliffe starred in all seven Harry Potter films, to both mass popularity and critical acclaim. Since Harry Potter, Radcliffe has acted in a range of films, television series and theatre productions. These include: Swiss Army Man, the Woman in Black, and Kill Your Darlings. Radcliffe has won many awards for his performances on both stage and screen.
