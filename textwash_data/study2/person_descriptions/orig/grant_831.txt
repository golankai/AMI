Hugh Grant is a long time English actor who has been widely praised for his performances in many of his films over the last few decades. Known mostly for his romantic comdey roles he surprisingly often mentions that acting was never his dream career but one that just happened by chance instead. He has won multiple awards over the years such as the BAFTA and Golden Globe awards for roles such as Four Weddings And A Funeral and Notting Hill. Hugh Grant has also been very open about how he doesn't like the spotlight on him that inevitably comes with a person of his fame, however he has since come around in recent years to say that he actually enjoys acting at least for the time being.
