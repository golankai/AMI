Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch CBE, best known as Benedict Cumberbatch,  is an English actor from London. He is 45 years old.

Cumberbatch is well known for his various roles on television as well as his roles on film. He is likely best known for playing ""Sherlock Holmes"" in the BBC show ""Sherlock"". This role won Cumberbatch a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Miniseries. Cumberbatch is also well known as playing ""Dr Strange"" in multiple movies within the Marvel Cinematic universe.
