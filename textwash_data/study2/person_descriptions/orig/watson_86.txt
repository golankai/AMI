Emma Watson, 31, is a British actress and activist who first came to fame playing Hermione Grainger in the Harry Potter film series when she was a child. She appeared in all eight Harry Potter films before going on to star in other films including Beauty and The Beast. Aside from her acting career, Watson is a feminist, using her fame and privilege to advocate for women's rights across the world. She works with the UN as a goodwill ambassador and is often featured in lists of influential people.
