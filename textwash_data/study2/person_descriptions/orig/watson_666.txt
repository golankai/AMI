Emma Watson is a British 31 year old famous actress, born in Paris, France but raised in the United Kingdom. 

Emma is well known for playing Hermoine Granger in Harry Potter sequel; she is an influential celebrity, and has been recognised for this by the 'Time' Magazine in 2015. Emma is Passionate about women's rights, gaining another award in Gender Equality. 

Emma Watson is also famously known for playing Belle in Disney's Beauty and the Beast which was re-released in 2017. 

Amongst Acting and being an activist, Emma is a model and has modelled for top brands such as Burberry and Lancome.
