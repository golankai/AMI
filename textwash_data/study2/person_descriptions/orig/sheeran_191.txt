A popular singer-songwriter, known for his guitar-playing, ginger hair, broad appeal and popular songs. His albums are named after mathematical symbols (e.g., +, -, x). He was born in Yorkshire, England, is 30 years old, and is married with one child. He loves Suffolk and has a massive house there. He is also a big fan of charity. He's had cameo appearances in some TV shows and films, including Home and Away, The Bastard Executioner, Game of Thrones, The Simpsons, Yesterday, Modern Love, and Star Wars.
