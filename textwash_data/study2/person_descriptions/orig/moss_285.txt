Kate Moss is a British supermodel who rose to fame in the 1990's. She has since has her own clothing range as well as being involved in music projects. 

Kate Moss was often in the limelight for her party lifestyle at the height of her career and due to drug allegations was dropped from several fashion campaigns in 2005. In 2012 she was the second highest earning top model, earning $9.2 million in one year. 

Kate Moss has a daughter called Lily who has already made her modelling debut and was seen recently on the runway of London Fashion Week. 

Kate Moss ranks at the 99th richest woman in the United Kingdom.
