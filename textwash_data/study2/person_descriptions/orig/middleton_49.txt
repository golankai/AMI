Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, is the wife of Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and is a member of the British royal family. Born on 9 January 1982, she attended the University of St Andrews where she would first meet her future husband, Prince William. They married at Westminster Abbey on 29 April 2011, in a live television broadcast. She is involved in charity work, notably for mental health. She has had 3 children with Prince William; Prince Greorge, Princess Charlotte, and Prine Louis. She has a younger brother and sister, James Middleton and Pippa Middleton respectively. Her parents, Carole and Michael Middleton, are business entrepeneurs.
