Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver who currently competes in formula one races for Mercedes. He is 36 years old and has 3 siblings. Lewis Hamilton has won seven world drivers championship titles and is a well known racer. He has been an active racing driver since 2007 when he first entered in the 2007 Australian grand prix  and later went on to win the 2007 Canadian grand prix. His latest racing entry was into the 2021 Russian grand prix. Lewis Hamilton was also listed as one of the 100 most influential people in a 2020 issue of the Times
