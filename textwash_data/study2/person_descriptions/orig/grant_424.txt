Hugh grant is a British actor. He has performed in many films including Bridget Jones, love actually, Notting hill and about a boy. He is a highly intelligent man who studied at the university of Oxford. He has won a golden globe and a BAFTA. He typically plays the same charming character in romantic comedies. He has many children and has been said to be involved in affairs. He was involved in the phone hacking scandal and had his phone tapped by newspapers owned by Rupert Murdock. He has been arrested on a number of occasions
