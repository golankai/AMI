Born in 1990, Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson is an English actress, who first gained recognition in the Harry Potter franchise. Since then she has been heavily involved in blockbusters and independent films, whilst also working on women's rights. 

She has been ranked as one of the highest-paid actresses in the world by Forbes and Vanity fair, and named one of the most influential people in the world by time magazine. 

She was originally born in Paris and then moved and raised in Oxfordshire. Where she was enrolled in Private school and trained at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach and Theatre arts. 
She is well known by 90's generation as Hermione Granger, the bookworm Gryffindor from Harry Potter.
