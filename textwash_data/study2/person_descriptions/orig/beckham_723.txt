David Beckham is a 46 year old, white, male, English footballer who is known in England as Golden Balls. He played for Manchester United between 1992 and 2003 where he won six Premier League titles, two FA Cups and a Champions League trophy. He moved to Real Madrid where they won La Liga before eventually moving to Los Angeles with his family to play for LA Galaxy. Once retired from football, Beckham continued to be involved in the sport and co owns two teams in Miami and Salford. He was awarded an OBE by the Queen and was heavily involved in the successful London 2012 Olympic bid. #he undertakes a lot of charity work and is an ambassador for UNICEF.
