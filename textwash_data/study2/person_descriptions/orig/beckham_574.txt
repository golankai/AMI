David Beckham is a English football player who is married to Victoria Beckham, he has 3 children 2 boys and 1 girl. He use to play for Manchester United football club and also for England first team. He has played for Ac Milan and also Real Madrid, he was born in 1975 and is currently 42 years old, in 2013 he was the highest paid football player. He is well known for his fashion choices and his many hair styles. He was born at Whipps Cross University Hospital in Leytonstone, London, England. He attended Chingford County High School in Ridgeway Park, Chingford. On 4 July 1999, he got married at Luttrellstown Castle in Ireland
