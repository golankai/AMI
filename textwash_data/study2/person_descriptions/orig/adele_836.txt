Adele is an English singer songwriter born in London, 1988. Adele supports football club Tottenham Hotspur. In 2017, Adele was ranked the richest musician under age 30 in the UK and Ireland on the Sunday Times Rich List, which valued her wealth at £125 million. She was ranked the 19th richest musician overall. On the 2019 list, she was valued at £150 million as the 22nd richest musician in the UK. 
Adele has one son who was born in 2012 with ex husband Simon Konecki. She is a supporter of the Labour Party. She also became an ordained minister to officiate at her friends wedding.
