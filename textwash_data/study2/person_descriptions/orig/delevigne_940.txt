Cara Delevingne is a British born model, actress and singer.  She is 29 years old and was born in Hammersmith, London on 12th August 1992.  Delevingne's modelling breakthrough occurred when she appeared in Burberry's spring/summer 2011 campaign. From then on in 2012 to 12014 she appeared in New York, London, Milan and Paris Fashion weeks for major fashion houses including Donna Karan, Oscar de la Renta, Fendi, Dolce & Gabbana.  Since 2012 Delevingne has taken on some acting roles including a part as Princess Sorokina in the film Anna Karenina.
