Kate Moss is a British Supermodel who was popular in 1990s. She was born in Croydon in England on the 16th of January 1974. She has a younger brother and half-sister. She was scouted at JFK Airport in New York on the way back from her holiday in the Bahamas in 1988 when she was only fourteen years old. She modelled numerous high end designer brands such as “Chanel”, “Louis Vuitton” and  “GUCCI”. She has one daughter, Lila Grace Moss-Hack who is also a model. There was a drug allegations in 2005 which she didn’t face charges later and she carried on her modelling career. She supports numerous charities such as “War Child” and “Elton John AIDS Foundation”.
