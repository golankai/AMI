Rocky Gervais is an English comedian, writer and actor. Brought up in the midlands, Ricky Gervais has always had a creative streak. Once in a musical group, he moves onto office work which inspired his character David Brent in the show the office.

The Office went on to become a huge success, so much so that there was an American version made staring Steve Carrell. The show was also made into a variety of other languages. He is also known for his stand up comedy, his stance against religion and animal cruelty.
