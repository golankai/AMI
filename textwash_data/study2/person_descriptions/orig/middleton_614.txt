She is the Duchess of Cambridge born in 1982 in Bucklebury Berkshire. She is married to Prince William the Duke of Cambridge. She and the Duke of Cambridge are currently second in the line of succession to the British Throne making Catherine a likely future Queen consort. They currently have 3 children Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis. She was educated at St Andrews School And Marlborough College, she then studied Art History at the University of St Andrews in Scotland. During her second year at St Andrews she shared a flat with Prince William and two other friends from 2003 - 2005.
