Kate moss is a British supermodel born Katherine Ann moss, she rose to fame in the early 90s  working with Calvin clien she rocketed to stardom and fashion icon status. Born in 1974 she began her own fashion businesses and has been involved in a number of musical productions after the success of her modeling career. Kate got into a bit of media trouble due to her party lifestyle and drug use this subsequently led to her being dropped from several fashion outlets in 2005. Seven years later she was cleared of charges and was able to resume her modelling career. She has a 1.5million pound statue sculpted of her which ended up in a British museum.
