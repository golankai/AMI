David Beckham, born on 2nd May, 1975 in Whipps Cross University Hospital in Leytonstone, London.  Beckham is a former English football player. His football career started in 1992, at the age of 17, with Manchester United. He retired in the year, 2013 after a 20 year career, in which he won 19 trophies. Over those years, he played for different for different teams, Beckham is known for him one of the greatest recognisable midfielders of his generation. Beckham is ranked high in the football industry and is known to be one of the highest paid player at one time, especially in 2013. He married Victoria Beckham in 1999 and they have four children.
