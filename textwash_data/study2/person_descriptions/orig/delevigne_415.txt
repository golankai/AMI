Cara Jocelyn Delevingne was born on 12 August 1992, in Hammersmith, London, to Pandora Anne Delevigne  and property developer Charles Hamar Delevingne. She spent her early days in Belgravia, London. She has two older sisters, Chloe and Poppy Delevingne, and a paternal half-brother, Alex Jaffe. Delevingne attended Francis Holland School for Girls in central London until she was 16 before moving to Bedales School in Steep, Hampshire. She has dyspraxia and found school challenging. She began modelling at the age of ten. Notable companies shes has walked for includes: Fendi, Trussardi, Moschino, Blumarine, DSquared2 and Dolce & Gabbana. She became selective. She also went into acting. She acted in movies such as  the face of an angel, Playhouse presents etc.
Delevingne sings, plays the drums and the guitar. She has also designed two fashion collections for DKNY and Mulberry. She openly identifies as a bisexual and pansexual
