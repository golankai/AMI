Born as Samuel Smith in 1992 is an English singer and songwriter. He was born in London, England. He started in 2007 and is active until now. His debut studio album ""In The Lonely Hour"" was released in 2014. The third single from that album, ""Stay with me"", was an international success. It became number one in UK and number two in US. He wrote song ""Writing's on the Wall"" which was a theme song for James Bond movie ""Spectre"" which was released in 2015.
In 2014 Smith came out as gay to the public.
