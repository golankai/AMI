Dame Judi Dench was born 9th December 1934 in York. Judi was wife to husband Michael who sadly died in 2001. She has 1 child Flinty born in 1972 and one grandchild Samuel born in 1997. Her grandson is commonly known for his TikTok videos including his grandmother. Judi has performed in a number of Shakespeare plays. She has won a bafta for most promising newcomer. Judi claimed international fame when she played M in the James Bond films. In 2011 Judi was awarded a Fellowship from the British Film Institute
