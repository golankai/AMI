Judi Dench, aged 86, is an English actress. She is known to be one of Britain’s finest actresses as a result of her effortless acting. Dench started acting in theatre performances. She has starred in many recreations of Shakespeare’s most famous plays, including roles such as Ophelia in Hamlet, Juliet in Romeo and Juliet and Lady Macbeth in Macbeth. Dench then ventured in the film industry as was recognised with a BAFTA Award for the Most Promising Newcomer. She has then been continuously awarded for her performances with her outstanding portrayals of character roles.
