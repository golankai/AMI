Benedict Cumberbatch is an extremely famous English Actor known for his main pets in many films. He gained worldwide recognition for his part playing Sherlock Holmes in the popular bbc tv drama. Benedict Cumberbatch attended boarding schools from the age of 8 and his acting abilities were quickly noticed. He drama teacher described him as ‘the best young actor’ he had ever seen. He is married to famous theatre and opera director Sophie Hunter. They have 3 children. Christopher, Hal and Finn ages 6, 4, and 2 years old.
