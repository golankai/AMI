Emma Watson full name Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson is an English actress most famously known for her role as Hermione granger in harry potter. her birthday is 15th April. Emma Watson was born in Paris but raised in Oxfordshire. she is an outspoken feminist she has travelled to Bangladesh and Zambia to promote education for girls. she graduated from brown university with a bachelors degree in English literature. Emma Watson has been ranked one of the highest paid actresses and named one of the most influential people in the world.
