David beckham full name David Robert Joseph beckham is a retired footballer born 2nd may 1975 he played for Manchester United, England and many football clubs around the world. he wore number 7 shirt or number 23. his nickname is golden ball. he married a spice girl posh spice Victoria beckham in 1999. him and his wife have 4 children Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruze, harper-seven. he owns a football club in the USA called inter Miami with his former team mate Phil Neville  being the manager of inter Miami. in 2001 he scored a free kick against Greece which qualified England into the World Cup 2002. he was known as one of the greatest and most recognisable midfielders of his generation
