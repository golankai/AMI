Also known As Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, Kate was born on the 9th of January 1982 (aged 39). She met her husband whilst studying at St Andrews University in Scotland. She married Prince William in 2011 and proceeded to have three children with him; George (aged 8), Charlotte (aged 6) and Louis (aged 3). She is known for being involved with multiple charities such as SportsAid, Action for Children etc. Kate is also known for his fashion style and is often seen on multiple best dressed lists, being praised for using her clothes more than once and varying between high street and high end brands.
