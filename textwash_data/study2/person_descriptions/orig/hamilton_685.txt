Lewis Hamilton is a successful grand Prix driver. He has driven for mercedes and McLaren. Lewis holds the record for the most wins, pole positions and podium finishes.
He is the first black driver to drive in F1. Lewis did not come from a wealthy family and his dad worked several jobs to enable him to peruse his career. 
Lewis does not drink and is a vegan. Hamilton was once in an on-and-off relationship with Nicole Scherzinger, the lead singer of the American girl group Pussycat Dolls. He lives in Switzerland to avoid paying taxes!
