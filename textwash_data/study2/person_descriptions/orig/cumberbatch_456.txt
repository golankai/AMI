Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor who has starred in many high profile films and TV shows, and has also acted on stage. He is probably best known for his portrayal as Sherlock Holmes in the TV series Sherlock, and his role as Doctor Strange in the Marvel Movie franchise. He has won many awards such as Emmy's and BAFTA's, and Time magazine have named him previously as one of the worlds most influential people. He also does a lot of work to raise money for charity. Cumberbatch is married and has 3 children. He is coined throughout the world as a handsome gentleman, as he is tall, with dark curly hair and blue eyes.
