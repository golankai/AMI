Daniel Radcliffe, born 23rd July 1989, born in London, is a British actor widely known for his role as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter movie franchise. He made his acting debut at 10 years old in the tv movie ""David Copperfield"" and just 2 years later began the ten year career of playing Harry in all 7 films. Becoming one of the worlds most famous and highly paid actors.
Since the filming of Harry Potter ended, Daniel has had success on both stage and screen, stepping away from the wizarding world in roles such as Arthur Kipps in the Horror film ""The woman in black"" and starring in ""Equus"" on both West End and Broadway.
He is currently is a relationship with actress Erin Darke, the couple met on the set of the film ""Kill your darlings"" in 2012.
