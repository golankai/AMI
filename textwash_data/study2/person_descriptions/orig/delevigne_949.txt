Born in August 1992, Cara Delevingne is an English model. Her first role as a model was at the age of ten in 2002, when she was photographed for Vogue Italia. She has appeared and walked the catwalk at London, New York, Milan and Paris Fashion Weeks. From 2015, she has paused her modelling career somewhat, instead moving to a focus on films. Her first role as an actress was in 2012, when she starred in Anna Karenina, alongside Keira Knightley. Since then, she has also appeared in music videos, including for Bryan Ferry and Rita Ora.
