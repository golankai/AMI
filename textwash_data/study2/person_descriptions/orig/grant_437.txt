Hugh John Mungo Grant born 1960 is an English actor. He was born in London, England. He started acting in 1982 and is active until now. He is known for his roles in ""Four Weddings and a Funeral"" and "" Notting Hill"". The first movie was a breakthrough for him. For that role he won a Golden Globe Award, as well as a BAFTA Film Award for Best Actor. He usually plays warm and sincere characters. He played Prime Minister in ""Love Actually"" alongside many famous actors like Alan Rickman, Liam Neeson and Emma Thompson.
