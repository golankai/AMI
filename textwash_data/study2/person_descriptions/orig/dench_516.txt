Dame Judi Dench is a world famous British actress known for starring in the James Bond movies as well as many other high profile movies in Hollywood. 

Dench also has a long history in theatre performing in many famous Shakespeare plays such as Hamlet and Macbeth.

Judi Dench is decorated in many awards from her film, TV and theatrical career managing to rack up a collection of BAFTAs Golden Globes and Screen Actors guild awards. 

Married in 1971 to her late husband Michael Williams who passed away in 2001 she has 1 child (Finty Williams) and 1 Grandson. Her grandson has also manged to gather an online audience through the popular social media app TikTok where Dame Judi will often feature in his video clips.
