Also most famously known for her role on Harry Potter, as Hermione Granger, Emma Watson is an English actress/activist. Born on the 15th of April 1990 (aged 31), Emma Watson is one of the highest paid actresses in the world. After the success of Harry Potter, Emma branched out into movies and lending her voice such as Belle in the live action Beauty and the Beast remake. Emma Watson is known for her work for women's rights. She became a UN Women Goodwill ambassador in 2014, in which she helped launch the compaign HeForShe, advocating for gender equality. She has also modelled for campaigns for brands such as Burberry.
