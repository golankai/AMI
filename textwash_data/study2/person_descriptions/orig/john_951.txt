Elton John was born Reginald Kenneth Dwight on 25 March 1947. He was raised in Pinner Greater London, found fame working his way up from a pub pianist to a staff writer for DJM Records. in October of 1973 he released Goodbye Yellow Brick Road his first solo album. He is an avid football fan of Watford football club, he becomes the chairman of his childhood club in 1976 and invested large sums of money into the club under his lead they finished runner up to Liverpool in the league in 1983. He sold the club in 1987. He now has a stand named after him as a show of appreciation by the club.
