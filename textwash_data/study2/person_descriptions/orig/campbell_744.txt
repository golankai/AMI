Naomi Campbell is a famous British supermodel who rose to fame during the late 1980s and early 1990s. She was one of the most in demand models during this period and into the early 2000s. She was the first black woman to be on the front of French Vogue. As well as modelling, Naomi has branched out into other areas including acting, writing and reality TV as well as being heavily involved in activism and charity work. Naomi has been in a number of high profile relationships including with U2 band member Adam Clayton, actor Leonardo DiCaprio and formula 1 driver Flavio Briatore.
