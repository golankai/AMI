Joanne Rowling is the infamous writter of the world famous, best selling book series, Harry Potter,which spawned on a train journey from Manchester to London in 1990. After an exhausting 12 rejctions, Harry Potter and the Philsophers Stone finally got published, its' popularity grew exponentially, leading to the books now becoming a franchise of films and the public wanting more. The unexpected success of the fantasy series of Harry Potter saw Rowling accounced the worlds' first billionaire author. She was born on the 31st July 1965, in Yate, England.
