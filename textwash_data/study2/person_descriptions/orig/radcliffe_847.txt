Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor, most known for his work as the starting role of the Harry Potter movie franchise. It has been 20years since the first Harry Potter movie was released, in which Daniel was 12. In the following ten years, he played the role in seven sequels. During which, Radcliffe became one of the world's highest-paid actors, gained worldwide fame, popularity, and critical acclaim, and received many accolades for his performances in the series. Radcliffe is a strong supporter of the LGBTQ+ community, showing his support more recently in publicly opposing the views made about the trans community by Harry Potter author JK Rowling.
