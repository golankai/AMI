Sir Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton MBE was born in Stevenage in 1985 and is one of 4 siblings.
When he was six his father bought him a go cart and encouraged and supported Lewis from the start of his racing career.  He soon started to win his races and became the youngest racer to win the British cadet karting championship at only 10 years of age.
He began his formula one career at the Australian Grand Prix in 2007 and has just won his 100th race making him the record holder.  He currently races for McLaren but has also raced for Mercedes.
