Daniel Radcliffe is a popular English actor best known for his breakout role as a child actor, playing Harry Potter in the popular adaptation of JK Rowling's book series. 
He has continued having success in his acting career after the end of Harry Potter, featuring in movies like Victor Frankenstein, The Woman in Black, and Imperium.
He has also ventured into stage acting, starring in both West End and Broadway productions over the years.
His birthday is on the 23rd of July, 1989 and he is currently 32 years old
