Adele is a British singer and songwriter. She rose to fame in the early 2000's with her song Chasing Pavements which became a big hit. 

She has released a number of albums, all of which are titled after the age she was when it was released. Her latest album is called 25.

She recored the theme tune to the James Bond film Skyfall, which was a massive chart hit. It also won numerous award at the Brit Awards and Grammys.

She is reportedly working on a fourth album however that has been postponed due to COVID-19.

Adele was married to an entrepreneur with which she had a son however they got divorced in 2021.
