Sir Elton Hercules John, born Reginald Kenneth Dwight on the 25th of March 1947 in Pinner, Middlesex, London. Elton John is a British singer, pianist, actor, songwriter, composer and music producer. In his 40 year career, he won 5 Grammys and one Oscar. Rolling Stone magazine named him one of the biggest 100 singers of all time. He sold over three hundred million copies of his albums. He was married to Renate Blauel from 1984 until 1988. He started dating David Furnish in 2005 and in 2014 the couple got married.
