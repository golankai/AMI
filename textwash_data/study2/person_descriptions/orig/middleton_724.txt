Kate Middleton, now known as Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge is the wife of William, Duke of Cambridge, second in line to the British throne. They met whilst studying at St Andrews University in Scotland in their late teens. She was presented with a wears the famous engagement ring of Princess Diana and the couple married at Westminster Abbey. She is a mother of three now, George, Charlotte and Louis and dedicates her time to royal duties, and being a parent. She is patron of over twenty charities ranging from children, mental health and environment endeavours. Time magazine has named her as one of the most influential people in the world.
