Elton John is a singer, songwriter, composer, and pianist. His works are era-defining. His musical genres span from rock to rock pop, glam rock and soft rock. He is known for his flamboyant outfits, from Union Jack sequinned suits and platform shoes to colourful glasses. In 2019, a biopic of his life titled Rocketman was released, starring Taron Egerton, titled one of the best films to be released in 2019. He has received a long list of awards and honours, he was knighted in 1998 for his charitable work.
