David Beckham was born in London, England and is famous for not only being an international footballer but fashion icon, philanthropist and business owner.

Beckham started his professional career at Manchester United, on loan to Preston North end and had a very successful career in Manchester before his moves to real Madrid, LA Galaxy, AC Milan, Paris St Germain. He became the first footballer to win league titles in 4 countries.


David is married to Victoria and they have 4 children. Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruze and harper.

David is part owner of inter miami who are part of MLS in the USA, he is also part owner of Salford City with the class of 92
