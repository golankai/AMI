Christian Bale is an American actor. He has played a variety of roles and characters throughout his career. Probably most well known for playing the lead in American Psycho and Batman in the Dark Knight trilogy. He had a very husky distinctive voice in Batman - arguably one of the best, if not the best, Batman. He often appears with dark slicked back hair. He is fairly slender and also has thin facial features. Each role he has played has been really different which is a testiment to how good an actor he is.
