Hugh Grant is one of Britain's best known actors, starring in many films including Love Actually, Bridget Jones's Diary, Music and Lyrics, Four Weddings and a Funeral, and many more. 
The 61 year old London born Actor first started his acting career in 1982 where he debuted on his first film 'Privileged', and has since gone on to win a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe Award, and numerous nominations for other prestigious awards. 
Hugh Grant has five children to two women and is currently married to Anna Eberstein.
