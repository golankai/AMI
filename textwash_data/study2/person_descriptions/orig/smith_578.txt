Sam Smith is an English singer and songwriter. Sam is 29 years old and was born and raised in London. Through Sams childhood they played parts in theatre productions and jazz bands. Sam Smith has come out as homosexual and speaks of being 'Genderqueer' rather than male or female. They later defined themselves as non-binary. They are open and honest about how they feel both sexually and gender wise though admits it wasn't always easy for them to be that way. Sams music is mostly pop, rnb and soul, with them as the main strong vocals.
