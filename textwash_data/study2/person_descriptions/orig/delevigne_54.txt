Cara Jocelyn Delevingne born on the 12th of August 1992 in Hammersmith, London is a British model, actress and singer. Cara started her modelling career after dropping out of school in 2009 and signing a modelling contract with Storm Management. She won the Model of the Year award from the British Fashion Awards in 2012 and 2014. Her acting career started in 2012 with a minor role in a movie adaptation of Anna Karenina by Joe Wright. Her most known role is the role of Margo Roth in the movie Paper Towns, based on John Greens book.
