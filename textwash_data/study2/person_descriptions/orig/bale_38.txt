Christian Bale is a British actor. Some of his best known roles are in The Machinist, American Psycho, The Big Short and the Dark Knight trilogy. Though not a method actor, Bale is known for his huge commitment to roles, notably losing a huge amount of weight for the role in the Machinist and for the role in Vice, putting on a large amount of weight. One of the best actors of his generation, his talents have helped him win an Oscar for best supporting actor and nominated 3 further times. He is extremely versatile and is his films have been very high grossing and he has been one of Hollywood's best paid actors.
