Dame Judi Dench, born in December 1934, is a British actress famous for film and television roles spanning her multi-decade career. She has most notably starred as ""M"" in 8 James Bond films from 1995 to 2015. In the 2021 film, No Time To Die, she was replaced by Ralph Fiennes. 

Aside from her acting career, Dench was married to fellow actor, Michael Wiliams, until his death in 2001.

Dench is also well known in the UK for her role in the film The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel, following a group of older British people who decide to move to India in their retirement.
