David Beckham is a former professional footballer who played for Manchester United and England. He played for other teams during his career and ended it playing for Paris Saint Germain. 

David is considered one of the best England players of all time and is well known for his famous free kicks and the iconic number 7 on his jersey. 

He is currently the co-owner of a football club in Florida called Inter Miami CF, however they have struggled to achieve success.

He is married to Spice Girl Victoria Beckham and they have 4 children together. A number of his children have gone on to become famous themselves, becoming models and artists.
