Emma Watson is a 31 year old actress, born in Paris but raised in the UK. She is most famously known for playing Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter franchise. She played this role for 10 years and was it was actually her first professional role of her acting career. Another note-worthy performance from Emma Watson was her role in the 2017 live action Beauty and the Beast, where she played the main character Belle. She is also very present in the fashion world, becoming a model at a young age and working with companies such as Vogue and Chanel. More importantly, she is very involved in activism and is a very outspoken feminist.
