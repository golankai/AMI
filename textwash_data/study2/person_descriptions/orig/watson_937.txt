Emma Watson is an actor who is famous for her role of  Hermione Grainger in the series of Harry Potter films.   Emma Watson is 31 years old and was born in Paris, France on 15th April 1990.  Emma Watson spent her childhood in Oxfordshire and attended the independent school, The Dragon School in Oxford.  Emma was cast in the role of Hermione Grainger eventhough her previous acting experience was limited to school plays.  After the Harry Potter film series ended Emma chose to carry on her education at the Brown University in USA where she studied English Literature.
