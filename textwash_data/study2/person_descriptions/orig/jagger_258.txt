Mick Jagger was born on the 26th July 1943.  He was born and grew up in Dartford. Born into a middle class family, his father Joe a former gymnast, his mother Eva was a hairdresser. Met the rest of the band at school, but lost contact when they all went to different schools. Mick went on to study at the London School of Economics.  Leaving to join the Rolling Stones who have become one of the biggest bands in the world. Married twice firstly to Bianca and then to Jerry Hall. Has 8 children.  Honoured with a knighthood in 2002.
