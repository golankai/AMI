Adele is an English singer with a powerful and emotive voice. She was born on May 5, 1988. She has so far released three albums to glowing reviews. Her songs have been featured in films, and she even wrote and sang the theme tune to James Bond film Skyfall. She has won 15 Grammy awards and 9 Brit awards, such as Best Album and Best New Artist. Her fourth studio album has been delayed due to Covid-19. She has one child with her ex-husband. She lives in LA, and even officiated a friend's wedding in her garden! She received an MBE in 2013.
