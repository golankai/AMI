J.K. Rowling is the pen name of famous author Joanne Rowling. She was born in Gloucestershire in 1965. Her most famous and well known work is the Harry Potter novel series which has sold more than 500 million copies worldwide. Her first Harry Potter novel was published in 2007. She founded a charitable trust in 2000 , named the Volant Charitable trust, this has an annual budget of just over 5 million pounds which is used to target poverty and social inequality, She has received honorary degrees from a number of universities, including St Andrews, Exeter and Harvard,
