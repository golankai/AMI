Sam Smith is a British singer, songwriter who is famous for ballads and slower songs. His voice is in a fairly high register for a man and he is a global icon. He Rose to fame in 2012 after collaborating with Disclosure on their debut single 'Latch' which oeakes at number 11 in the UK singles chart. He's 29 years old and has won many awards for his musical abilities. After his collaboration with Naughty Boy on their single 'La La La' reached number one, his next single 'Money on My Mind' also reached number one. His debut album won 4 awards at the Grammys along with 2 other nominations.
