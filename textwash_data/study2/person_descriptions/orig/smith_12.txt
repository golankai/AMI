Sam Smith is a singer song writer from London who has had a highly successful music career. Smith is highly decorated: they've won four Grammy Awards, three Brit awards, three Billboard music awards, one American music award, a golden globe and an academy award. Smith has also had five number 1 singles and is an all round advocate for acceptance and love. 
Smith recently came out as gender queer changing their pronouns to they/them. This was very important for the LQBTQ+ community to have such a powerful role model and advocate for equality to be a member of that community.
