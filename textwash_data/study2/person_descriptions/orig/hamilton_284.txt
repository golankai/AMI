Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula One racing driver. Previously a driver for McLaren Hamilton currently races for Mercedes. He hold the title alongside Michael Schumacher for the most Championship wins, totalling 7. 

Hamilton is a fierce campaigner against racism and endeavours to increase diversity in Formula One. He is also passionate about climate change and conservation, known to collect plastic waste on beaches. 

He also campaigns for environmental, human and animal rights and uses his social media platform to highlight these issues.
