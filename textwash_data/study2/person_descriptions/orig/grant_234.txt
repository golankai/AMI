Hugh Grant is a 61 year old actor from London, England. Over his career, he has been in several famous movies such as 'Sense and Sensibility', 'Four Weddings and a Funeral', 'Notting Hill', 'About a Boy', 'Bridget Jones' Diary' and 'Love Actually'. He is typically known for portraying charming, romantic leads in his movies. Many people will describe his comedy as satirical, which adds to the wit he brings to his characters. 

In his personal life, he is married to Anna Eberstein and has 5 children.
