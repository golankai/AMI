Daniel Wroughton Craig is an English actor. Craig was born in 1968 in Chester, England but moved to London when he was 16 to start his acting career. Craig is best known for playing James Bond in the critically-acclaimed film series, beginning with Casino Royale, which brought him international fame. 

Craig continued to star as James Bond in Quantum of Solace in 2008, Skyfall in 2012, Spectre in 2015, and No Time to Die in 2021.
His fame has brought him to multiple GQ magazine covers and appearance on talk shows. Craig's current net worth is US$160 million. Craig is also known for his marriage to Love Actually star Rachel Weisz.
