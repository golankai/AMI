Emma Watson is a British actress most famous for her portrayal of Hermione Grainger in the Harry Potter film series. 

Emma started acting at around age 10 or 11 alongside fellow cast mates Daniel Radcliffe and Rupert Grint. 

Emma appeared in all 8 Harry Potter movies and has since acted in several more movies. 

Emma is largely recognisable due to being in one of the biggest film franchises ever. For a large part of her life she has had long dark hair. 

She is now 31 years of age. She was born in Paris and goes by the full name of Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson.
