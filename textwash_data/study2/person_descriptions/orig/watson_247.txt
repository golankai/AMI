Emma Watson stared as Hermione Granger, the bookish, quick-witted friend of Harry Potter, in the eight Harry Potter movies. From there she’s gone on to star in many film roles, including a role as Belle in the live action remake of Disney’s Beauty and the Beast. Alongside her acting career, she’s also worked as a model including work for Teen Vogue, Vanity Fair and Burberry. Emma has been actively involved in charitable work promoting education for girls throughout the world. She spoke at the UN headquarters to urge men to advocate for gender equality and was made a UN Women Goodwill Ambasador.
