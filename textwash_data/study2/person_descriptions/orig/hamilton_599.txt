Sir Lewis Hamilton was born on the 7th Jan 1985 in Stevenage and has 1 brother. Lewis is a British racing car driver. He has competed for Maclaren but current races for Mercedes. Lewis is currently the only black driver of the season. Lewis has won a joint world championship alongside Michael Schumacher and holds the record for the most wins. He has won 100 races, 7 championships, 101 pole Positions and 176 podium finishes. His first win being in 2007. Lewis has dated a singer, Nicole Scherzinger
