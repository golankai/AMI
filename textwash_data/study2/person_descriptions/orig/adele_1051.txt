Adele is a British singer who is 33 years old. She is a singer and a song writer and a solo artist. She is world known and is extremely famous for her hit songs such as ""Rolling in the deep"", ""Hello"" ""Someone like you"" and ""Set fire to the rain"". She is an above average height of 175cm. She has sales of over 120 million. She is well known for her extraordinary vocal range and abilities. She is also blonde and looks very mature and grown in her features. She used to be on the heavier side in terms of weight but is now slimmer.
