Sam Smith is a well- known singer and song-writer who started his career by auditioning in the show The X factor. He was born in 1992 and is 29 years old. His third single is what brought him international success. ''Stay with me'' being number 1 in the UK and number 2 on the US billboard charts. One of his songs was also used as a James Bond theme song for the film Spectre. ''Writings on the wall'' became one of the most popular songs and one a golden globe award as well as an academy award for the best original song.
