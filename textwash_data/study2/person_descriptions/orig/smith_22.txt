Sam Smith is an English singer-songwriter best known for his hits which include 'Like I Can', 'Too Good At Goodbyes' and 'Stay With Me'. He rose to fame after his breakthrough with Disclosures song 'Latch' which he featured on. 'Writing's on the Wall', the theme song to Spectre, the 24th James Bond film became the first theme tune to reach number one in the UK. Sam came out to the public as gay, and acknowledged a relationship with actor and model Jonathan Zeizel. Sam has won numerous awards over the years including four Grammy and three Brit Awards.
