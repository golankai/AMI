Katherine Ann Moss is a supermodel and businesswoman from Britain who rose to fame in the 1990's. She was born in Croydon in 1974 making her 47 years of age. She is also 5ft 5 which is smaller than the average supermodel. She is also very well known for her figure, and role in size zero fashion. Moss has models for a variety of major fashion brands, one being Calvin Klein.

Kate Moss has also collabed with the huge cosmetic brand Rimmel to create a range of lipsticks. 

Some of Kates most successful parts of her career include an award received at the 2013 British Fashion Awards which acknowledged her contribution to fashion over 25 years. Along with Kate being a contributing fashion editor for British Vogue.
