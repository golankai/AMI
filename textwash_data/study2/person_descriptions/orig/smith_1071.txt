Sam Smith is a British singer and songwriter born in 1992. He rose to fame in 2012 after collaborations with disclosure and achieved his first number one single, with la la la and naughty boy. His first album won 4 awards and gained him alot of success and rocketed him into the mainstream media. As of 2021 he has sold over 30 million albums. Sam Smith has caused alot of controversial media attention when he announced he wanted to be known by they/them nouns,  he grew up in Cambridgeshire I a small village.
