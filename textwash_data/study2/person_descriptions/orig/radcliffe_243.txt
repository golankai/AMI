Daniel Radcliffe is an English actor best known for his role as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter films. He is 32 and from London. As well as films he has also starred on stage plays and musicals. 
He lives between New York and London. 
He has no children but a long term girlfriend. 
He started acting when he was 5 years old and made his acting debut when he was 10 in a BBC programme called David Copperfield.
Other films he has been in include The Woman in Black, Victor Frankenstein, The Gamechangers, Now you see me and The Lost city of D.
His religious background is Jewish but he has also stated that he is An atheist.
