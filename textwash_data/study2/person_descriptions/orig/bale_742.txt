Christian Bale is an English actor, who was born in Wales to English parents. He is well-known for his willingness to physically transform his body in order to play certain roles. This generally includes extreme diets in order to put on or lose weight. He has been acting from a young age starring in big-budget films as a child including Empire of the Sun and Henry V. Christians other notable roles include Batman in the Dark Knight trilogy, Laurie in Little Women and Patrick Bateman in American Psycho.
