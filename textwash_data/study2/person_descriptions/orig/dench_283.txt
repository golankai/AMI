Judi Dench is a British actress best known for roles in a range of films including James Bond.

Judi Dench lives in Surrey in the UK and is known to be a Quaker, something she has talked about publicly. Dench has appeared in a number of Bafta winning films and many BBC1 dramas. She was also awarded an Olivier award for her appearance in ""A Little Night Music"". Dench made her first professional appearance in 1957 at the Old Vic Company and recently completed a comedy film released in 2020. 

In 2020 Dench became the oldest person to be featured on the cover of British Vogue.
