Lewis Hamilton is a very famous British racing driver, he has been awarded an MBE and he has been knighted so is Sir Lewis Hamilton. He was born 7th January 1985 in Stevenage Hertfordshire, He is the first and only black driver to race in Formula one, he has been subject to racist abuse overtime, at present he competes in Formula One for Mercedes. He has not always driven for Mercedes, he drove for McLaren for around 5yrs. He has won a record record Seven World Drivers championships titles the same has Michael Schumacher. He also holds the record for the most wins.
