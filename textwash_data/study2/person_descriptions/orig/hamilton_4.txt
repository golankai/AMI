Lewis Hamilton is a Formula One British racing driver. He used to drive for MacLaren but now competes for Mercedes. Aged 36, he has had 100 wins and competed in 7 championships. He is hailed as one of the Britain's greatest, if not the best, racing drivers and has won 4 titles in a row. He started driving at a young age, and his father promised to fund and support him in his career. He was the youngest driver to ever win a British carting competition and has inspired countless other children to follow in his footsteps.
