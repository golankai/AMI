Ed Sheeran is a British musician and compositor. He is born the 17th February 1991 in Halifax. He signed a contrat with the record company Atlantic Records for his first album « + ». After that, he released two other albums : « x » and « Divide ». He is red headed with blue eyes. He is also known for a career in cinema. We can see him in movies like Bridget Jones Baby by Sharon Maguire or Yesterday by Danny Boyle. He married Cherry Seaborn in 2019 and they have a girl named Lyra. His last single is « Bad Habits » released the 25th of June 2021. During his childhood, he was member of a choir. He has been accused of plagiarism for his songs « Shape of You » and « Thinking out Loud ».
