Judi Dench is an Academy award winning British actress that is held in high regard. She has also been nominated for an academy award another seven times, won 2 golden globes and won six Laurence Olivier awards throughout the duration of her career. She was names as an Order of the British Empire and later Dame Commander of the British Empire. Dame Judi Dench started out her acting career in the theatre but later starred in blockbuster movies such as the James Bond franchise, Shakespeare in Love and Chocolat.
