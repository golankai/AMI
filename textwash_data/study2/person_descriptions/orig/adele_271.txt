Adele is an English singer who has had many hits including Rolling in the Deep and Chasing Pavements. She has sold over 120 million records worldwide.
She is 33 and was born in London. She is married and has one child.
In 2012 she released the single Skyfall which was the soundtrack to the James Bind film of the same name. Skyfall reached number two in the UK singles chart.
Adele has won 15 Grammy awards and 9 Brit awards. Adele has performed at Glastonbury music festival and was on the pyramid stage.
Adele supports the football club Tottenham Hotspur and is a Labour supporter.
