Benedict Cumberbatch, born 19 July 1976 is a very well known British Actor, known for performances both on stage and screen with the most significant being his role in Sherlock Holmes. 
He has received a number of prestigious awards during his career, including a Primetime Emmy Award, a British Academy Television Award, and a Laurence Olivier Award as well as nominations for many other titles. In 2014 Time Magazine included him in the annual list of top 100 most influential people in the world, and by 2015 he had received a CBE by the Queen to honour his services to the performing arts and to charity. 
Cumberbatch Is married to English theatre and opera director Sophie Hunter, they have been married since February 2015. They also have three sons.
