Daniel Craig is a British male actor in his fifties. He is currently best known for playing the part of James bond in the 007 films. 

Craig was born in Chester, in the north of England. He began acting at school and ended up graduating from Guildhall school of music and drama. 

The first film in which he played James bond was casino royale which was released in 2006. 

Craig was strongly opposed to the Brexit vote and campaigned to remain. 

In 2011 Daniel Craig married Rachel weisz. They share a daughter together.
