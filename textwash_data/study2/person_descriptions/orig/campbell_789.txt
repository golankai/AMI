Naomi Campbell is British model. She is born the 22th of May 1970 in London. She is tall, has dark skin, brown hair and brown eyes.  She starts her career in the music clip « Is This Love » by Bob Marley in 1978. She did her first magazine cover in April 1986 for Elle England. After that, she cat walked for many high fashioned brand such as Ralph Lauren, Versace, Dolce&Gabbana, Louis Vuitton, Vivienne Westwood etc… She is engaged in human rights. In 1994, she broadcasted her single Baby Woman. Naomi Campbell is know in many polemics like the Daily Mirror, the Diamonds of Charles Taylor or for brutality against her assistants.
