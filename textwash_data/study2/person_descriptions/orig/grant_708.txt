Hugh Grant was born in London in 1960. He is a British actor the jumped to the international screens in the 1990s with the movie Four Weddings and a Funeral (for which he won the Golden Globe). He kept working on successful romantic comedies through the 1990s and 2000s. On the 2010s, he started experimenting with other kind of roles, being also acclaimed by the critics and receiving several international awards.

He is married to Anna Eberstein, and has three children with her. He also have another two children with another woman.
