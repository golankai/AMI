Catherine Elizabeth Middleton, better known as Kate Middleton, was born 9th January 1982 in Reading to her parents Michael and Carole. Married to Prince William, she is now referred to as Cather, Duchess of Cambridge, and is 2nd in line to becoming future queen consort. 

Kate grew up in Berkshire and studied Art History at the University of St Andrews in Scotland, where she ultimately met William in 2001. Before they announced their engagement in November 2010, Kate held jobs in retail and marketing whilst pursuing charity work.

Kate married William in 2011 and has since had 3 children; Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis.

Since becoming a member of the Royal Family, the Duchess has undertaken royal duties and engagements in support of the Queen, holding patronage within over 20 charitable and military organisations, to include Action for children and the National Portrait Gallery. 

Kate has been named one of the Times '100 Most Influential People in the World'  in 2011,2012 and 2013.
