Naomi Campbell is one of the most recognisable faces of our time. Born in 1970 in London she was discovered at 15 and went on to become an extremely popular top model walking for many high end fashion houses. The demand for her and her success had her named as a super model. There were 6 models named the big six to which she was given the title of mega model  of them all.

Over her long career naomi has been well known for her diva like demands and tantrums, substance abuse and stints in rehab as well as a long list of famous boyfriends.

Naomi has recently become a mother for the first time  at the age of 50.
