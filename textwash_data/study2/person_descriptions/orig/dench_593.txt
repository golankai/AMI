Judi Olivia Dench was born in York on 9th December 1934. She is best known as one of the best British actresses. She has played roles ranging from James Bond films, to Shakespearean dramas. Her career began in 1957 when she made her professional debut with the Old vid company. Following from this in the years later she performed in several Shakespeare plays. She played Ophelia in Hamlet, Juliet in Romeo and Juliet, and Lady Macbeth in Macbeth. In 1968 she won a BAFTA award as the most promising newcomer.
