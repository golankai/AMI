Joanne Rowling is a world renowned author and creator of the massively successful Harry Potter book series. She is also a film and TV producer.

Rising to fame through the success of the Harry Potter universe she crafted which other than being the best selling book series in history was adapted into 8 highly ambitious and acclaimed mainline movies as well ass spin off movies and other fiction. The Creation of these characters kickstarted the careers  of young actors such as Daniel Radcliffe and Emma Watson  who have gone on to star in many more movies and TV shows.

The widespread appeal of Harry Potter also made certain tourist attractions in the UK famous and attracted visitors form all over the globe like Alnwick Castle and Kings Cross Station. 

Married to Jorge Arantes in 1992 and divorced in 1995, she married again later in life to Neil Murray in 2001 and has 3 children.
