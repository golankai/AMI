Ginger haired musician famous for solo songs performed with guitar and singing. He writes music for his own performance as well as ghostwriting for other musicians, famously credited for writing “love yourself” by Justin Bieber. He married his childhood friend Cherry Seaborn in 2019. He is visibly recognized by his long messy hair, glasses, colorful tattoos on his arms. He’s recorded 5 albums and sold more than 150 million records worldwide. Alongside being awarded an MBE in 2019 he was named artist of the decade by the official charts company
