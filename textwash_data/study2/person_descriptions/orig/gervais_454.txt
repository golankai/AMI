Ricky Gervais is best known as the creator of ""The Office"", in which he also played the lead character ""David Brent"".  Prior to his acting career, Gervais was a New Romantic pop singer, who attempted a career in music but failed to generate the requisite level of success/ fame/ adulation.  After The Office, he went on to write and star in the TV series ""Extras"", which he co-wrote with his frequent collaborator Stephen Merchant, and which featured cameo roles from many famous faces from film, TV and music - including Robert De Niro, David Bowie, Kate Winslet and Daniel Radcliffe.  He has written several comedic books such as Flanimals.
