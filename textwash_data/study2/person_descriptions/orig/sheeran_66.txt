Ed Sheeran is a singer, who is best known for being a big ginger, guitarist and singer. He is best known for coming from ""nothing"", busking on the street, and now has various critically acclaimed albums, called plus, divide, multiply and equals. He has had various hit singles, which have risen to number 1, and sells out large arena tours. He made an appearance in the film Yesterday, which focused on life without the Beatles. His talents as a songwriter were challenged in the film, and he only made a short appearance. He is very private, and is married, which was a very private ceremony, away from the public eye.
