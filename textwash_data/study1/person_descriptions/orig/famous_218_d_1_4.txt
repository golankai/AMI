Elton John is a flamboyant figure who wears outrageous clothes, loud individual spectacles all in vivid colours and sparkles. He is short of stature and wears platform shoes, also in bright colours with sparkles. He has a gap in his two front teeth.
He is an older gentleman and had thinning hair as a young man but now supports a healthy mane following a follicle transplant. He is now a little rotund. He smiles a lot and appears emotional at times, which was obvious when he played at the funeral of Princess Diana. He sings and plays the piano and has been a successful musician for many years. He is considered iconic by many.He supports charities.
He is married with children but keeps his private life fairly private.
