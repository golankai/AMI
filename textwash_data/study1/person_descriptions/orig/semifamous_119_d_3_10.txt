Irvin Brooks is a guy that is related to sports, especially baseball, and it always has been, he's and amazing hitter, and he for the Brooklyn Royal Giants. Many people are confused about the origin os this player because a lot of reference books said that his name is ""Chester"" Brooks (the first time appeared printed was in an article made by Cumberland Posey)and that he was born in the Bahamas, but thats not true he actually was born in Key West, Florida, and people during his carrer always refered to him as Irvin.
