Mick Jagger, formally known as Sir Michael Phillip Jagger, is a singer, songwriter, actor, and producer. Jagger was born on the 26th of July in 1943 in Kent, UK. He rose to fame as the lead singer of the infamous band 'The Rolling Stones' and later as a solo performer. Jagger was even knighted for his contribution to music in 2003. The singer has also ventured into the film industry, dabbling in actors and more notably forming Jagged Films. In his personal life, Jagger has fathered 8 children and has 5 grandchildren. He is currently 77, and was even planning on touring in 2019- but was interrupted by health issues.
