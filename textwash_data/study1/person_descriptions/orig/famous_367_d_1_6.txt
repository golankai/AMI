Adele is 32 years old and is known as a world famous pop singer.
She attended the famous BRIT school and graduated from there in 2006. She released her first album in 2008 titled, 19, which went platinum in both UK and USA. 
She released her 2nd album, titled 21, in 2011 and this was even more succesful than the first album. There were numerous hit singles on this album.
She then went on to write and sing the theme tune for the James Bond movie, Skyfall. This earned a various awards inclusing an Academy Award.
Since then she has released a third album, titled, 25, which was just as succesful as the first two.
She is now known world wide as one of the worlds best selling artists.
