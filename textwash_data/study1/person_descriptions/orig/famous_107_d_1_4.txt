Elton John is a famous singer and talented pianist from the UK and has been for 5 decades. He is a pop singer and one of the first openly gay singers (although previously married to a woman). He is a flamboyant character with a reputation which he likes to encourage as a diva. Elton John is married to David Furnish and they have adopted children. He is famous for his White parties where all guests wear white and entertainment is very elaborate. Elton John changed his name when he became a singer, it is not his birth name which is Reg Dwight. Elton John has a loud sense of dress and likes to make a statement particularly of his glasses. One of the most famous songs of his career was the song he sang at Princess Diana's funeral called 'Candle in the Wind'.
