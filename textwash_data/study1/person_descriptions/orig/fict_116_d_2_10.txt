Amelie Crooks is a famous IT trainer known for setting up bootcamps that teach other women how to code. Her courses are popular all over the world and she has been teaching them for 10 years now.
Amelie Crooks was born Austria but moved to UK when she was 18 to start her Bachelor degree in a UK university. In her course she was one out of 2 women. This gave her the idea that there should be more women in tech. As her private life, she keeps a low profile but people believe she is currently single. When she opened her Bootcamp she had full support of her at the time girlfriend.Ironically her girlfriend fell in love with another girl on one of the first courses.Despite this Amelie Crooks kept teaching and did not let the pain stop her.
