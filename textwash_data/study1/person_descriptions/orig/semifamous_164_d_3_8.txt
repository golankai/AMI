Alexsander Gryazin is an ex Russian football player who made his debut in 1992 for Moscow. He was born in 1978 and spent his time growing up in remote Russia and playing football before being scouted for the team.  He has blonde hair and blue eyes and is of a medium build. He played in 9 different teams all the way up until 2005 when he was 30 years old and seemingly ended his career. The longest time he spent at a club was 4 years from 1996-2000 when he played with FC Saturn Ramenskoye. His shortest time at a club was at FC Metallurg Lipetsk when he was 26 years old from 2001-2001 and he was there for less than a year.
