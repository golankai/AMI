Amelie Crooks is a white, female, Austrian born 9th September 1978. 
Whilst Austrian born Amelie studied in the UK and graduated with an IT masters from Cambridge University. 
Since then Amelie has gone on to work for several large organisations including Amazon, Tesco and most recently the NHS. With her IT Masters she specialises in IT training and has over 20 years experience in this field. 
Amelie is now single but has previously married and divorced in 2018. She shares 5 children with her ex Husband. Her children are John, Paul, George, Ringo and Linda named after her favourite band, the Beatles.
