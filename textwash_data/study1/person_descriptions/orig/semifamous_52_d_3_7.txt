Charles Black was a highly respected University lecturer at a variety of academic institutions most notably Yale.
He was a highly intellectual outspoken man, often critical of both the death penalty and racism.
He has published a number of literary works that helped to model and change the teaching and implementation of criminal law along with human rights.
There was more to the man than law and teaching, he was keen follower of music along with writing poems and non-fiction.
He sounds like he was a very interesting gentleman who would have featured heavily on social media and news reports if he still lived today.
