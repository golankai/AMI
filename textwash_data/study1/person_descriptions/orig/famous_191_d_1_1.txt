Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor, best known for playing Sherlock in the recent BBC adaptation along with Martin Freeman.  He trained at the London Academy of music and dramatic arts [LAMDA]. His other credits include Doctor Strange in the marvel movies, The Imitation Game, Star Trek - Into Darkness and 1917.  He is also an accomplished theatre actor with roles including Hamlet in Hamlet, Directed by Lyndsey Turner, and Frankenstein at the National Theatre, recently streamed on YouTube for free.  In 2015 he received a CBE for his services to arts and charity and in 2014 was voted in the Top 100 most influential people in the world by Time Magazine.
