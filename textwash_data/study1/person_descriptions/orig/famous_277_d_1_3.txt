Emma Watson an actress, who was born in France and had moved to England at the age of six. From a young age she had an ambition to become an actress, thus she played key roles in school productions and has led to her being featured in the major cinema hits i.e. Harry Potter and Beauty and the Beast. She has also been involved in modelling and acts as an activist supporting Women's Rights. Throughout her years she has been recognised for her work as was awarded as British Artist of the Year in 2014 and has been appointed as an Ambassador for the UN Women Goodwill and helped in launching an UN Women campaign .
