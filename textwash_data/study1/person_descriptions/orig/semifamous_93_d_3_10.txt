Irvin Woodberry Brooks was an American baseball. He was born June 5, 1891 and dies February 4, 1966. Brooks was born in Key West, Florida however everal reference books incorrectly list him as ""being born in the Bahamas,  and during his playing career he was known as ""Irvin"" (or occasionally as ""Irving""), and then later by the nickname ""ChesterFrom 1918 to 1933 he played pitcher, infielder, and outfielder. Brooks spent his entire playing career with the Brooklyn Royal Giants and was an outstanding hitter. He is inducting in the baseball museum and is in an all-time league baseball all-star team.
