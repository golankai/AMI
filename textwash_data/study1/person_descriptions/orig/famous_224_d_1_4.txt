Elton John is a very famous musician who is able to write both catch pop songs as well as slower emotional ballads. He is also famous for his sense of style and outlandish outfits he wears whilst performing. He struggled with both his sexuality, substance issues, and fame; he spent time in rehab due to these issues. His life was made into the film 'Rocketman' which was well received critically. He is quite short and has put on quite a lot of weight since his youth. He has taken to mentoring with younger lesser known artists however appears to have a cynical view on the state of current music industry believing there to be very few artists of quality around.
