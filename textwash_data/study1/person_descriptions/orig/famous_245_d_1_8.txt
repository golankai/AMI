Born in 1974 in Wales, Bale is a well recognised and awarded British Actor. He is the son of a cirus performer and commercial pilot. He grew up in Wales, England and Portugal. 

He began acting aged 13 in Empire of the Sun. Taking on supporting roles over the next 10 years in Little Women, Pocahontas and American Psycho. 

After which he played roles in Batman, Terminator Salvation and The Fighter. He has been named as a sex symbol and among the 100 most influential people in the world. 

He currently resides in Los Angles with his wife Sibi and their two children. He is a patron of the environmental charities, namely the World Wildlife Fund, Sea Shepherd Conservation Society and Greenpeace.
