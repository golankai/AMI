Aleksandr is a Soviet and Russian pop singer and actor. He has received various awards including the Honoured Artist of Russia And the chanson of the year award.

aleksandr graduated from music school, studying piano, after graduating from eighth grade he studied radio apparatus construction.  He dropped out and began to play in mytishchi ensemble.

He worked in an ensemble band Dobry molidtsy, he released 7 albums, 3 of them were a collection of his own songs,
