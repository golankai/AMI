Clyde is an Indian professional footballer who currently plays for Ozone FC. When he was younger, Clyde attended a very prestigious private school in Mumbai where he played football during PE lessons. Clyde did not have to try very hard to achieve his football goals as his best friend from school's, Bonnie, dad was the manager at Mumbai football club where Clyde made his debut in 2016 at the age of 23. He quickly became Mumbai's best defender and then transferred to Ozone football club where he has played since 2018. Clyde has a bad attitude which stems from him being spoilt when he was younger. He often fights with managerial staff at his clubs and is often not selected.
