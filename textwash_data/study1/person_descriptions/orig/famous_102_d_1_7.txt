Daniel Radcliffe is an English man who is most well-known for playing Harry Potter in the titular movies, but has also acted in and produced numerous other movies and TV shows. He was born on the 23rd of July 1989. He has brown hair and blue eyes, and stands at 5'5. He has has over 40 acting credits on IMDB, spanning all the way back to 1999 and has since garnered an estimated net worth of over $110 million according to multiple online sources. He started acting as a child, and was still attending school during his first roles of Harry Potter, and was teased by the other school students because of this.
