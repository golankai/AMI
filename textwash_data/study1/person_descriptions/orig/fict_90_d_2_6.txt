Rinda Schuster is an English businesswoman, currently working as a sales executive. She was born in 1951 to British parents in Delaware City, Delaware, United States. She moved back to England at the age of 2 with her parents and siblings and went to school in the suburbs of East Sussex. Schuster pursued Journalism and Mass Communications at Queen Mary University of London and worked as a journalist for a few years after her undergraduate degree. She then started her own e-commerce store and experimented with entrepreneurship before getting married at the age of 33 to Donald Schuster, an English film director. They now live in South London with 2 children.
