Elton John is a famous musician/singer. While he is known for his various pop songs, he also worked on the soundtrack to 'The Lion King' alongside lyricist Tim Rice. Of these works, three of his songs were nominated for the 'Best Original Song' Oscar in 1995, of which 'Can You Feel The Love Tonight' won. More recently, he is known for the biopic film 'Rocketman', and his musical career was the focus of a Christmas advert for retailer John Lewis.

He is one of music's most prominent gay icons, and is currently married to David Furnish. He has previously voiced his opinions on all manner of political issues. As well as supporting homosexual rights, he was also a vocal supporter of remaining in the European Union in 2016.
