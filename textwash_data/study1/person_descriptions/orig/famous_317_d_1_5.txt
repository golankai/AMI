Mick Jagger is an english singer from Kent, popularly known for being the lead and songwriter for the Rolling stones band. He is known as a rock legend and has an upbeat, life of the party personality. However band mates such as Richard who jagger had a strained relationship with, describes Jagger to be a difficult member to work with, drawing on words such as ""snob"" and ""unbearable"" but still somehow ""loveable"". Jagger is a man who lives life of the edge, he was briefly arrested in 1967 for substance abuse. Jagger has had a colourful love life being romantically involved with different women and is the father of 8 children.
