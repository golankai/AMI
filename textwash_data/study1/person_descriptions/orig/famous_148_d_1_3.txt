Emma Watson is a 30-year-old actress, widely known for her role in the Harry Potter franchise by starring as one of the lead characters Hermione Granger. Along with this, she has become involved in multiple philanthropic organisations to become one of the most widely known stars in the world. For example, she is the UN Goodwill Ambassador and helped launch the HeForShe campaign for gender equality

Other ventures include her modelling career working with famous brands such as Burberry. For both her philanthropic roles as well as her acting, she was won many accolades including the Boston Society of Film Critics award
