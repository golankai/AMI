Emma Watson is a British actress, most famous for her role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter series. Most recently she has starred in the live-action remake of Disney's Beauty and the Beast and the 2019 adaption of Little Women. Emma is also well known for her activism and is a vocal feminist, advocating for women's rights around the globe, notably as a UN ambassador, launching the HeForShe campaign in 2014. 

After starting her acting career at the age of 10 in the Harry Potter series, Emma juggled her education with filming, often tutored on set and received very good grades. She then attended Brown University, where she graduated with a bachelors degree in English literature.
