Irvin Brooks was an American citizen who played for the Brooklyn Royal Giants. A black man, he played in the negro league. His date of birth was 05 June 1891 and he died 04 February 1966. 
His nickname was Chester and some articles attribute this as his name. By all accounts he was talented, described as an outstanding hitter. Although he played pitched infielder and outfielder positions in his career. The Wikipedia article indicates that his career spanned 1918 to 1933. Born in key West, Florida, he lived to the age of seventy four, which feels a good age for someone living in that time. A brief spell in Atlantic City saw him aligned to the Bacharach Giants in 1931. 
It is a testament to his career that a Wikipedia page is in his name in 2020.
