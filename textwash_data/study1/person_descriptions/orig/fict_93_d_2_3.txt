Hasan Daniel is a Consulting civil engineer who studied his Civil engineering degree at Toronto University. He currently resides in his homeland of Canada. He has travelled around the globe working on engineering projects. He specialises in bridges and has been involved in many famous bridge building projects such as the world's longest sea crossing bridgein China. Including its access roads, the bridge spans 55km (34 miles) and connects Hong Kong to Macau and the mainland Chinese city of Zhuhai. It took over nine years to build and is in the top 10 impressive bridges in the world. He is 31 years old and in a relationship.
