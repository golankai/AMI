Clyde Fernandes, born in India in 1993, is a rising star in professional football. His previous enthusiasm for the sport earned him a place on the under-sixteens national Indian team in 2010, with whom he travelled to play abroad in South Africa. His professional debut was at the age of 23 in 2016, playing for Mumbai Football Club - his hometown team - against Shillong Lajong, another Indian club. His debut lasted 59 minutes, and the match was tied. He remained with Mumbai until he transferred to Ozone Football Club in 2018. Now based in Bengaluru, he currently holds the position of defender for Ozone FC in the I-League 2nd Division.
