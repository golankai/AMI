Dr Brantley Ratke is a 37 year old educator and viking warfare historian based in Sweden. Ratke studied in Gothenburg, leaving his birth town of Malmo. He Returned to Malmo in 2013, leaving in 2016 following increasing violence, crime and explosions occurring as a result of the Swedish Government's response to the 2015 Migrant Crisis.

Ratke focuses on late period Viking civilisation and has published 3 books on the topic, and has adapted well to newer digital platforms: He has gained over 30 million views on streams discussing his work and was hired as a consultant on the show 'Vikings'.  

Outside of his academic work, Dr Ratke is well known as a batchelor and advocate of the New Temperance Movement, or Revived Temperance, a promotion of lower to zero alcohol living.
