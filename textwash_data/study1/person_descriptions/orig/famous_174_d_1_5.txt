He was born in Dartford in 1943 and has been married twice, once to Jerry Hall and once you L’Wren Scott. Mick Jagger has 8 children. He has a net worth of 360 million dollars. 

Mick Jagger can play the harmonica and guitar as well as being an extremely talented vocalist.
