Emma Watson is an English actress best known for her starring role in the Harry Potter series of films.
She was born in Paris in 1990 to English parents (both lawyers) and moved to Oxfordshire when she was 5 years old.
Her film career began at age 10 with her portrayal of Hermione Grainger in the original Harry Potter movie.
In later years as well as acting she has promoted womens rights and was appointed an UN Womens Goodwill Ambassador in 2014.
She has a bachelors degree in English Literature from Brown University (Rhode Island, USA).
She was also modelled for Burberry and Lancome.
Her most recent role was as Meg March in the 2019 film adaptation of Louisa M Alcotts iconic novel 'Little Women'
