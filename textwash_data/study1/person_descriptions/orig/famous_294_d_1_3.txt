Emma Watson is an English born actress model and activist. She is best known by the majority of the general public for her role of  Hermione Granger is the Harry Potter trilogy of films which where and still are hugely popular. She was born on the 15th April 1990 which makes her 30 years of age. Since the end of the Harry Potter trilogy in 2011 she has appeared in around eight movies such as Noah and the beauty and the beast. Throughout Emma's career she has ben nominated for numerous film awards and her successes include best supporting actress  and British artist of the year amongst many. Watson is a staunch feminist and has promoted education for girls, visiting Bangladesh and Zambia to do so. In July 2014, she was appointed as a UN Women Goodwill ambassador. In September, Emma delivered an address at UN Headquarters in New York City to launch the UN Women campaign  which calls for men to advocate for gender equality. In that speech she said she began questioning gender-based assumptions at the age of eight when she was called bossy. Watson's speech also described feminism as ""the belief that men and women should have equal rights and opportunities and declared that the perception of man-hating"" is something that has to stop. Emma later said she received threats within twelve hours of making the speech, which left her upset. In Emma's personal life she is said to be dating a male for around a year.
