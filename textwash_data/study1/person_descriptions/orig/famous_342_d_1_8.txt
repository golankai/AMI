Christian Bale is an English actor who has won an Academy award and 2 golden globes. He had his first role in 1987 at age 13 in Empire of the Sun. He gained a lot more popularity when he played the serial killer in American Psycho in 2000. In 2004 he loast 63lb for his role in the machinist but within 6 months he gained 100lb to star as Batman in the Dark Knight trilogy. He took on more starring roles after that and won a golden globe and academy award for best supporting actor for his role as Dicky Eklund in The Fighter in 2010. He also won a golden globe for best actor in 2019 when he starred as race car driver Ken Miles in Ford v Ferrari.
