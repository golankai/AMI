Irvin Brooks was a pitcher For the Brooklyn Royal Giants Baseball Team. He was born in 1891 at a time when attitudes to black people in America were still of racial segregation, so he played in the Negro Leagues, the home of the professional black teams. Irvin was a wonderful player and all time Baseball all star team for his league. He was born in Key West Florida and was mistakenly known as ""Chester"" back in the day. His career spanned 15 years which shows his commitment and dedication to the game of baseball. Not much is known about Irvins private life and he passed away in 1966 at the grand old age of age 74. He treasured many fond memories of his time at the Brooklyn Royal Giants.
