Ferd Homenick is a 53 year old, single man who resides in Bruges in Belgium. He works as a craft beer dealer, and has gained a reputation for championing Belgiums wide range of craft beers, promoting them around the world by attending food and alcohol trade fairs and festivals. He is passionate about fruit beers, which originated in Belgium and have been brewed for centuries. Little known outside of the country, he writes a blog and runs his own YouTube channel to promote these beers and has started to build a strong online following, both from others in the industry and from interested consumers.
