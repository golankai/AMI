Hermann Pacocha Jr. is a successful middle-aged Marketing Executive who lives in Spain.
In his personal live he is settled and has forged a relationship with his partner.

He first became interested in marketing when he helped to promote and advertise his parent's business.
He was successful in every level of education which concluded with him studying marketing at the University of Barcelona.
Hermann is a determined and loyal character, to this day he still maintains and develops the marketing strategy for his parents business which has now become the family business.
