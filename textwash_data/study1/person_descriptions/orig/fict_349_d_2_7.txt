Mrs. Liana Glover MD is a chartered accountant who currently resides in Ljubljana, Slovenia. 

Mrs. Glover initially trained as a doctor at the university of california, berkeley and moved to New York to begin practising. Two years after her move however, she met and fell in love with Dennis Jones. Jones lived in France, and for 3 years they maintained a long-distance relationship before deciding to give up their jobs and travel around the world together. 

When money became short, they decided to settle in Paris, France. Not wanting to return to the stress of medicine, but wanting to train in a reliable job, Mrs Glover decided to retrain as an accountant.
