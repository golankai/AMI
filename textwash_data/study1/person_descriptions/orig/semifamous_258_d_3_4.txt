Clyde Fernandez is a professional soccer player, born in India on the 18th of October 1983 which would make him 26 years old. He is quite tall and has been playing professional soccer for roughly 4 years, since his debut for Mumbai football club on the 11th of January 2016. He played for the Mumbai football club from the year 2016 to the year 2018 and switched to Ozoe FC in 2018 to play as a defender, in which he holds the football jersey number 27. He is quite physically fit as one would expect from a professional athlete, he has a quite tanned brown skin, and he has made it was out of seven appearances so far for his team Ozoe FC.
