Enoch Lincoln, a distant relation to Abraham Lincoln, lived in the USA in the late 18th - early 19th Century. He was born into a family of lawyers and, after graduating from Harvard University, followed in the family profession practising in Salem, Massachusetts. He then went on to become a member of the United States Congress for eight years serving in the 15th through 19th congresses until he retired in 1826. He also acted as the governor of Maine after winning 3 elections all with a majority of 90%+ in the last two years of his life. His body now rests in a mausoleum opposite the state house in Maine.
