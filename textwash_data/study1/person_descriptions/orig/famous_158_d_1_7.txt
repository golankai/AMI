Daniel Radcliffe (born 23/07/1989) is an actor who rose to fame playing the boy wizard Harry Potter in the Harry Potter film franchise based on the books written by J K Rowling. He played Harry in all eight movies during which time he became one of the highest paid actors in the world. 

He has since gone on to star in a string of other movies such as The Woman in Black and branch out into stage acting.

He splits his time between New York and London and has been dating Erin Darke for several years. He supports several charities in particular childrens ones and has left-centrist political views.
