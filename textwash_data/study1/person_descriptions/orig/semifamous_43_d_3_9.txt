Walter K Chorn born on 21st January 1885 he had his own law firm for 2 years but his real love was playing football he played for the Vanderbilt Commodores where he was considered to be an excellent player but was also well liked due to his commitment to the team and the game which he gave his all too. He was in the fraternity Kappa Sigma at college and then pursued a career in law then he was a one time insurance superintendent.
He sadly died at the young age of 48 in Missorouri on 26th February 1933. He was remembered for his position of guard and tackle in the Vanderbilt team. He had a number of career highlights including the all southern in 1906.
