Walter Knaus Chorn was a football player,  lawyer and insurance superintendent of the state of Missouri (though that was a one off). 
He was born on the 21st January 1885 and died 26th February 1933. He weighed 170lbs. 

He went to Central College in Missouri and Vanderbilt University in Tennesse, where he played football and was a member of Kappa Sigma. He played the positions of a guard and a table for the Vanderbilts Commodores football team. He graduated University with a LLB in Law. 

In 1909 he had opened his own law office and started reviewing Missouri laws. By 1913 he was a chief clerk in the Missouri Supreme Court.
