Dr. Dwaine O'Conner is 62 years of age and resides in Germany, he is currently in a long term relationship. He works as a Hydrographic surveyor in Berlin, In his spare time enjoys many pastimes including all forms of exercise especially cycling & swimming, he was once an Olympic swimmer for his country and swam in the 1984 Olympics in Los Angeles.

He studied at Cambridge University where he achieved many honors and met his long term partner, they many different pets, cats, dogs and reptiles.

He and his partner enjoy cruising around the world and have just returned from a tour around the caribbean where they did plenty of snorkling.
