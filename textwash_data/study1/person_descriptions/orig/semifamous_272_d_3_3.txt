Yassine El Ghanassy  is a Belgian footballer who plays  left wing.
He started playing when he was 15 at the team Fayt-Manage At La Louvière he was signed by the youth where he quickly gained the attention of many sides. At the age of 16, he went to the English team Birmingham City, impressing on trial, but a transfer didn't follow as La Louvière wanted a transfer price which was too high for such a young player. El Ghanassy received a 6 prison sentence after appearing in court for a speeding offence . He was given the jail termand was banned from driving for two years. He was caught for reckless driving in June 2016.
