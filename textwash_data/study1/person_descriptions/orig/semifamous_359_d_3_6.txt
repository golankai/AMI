Malcolm Stephen  Thomas Dunston is a former England cricketer. He was born in 1950, making him 69 years old. He was born n Cornwall. He made his first debut in cricket playing against Devon. He batted with his right hand. After this between 1971-1974 he represented England in 12 first class matches. He also played for Minor counties West and minor counties South. In 1984-1988 he represented the country in eight trophy matches the last of which was against Dorset. He had a long career playing cricket and always did his best for his country. He had a batting average of 16/64. He enjoyed 20 years at Cornwall.
