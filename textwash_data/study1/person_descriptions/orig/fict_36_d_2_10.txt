Amelie Crooks was born in the south of Spain, (Malaga) in 2 February of 1978. Her father is from Spain and her mother is from France. She studied in Oxford university did a MBS Degree in computer science in Cambridge. She is a very successful woman. She has written several books about Flask Web development in Python.
Her first book was written in 2010, “How to Code in Python using Flask”. It was very popular and she sold over 8,9M copies of the first edition.
In 2015 she move to Austria to do what she loves the most, teaching and training web developers.
She is a single at the moment waiting for her blue prince to come.
She is an IT trainer but her passion is about photography.
