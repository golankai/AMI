Clyde Cyril Fernandes, is a 26 year old (born 26 / 10 / 93) professional Indian footballer, currently playing for Ozone Football Club in the the second division. He plays in the defender position. He is 1.83 metres tall.
He made his debut for Mumbai Football Club in January 2016, playing for fifty nine minutes, before the conclusion of the game at a 0 - 0 draw. He played in a total of seven appearances for the club. SInce his initial apperances, then he has had no further appearances, either for Mumbai Football Club or Ozoe Football Club.
He has also played for the Indian under 16 team against South Africa.
