Ms. Lucia Hamill was born in Ireland and a very lively and interesting character. She was known in the local village for being very proactive and involved in many of the church fete and festivities that were carried out during the year. She was famous for making marmalade jam and also was very well known for her sloe gin which she distributed around her friends for a very good winter warmer on those cold and chilly night. She was approximately 5 footAnd had an amazing sense of humour, also she was very knowledgeable in martial arts which was interesting. Her favourite food was India and she always hosted dinner parties to show off her cookery skills
