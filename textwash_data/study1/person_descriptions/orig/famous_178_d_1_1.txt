Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor, aged 44. He is best known for portraying the title character in Sherlock, a television programme which ran for 13 episodes between 2010-2017. He has appeared in numerous plays, television programmes and films, including Shakespearean plays, War Horse, Atonement, The Hobbit, Dr Strange, Avengers Infinity War, and Star Trek Into Darkness. He has narrated a number of natural history documentaries for National Geographic, the BBC, and Discovery channels. After narrating one documentary about penguins he was mocked on the Graham Norton show as he can't pronounce the word 'penguin'! He also regularly appears in radio plays on BBC Radio 4. He also narrates audiobooks.
