Ed sheeran is a young British solo musician who exploded onto the music scene in early 2011, since then he has racked up a number of number one hits both in his own right and as a collaboration with other artists. Some of his hits include “shape of you” and “Lego house” Sheeran has won a number of awards for his music and has also been awarded an MBE. His latest album % set the record for most number ones from a single album. His debut single ""The A Team"" was released as a digital download in the UK and entered the Chart at number 3, selling over 58,000 copies in the first week. It was the best-selling debut single and the overall eighth-best selling single of 2011, selling 801,000 copies in total.
