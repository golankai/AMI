Hermann Pacocha Jr. is the eldest of three children born to Hermann and Carmen Pacocha. He spent his childhood in Bilbao before attending university in Barcelona. He has a great passion for football which he played at a high level as a junior. After graduating he got a job in marketing at Malaga football club. He currently works at Valencia football club as a marketing executive and enjoys his job. He is married, he met his wife Alicia at university, and they have two dogs and one daughter. They enjoy going for long walks in the countryside and along the coast around Valencia where they live. Hermann also enjoys playing football with his 10 year old daughter.
