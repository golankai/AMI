Ed Sheeran is a singer/songwriter and guitarist. He has had several hit records. His style of music is pop. He self-released his first records before being signed.

He is from Yorkshire and was born in 1991. He moved to London when 14 years old and played gigs while writing new material. As time went on he managed to get support slots with larger acts which allowed him to expand his fan base, and his first label-released LP sold over a million copies in the UK.

As well as his own material, Sheeran has written songs for other acts such as One Direction, Taylor Swift, Tim McGraw and Grace Hill.

He was married in 2017 married and has one child, a daughter, born in 2020.
