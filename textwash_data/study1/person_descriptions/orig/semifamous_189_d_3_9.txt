Walter K chorn was born January 21 1885 in Howard county missouri. He died in kansas city missouri on 26 February at the age f just 48.  Walter was the youngest of 5 brothers. as chicldren the brothers would play football  this led walter to a love of the sport he practiced when ever and where ever he could. he had dreams of becoming a world famous football player  and hearing the crowds chanting his name. Walter attended Central College in Fayette, Missouri and Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee. during this time he studied law as well as playing the position of guard with the vanderbilt football team. Walter opened his own law firm after graduating with a law degree. He married a fellow student from his  university they had 2 children. a girl and a boy. Walters son followed in his footsteps to become a lawyer
