Sam Smith is a young male singer and songwriter. His first major song was latch. He lives in London and is 28 years old. He has facial hair and a short hair cut and sometimes wears glasses. He mostly wears a blazer with a jumper underneath. He has been known to post controversial tweets on twitter. He has dark brown hair and used to date Brandon Flynn who was in the Netflix series 13 reasons why. Sam Smith has also done a song for a James Bond film as has Adele. Other songs he has written include Too Good at Goodbyes, I'm not the only one and Stay with Me. He has featured as a singer on the Graham Norton Show.
