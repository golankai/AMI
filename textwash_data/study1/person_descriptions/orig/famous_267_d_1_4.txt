Elton John is a world renowned singer song writer from England.  He has had many number 1 singles and albums.  He has completed many world tours and has also written musicals.  His most recognised songs include Your Song, Rocket Man, Crocodile Rock, Goodbye Yellow Brick Road, Circle Of Life, Candle In The Wind (which he re recorded and sang a new version of at the funeral of Princess Diana) Nikita, I'm Still Standing, Saturday's Alright For Fighting and I Want Love.  He is married to David Furnish and has a child.  A biopic called Rocket Man was recently released and Elton con tributed greatly to the film himself and wrote new music for it.
