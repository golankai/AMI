Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor.  Best known for his roles of Sherlock, in which he played the eponymous character, and Doctor Strange in the Marvel Universe Films.  Born in London, England in 1976 he rose to fame in 2004 playing Stephen Hawking in the TV movie Hawking but it was 2010 before his breakthrough role on the TV series playing Sherlock Homes.  He has worked in film, theatre, radio and TV.  After university he took a gap year and spent time volunteering in a Tibetan Buddhist Monastery in India where he taught English.  His female fans were known as Cumberbitches, but are now known as the CumberCollective or Cumberpeople.  Benedict  Cumberbatch was reportedly uncomfortable with Cumberbitches because it was not inclusive and pejorative.
