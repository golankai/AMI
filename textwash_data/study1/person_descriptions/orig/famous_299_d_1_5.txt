He is an English singer, famous for the song ‘Moves like Jagger’. He is a well known member of the group The Rolling Stones. He is also a songwriter and film producer. He is known for the movies Performance, Freejack, Shine a Light and Running Out of Luck. The Rolling Stones have produced lots of music over the years songs such as Sympathy for the Devil, You can’t always get what you want, Jumpin Jack Flash and She’s a Rainbow. He has been married in the past but is with a new partner currently. He also has many children. He grew up in Kent, not too far from London. Jagger has written most of the Rolling Stones' songs together with Richards, and they continue to collaborate musically. In the late 1960s, Jagger starred in the films with Performance (1970) and Ned Kelly (1970) to a mixed reception. He began a solo career in 1985, releasing his first album, She's the Boss, and joined the electric supergroup SuperHeavy in 2009. Relationships with the Stones' members, particularly Richards, deteriorated during the 1980s, but Jagger has always found more success with the band than with his solo and side projects.
