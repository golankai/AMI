Christian Bale is a method actor, he is known for drastically changing his looks for his movies and getting into character for weeks ahead of filming. He is extremely good looking and played Batman.  He was born in Wales although most people think he is American and has worn two golden globes as well as an academy award. His mum was a circus performer and throughout his childhood he lived in many countries such as Portugal, England and South Africa. He is really well known for staring in cult films such as The Machinist and also does focus on many indie films. He won an Oscar in 2011 for Best Supporting Actor through staring in a film with Mark Whalberg called The Fighter, where he played Marks younger brother in the film.
