Hugh Grant is a well known British actor and producer, his most iconic role was in Four Weddings and a Funeral, Notting Hil, Music and Lyrics and many more. Through-out his career he also had many smaller roles mainly in television. He was won numerous awards from his acting career from a golden globe to a BAFTA. Hugh Grant has grossed over 3billion dollars worldwide from his 29 movies / tv-shows as of 2018. Born on September 9th 1980 in London he is currently 60 years old and is married to Anna Elisabet, he also has 3 children. Before Hugh Grant got into performing he studied English at oxford.
