Mr Enoch Lincoln (born in 1788 and died in 1829) is most famous for being a US Representative of Massachusetts and Maine and governor of maine for two years before his death. He was a Democratic-Republican representative (party founded by Thomas Jefferson and James Madison, promoting, among other values, political equality). 
Enoch was a lawyer by training and elected and re-elected for public service. The vote is his favour is reported to be overwhelming on several occasions. A town in Maine is named after him - ""Lincoln"". 
Interestingly, it is alleged that Mr Enoch Lincoln is a distant relative of the 16th USA president.
