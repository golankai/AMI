Daniel Radcliffe is a short, white British actor in his early 30s. He has short brown hair, blue eyes and dark bushy eyebrows. Sometimes he is seen with a short beard. His skin is pale. Radcliffe's most famous acting job was that of the character Harry Potter, whom he portrayed during his childhood. When portraying this character he would wear a school uniform and round circular glasses, as well as a scar which was painted onto his forehead. He has a social media presence. Recently on Twitter he was seen clashing with J.K. Rowling, the writer of the Harry Potter series, due to her political views, suggesting that Radcliffe is not afraid to stand up for what he believes in.
