Emma Watson is a British actress, model and activist.  She is 30 years old and was born in Paris, France but was brought up in Oxfordshire. She trained as an actress at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach Theatre Arts.  She first found fame with her role in the Harry Potter films as Hermione Grainger.  She has then taken on many other roles, such as Belle in Beauty and the Beast. She has also had a few TV roles including a role in The Vicar of Dibley. She is currently single and describes herself as a spiritual Universalist. She also attended Brown University and completed a degree in English literature.
