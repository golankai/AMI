Sir Elton John is a 73 year old singer and songwriter, most notably known for his song 'Candle in the Wind' from 1997. The genre of music is rock, soft rock, and glam rock. 
He plays the piano, and regularly performs tributes to notable late celebrities, including Princess Diana. He wears bold framed glasses, and has sandy coloured hair. He has two children and has been married twice. 
He owned Watford football club for a few years, as in an avid fan of football. He also featured in the 2018 John Lewis advert, which celebrated his life from childhood until now. 
He is the patron of many AIDS charities, and has donated a lot of his time to supporting this cause.
