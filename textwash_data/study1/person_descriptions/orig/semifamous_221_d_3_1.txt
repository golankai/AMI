Enoch Lincoln was an American man from Massachusetts. He studies at the prestigious Harvard college which is now Harvard university He worked as a us representative and was also the governor of Maine for some time as well. He studied law for many years in various places in the United States aside from Harvard. The town of Lincoln which is in Maine where he worked, is named after him. Lincoln, who shares a name with a former United States president Abraham Lincoln, was actually related to him. He was elected several times in several different positions in the political party of the democratic republicans. Till today he is a very famous figure in Maine.
