Enoch was born in 1788 in Massauchsetts and was known for being a US representative for the state. He was the Governor of Maine from 1827 until his death in 1829 and a Democratic Republican. He studied to be a lawyer and in 1811 he began practising this role in Salem. In 1819 he became an elected member of the American Antiquarian Society which is the oldest History Society in American history with a national focus. The city of Lincoln, Maine is named after him. He won each term as Governor of Maine with over 90% of the votes so was very popular with the people. Enoch Lincoln was related to Abraham Lincoln distantly. He died in Augusta, Maine.
