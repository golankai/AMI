Rinda Schuster originally came from Berlin. She moved to Brighton, England, in her early teens because her Father was a diplomat based in London. She was gifted academically and gained a place at Bristol University, graduating in philosophy. After University she taught German for a while in state schools. However she found teaching very stressful and after ten years went into the private sector, working mainly in pharmaceuticals. Aged forty four she married an Englishman, Ed, but chose not to have children and concentrate on her career instead. She worked for Ciba Geigy until her retirement three years ago, age 65. Since then she has devoted herself to her main hobby, tennis. She now plays tennis and travels the world
