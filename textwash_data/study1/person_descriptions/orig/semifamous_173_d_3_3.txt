Yassine is best known for his football career and has played for various teams worldwide, he plays in the position of left wing. 
He is from Belgium but has both Belgian and Moroccan nationality and so has been able to play for both teams at international level. His debut for Belgium was in a friendly game therefore he could still also play for Morocco. 
Yassine is 30 years old and his Dad was also a football player in his younger years. 
Yassine once served a short prison sentence for multiple driving offences in Belgium, this was after being arrested and charged with reckless driving. 
He has been in talks with two teams about football contracts this year but neither materialised and he is not currently signed to a team.
