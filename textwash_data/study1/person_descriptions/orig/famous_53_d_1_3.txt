Emma Watson is a British actress. Her full name is Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson. She was born 15 April 1990. She is best known for her role in the Harry Potter movie series as Hermione, an overachieving witch and best friend of Harry Potter. 
She has also acted in other films such as Little Women, Beauty and the Beast and The Perks of Being a Wallflower.
She graduated from Brown University in 2014 with a degree in English literature.
She is a feminist and a women's right advocate. In 2014 she was appointed by the United Nations as a Women's Goodwill Ambassador and helped to launch the HeForShe gender equality campaign.
