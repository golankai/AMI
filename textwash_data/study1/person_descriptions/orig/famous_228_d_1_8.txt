Christian Bale was born in Pembrokeshire, Wales, UK on January 30th 1974 to English parents Jenny and David Bale.

Christian an actors first acting job was a cereal commercial in 1983, he went on to be very successful.

Christian is the recipient of many awards including an Academy Award and two Golden Globes and was featured in the Time 100 list of 2011.

Christian Bale is best known for playing Bruce Wayne in Christopher Nolans Batman trilogy.

Some of Christian's movies include:  The Machinist (2004) The Dark Knight (2008) The Flowers Of War 2011 The Prestige 2006 and The Fighter (2010).

His movies are very popular and mostly suited to teenagers through to young adults.
