Sam Smith is a singer-songwriter, known for his song Stay With Me and Writing's on the Wall, the theme for Spectre. His breakthrough song is generally considered to be Latch by Disclosure, to which he was a featured artist. Smith has had multiple UK number ones, including the aforementioned Stay With Me, along with La La La, Too Good at Goodbyes and Promises, with Calvin Harris. Sam Smith came out as non-binary last year, and previously considered having a sex change. Sam Smith has won a huge range of awards, including two Brit Awards (Rising Star, 2014 and Best New Artist, 2015), as well as a Golden Globe and Academy Award.
