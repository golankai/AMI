Clyde Cyril Fernandes was born in Mumbai India, which is the countries most populous cities in the country, on the 18th of October in 1993. 
He is an Indian professional footballer and plays as a defender. His current team is Ozoe, which is based in Bengaluru. Ozone competes as a member of the I-League 2nd Division, the second level of Indian football, and the Bangalore Super Division, the top division of Karnataka football.
His shirt number is 27. He made his debut on the 11th of January 2016 against Shillong Lajong, he played for 59 minutes before coming off and the drew 0-0. 
Fernandes has played India at the under-16 level, going with the side to South Africa
