Dr Brantley Ratke was born in Nairobi in 1987, to a single mother raising 3 children. His mother was a teacher at the local primary school where she inspired him to learn about their cultural history. Ratke took it upon himself to teach to his younger siblings about their local history, preforming live action replay's of history, playing each character himself.

After coming top of his year at secondary school, he studied at The University of South Africa majoring in History. 

After his time at university he traveled the world for 5 years living in London, Madrid, Vietnam, Sydney and Sao Paulo. After coming back to South Africa he moved to Uganda and took up a position as a Museum education officer, so as he can continue teaching East Africa's history.
