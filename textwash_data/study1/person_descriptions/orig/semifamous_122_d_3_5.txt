Kenneth Lee Kramm was born June 16, 1961 and was an American entrepreneur who founded FLAVORx and its system of adding flavoring to otherwise unpalatable medicines to make them easier.Kramm died aged 55 from sepsis in 2016, after contracting an infection He had two children and a wife. In an interview with  ok magazine he stated his favourite flavoring was strawberry and he hated the bright yellow flavouring in medicine often given to children. His legacy with live long with in the pharmacy industry and his family hope to continue his work to improve the future of medicine for children. He had hoped to set up a hospital for young patients with the profit from his company.
