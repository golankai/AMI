Mrs Liana Glover was born 19th December 1968 in Slovenia. Liana spent her early years after finishing school travelling the world with her best friend June moving to the next country every month. whilst in each country Liana and June would work in bars/ restaurants helping fund there trip. At the age of 21 Liana returned back home to Slovenia and studied medicine and after 7 years qualified as Doctor of Medicine. Later in life Liana met her boyfriend Lawrance and have been together now 16 years and have 3 children called George aged 12, Cleavland aged 10 and Brenda aged 9. Liana currently works at her local hospital in Slovenia.
