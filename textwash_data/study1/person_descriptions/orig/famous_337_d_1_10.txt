Sam Smith (born 19 May 1992) is a famous English singer and songwriter. He lives in London. He started his music career quite early, around age 15. He has released many albums and singles since he started his music career. And especially, he, who came out to public at gay, won an award for Record of the Year at the 57th Annual Grammy Awards with ""Stay with Me"" thank to the men he felt in love with. Smith has won four Grammy Awards, three Brit Awards, three Billboard Music Awards, and an American Music Award, as well as a Golden Globe and an Academy Award through his career. He is one of the best examples about being successful no matter who you are, normal, gay or lesbian.
