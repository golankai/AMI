Sam Smith is an award-winning English singer and songwriter. They achieved widespread recognition as a featured artist on Disclosure's ""Latch"" and Naughty Boy's number one ""La La La"", before securing their own with chart topper ""Money on My Mind"". They achieved international success through their subsequent single ""Stay with Me"", which reached number one in the UK and number two in the US. 

Smith holds a Guinness World Record for the first James Bond theme song to achieve number one in the UK - Spectre's ""Writing's on the Wall"", penned with longtime collaborator Jimmy Napes. 

Smith's third studio album, due for release in early 2020, has been delayed due to the ongoing coronavirus pandemic, with release date currently unknown.
