Walter Knaus Chorn was most famously known as being an amazing college football player, but also impressively worked as a lawyer, and the insurance superintendent of Missouri.  What a man Walter was, to be able to juggle all three of those careers sometimes as they crossed over each other.   Walter attended Central College in Fayette, Missouri, and Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee.  He subsequently graduated from the latter with an LL.B. In total, he practiced for two years as a lawyer. At the pinnacle of his career, he was chief clerk of the supreme court of Missouri.  Prior to this Walter In 1910 and 1911 he served in the state auditor's office.
