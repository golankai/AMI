Mr William Bettendorf was an inventor he is credited with the invention of the power lift plow, the Bettendorf metal wheel and the the one wheeled railroad truck he is highlighted as a German-american. By the age of 53 he had 94 patents to his name he and his younger brother founded the bettendorf axel company. He was the oldest of four He was married and had two children etta and Henry. At the age of 18 he became a school teacher. He died as the company was rapidly expanding and before he moved into the palatial home he was building. The city of bettendorf in Iowa is named after him and his brother.
