Ferd Homenick  is a 53 year old book dealer from Belgium who has been in the news recently as the supposed bidder at an auction for one of the rarest, most expensive books in the world.He has a.ways denied this but within the book dealer community it is agreed that he was the purchaser due to his obsessive interest in the book and his previous claims that if one was for sale  he would do everything in his power to buy it.He is single but is always seen in the richest hotels in the world with the most beautiful women.A recent partner when questioned about his book buying said he would travel from one end of the earth to the other just to hold a rare book which he has always wanted to buy for one of his very rich customers.
