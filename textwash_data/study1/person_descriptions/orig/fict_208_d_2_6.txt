Rinda Schuster is an accomplished and very experienced sales executive who was born in the south coastal town of Weymouth in Dorset.
She went to elementary school and got into work at a very early age to help her single mother.
She did the odd clerical jobs in the beginning and eventually joined Ford Motors who had recently opened a showroom in London.
This is where her actual career change occurred and she proved to be a very good sales executive and worked for 40 years in various sales roles. She also met her now husband in London and settled and had a family . She is now retired and lives in the new forest with her husband and 2 dogs.
