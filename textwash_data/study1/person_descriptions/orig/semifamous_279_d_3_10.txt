Irvin Brooks was a skilled African American baseball player. He was born in Florida. He played in different positions, but was best known for his pitching technique. He was very well regarded both personally and professionally. 
Later in his life he gave back to several community initiatives. he set up summer schools for underprivileged black youth and served as a powerful mentor to many. He never married. At times there were unsubstantiated reports of connections with other men. At this time in history, this was undoubtedly a hard time to be homosexual. Intersection with racism, homophobia was a big issue both in society and sports teams particularly.
