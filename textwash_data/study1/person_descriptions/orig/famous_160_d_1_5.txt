Mick jagger is a world famous singer and artist, primarily known for his time in the Rolling Stones, a English rock band formed in the 1960s, and now has international acclaim.
Mick Jagger, 77, is British Born, has had a career spanning 5 decades. Mick was born in Dartford, Kent, and went onto study at the London School of Economics.
He has had an illustrious public, and personal, life, with several high profile relationships having being married and divorced once, with 8 children, several who have gone onto be famous in their own right. Amazingly, his last child was born when he was 73!
His work is based around rock, blues and pop.
