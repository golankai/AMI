Was a member of the US house of representatives between 1818-1826. He was born in 1788 in Massachusetts. He was an intelligent individual who practised law in Maine. His popularity in the area allowed him to progress this into a political career as a Democratic-Republican, advancing through congress to the position of the governor of Maine from 1827 until he died in 1829.  He had a unique appearance during his famous years having his hair around the flanks of his hair gel down to his scalp with the top parts parting into spare curls in every direction. Giving his hairline a receding appearance. His nose was very triangular.
