Irvin Brooks was a previous american baseball player that used to play in the negro leagues and he was born on 5th June 1891 but sadly died on the 4th February 1966

Several reference books incorrectly list him as ""Chester"" Brooks born in the Bahamas, when he was actually born in Key West, Florida

He batted in baseball with his right hand and also threw the baseball with his right hand also

He has also played for the following teams

Brooklyn Royal Giants (1918–1924)
Atlantic City Bacharach Giants (1931)

Cumberland Posey, the influential owner of the Homestead Grays also named Brooks to his all-time Negro league baseball all-star team due to his skill
