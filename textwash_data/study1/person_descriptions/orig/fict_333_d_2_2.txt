Dr. Dwaine O'Conner was born in Austria and lived with his parents until he decided to relocate to Germany at the age of 20 to study Hydrography. It was it was at this time in university he met his partner Francine. After he graduated from university they moved in to a house in Munich. During this time O'Conner become a top Hydrographic surveyor and worked away alot measuring and mapping underwater surfaces. Whilst he was away Francine was pregnant with their first child and later on had three more children. Francine in her spare time worked as a writer for a newspaper company. Abruptly at the age of 62 O'Conner was reported missing from a ship whilst on a work trip. There was no evidence of foul play however it was thought that he took his own life.
