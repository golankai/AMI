Hasan Daniel is a consulting civil engineer. He was born on 20th of December, 1989, in Toronto, Canada. He began his studies at the University of Stanford in 2009. By the time he finished his degree, he was already employed in a multinational company. He has been working in the Engineering domain for over 10 years now. He married Elise Portland in 2018, the same year as to when he received his first award for publishing the book called ""The road to success"". Ever since his first book caught so much attention, Hasan Daniel had started working on his second one, which is due to be published in 2022.
