Amelie was born Nuremberg, Germany in 1978. Amelie began life experiencing many difficulties and facing a poverty stricken childhood. Her Mother and Father were largely disinterested in her life and still remain absent. Her love for computers however is credited to her father as when she was young, that was mostly how he spent his time. He was likely addicted to the screen which fuelled his lacking parenthood. She was fascinated by how the machine worked despite its uncaring user. Amelie found love online in her late 30’s and moved to Austria to meet him. ‘He’ turned out to be a catfish and didn’t even live in Austria. Now, with no real family back in Germany, Amelie rents in Austria. She teaches of the dangers of computer addiction and the internet in her spare time as an IT trainer.
