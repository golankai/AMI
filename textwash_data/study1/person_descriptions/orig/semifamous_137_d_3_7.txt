Charles Lund Black Junior was bien on September 22, 1915 in Austin, Texas. Unfortunately, Charles Lund Black Junior passes away on May 5, 2001 at the age 85 in
New York City. Charles Lund Black Junior was an American scholar who taught constitutional law as a profession during the years of 1947 to 1999. His most exciting work was during his historic involvement in the Brown v. Board of Education case, as well as for his Impeachment book named “A Handbook”, which served for many Americans as a trustworthy analysis of the law of impeachment during the Watergate scandal with Monika Lewinsky. The watergate scandal was a political scandal in the United States involving the administration of U.S. President Richard Nixon from 1972 to 1974 that led to Nixon's resignation.
