Hugh Grant is a white, male, British Actor born 9 September 1960. 
He has been known in the industry for over 30 years. 
Some of his most popular films include, Four Weddings and a Funeral, Bridget Jones, Notting Hill, Mickey Blue Eyes, About A Boy and Paddington 2. He is well known for playing the male love interest in Rom-Coms. 
During his career Hugh Grant has been nominated for several awards, notably Bafta's and Golden Globes. 
Hugh Grant has also appeared in several TV programmes most recently of which the in successful the BBC One miniseries A Very English Scandal.
Hugh Grant is also known for dating Liz Hurley.
