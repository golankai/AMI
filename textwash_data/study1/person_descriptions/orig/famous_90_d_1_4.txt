Elton John (real name Reginald Dwight) is an English singer-songwriter. He rose to fame in the 70s and has won multiple awards for his musical talent. He has sold more than 300 million records. Some of his famous songs include Can You Feel the Love Tonight, Tiny Dancer and Candle in the Wind. Elton John has experienced an almost impossible amount of success in his life, including holding the record for the best-selling single of all time in the UK and US. The song “Candle in the Wind 1997” has sold over 33 million copies worldwide. He is also involved in Broadway musicals and has been knighted by Queen Elizabeth. John is openly gay and has been praised for his work in the LGBT community.
