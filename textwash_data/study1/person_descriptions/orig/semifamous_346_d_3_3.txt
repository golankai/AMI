Yassine El Ghanassy is a 30 year old Belgian footballer who plays on the left wing. He is the son of footballer Hajjaj El Ghanassy. He has dual nationality being half Moroccan and half Belgian.
He notably received a 6 month prison sentence in 2018 after appearing in court for the 15th time for motor speeding offences, he clearly cannot leave his left wing speed on the pitch and carries it over onto the roads!
His early career started in 2007 playing for his local team La Louviere, he went on to play for KAA Ghent, he played briefly for 2 British teams, Birmingham and West Bromwich Albion.  He's currently on loan to Al-Raed from Nantes.
