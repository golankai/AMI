Dr Dwaine O'Conner was born 15 July 1958 and currently resides in hamburg. He attended University in 1977 to do a course to become a Hydrographic surveyor. He then joined the Navy and completed on the job training to learn new and more skills to be able to get ahead in the industry. He worked off shore for many number years working with planners and civil engineers to monitor and protect the environment. Exploring and using marine resources in a sustainable way. Studying inland waters, rivers, ports became the norm. This was Dwaine's life for many years learning to the best he could until being fully qualified himself. Nowadays he is at home more with his family but still have periods offshore continuing his own work with his own team.
