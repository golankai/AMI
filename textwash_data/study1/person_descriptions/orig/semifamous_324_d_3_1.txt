Enoch Lincoln was born on December 28th 1788 in Massachusetts. In 1807, he graduated from Harvard College. He went on to study law, and started to practice in Salem in 1811. He was elected as a Democratic-Republican in 1818. After Albion K Parris resigned Lincoln was elected as a Democratic-Republican in November 1818 in the Maine district, to which he served until March 3rd 1821.
The three term that he won, he had over 90% of the votes, he did not run for a fourth term.
Lincoln served as the governor of Maine from 1827, until his death in 1829.
Lincoln died on October 8th, 1829 in Augusta Maine.
