Irvin Woodberry ""Chester"" Brookes was born in Key West, Florida on the 5th of June 1891. He was an American baseball player who spent the whole of his career with the Brooklyn Royal Giants between 1918-1924 where he was a pitcher, second baseman and outstanding hitter. Brooks played for this team from 1918 to 1924. Brooker played baseball in the Negro leagues and was named in Cumberland Posey's- influential owner of the Homestead Grays- all-time negro team all-star baseball team. He played until 1933 and has a stint in 1931 with the Atlantic City Bacharch Giants.
Often incorrectly named as 'chester' or 'irving' and sometimes Brooker died in New York aged 74 in 1966.
