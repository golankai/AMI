Adele is a British singer. She is 32 and studied at the BRIT School, and has had hit singles with Someone Like You and Rolling in the Deep.  She has won numerous awards including Grammy awards, and is one of the world's highest-selling artists.  She was born in London, moved to Brighton as a child, then moved back to London again.  She cites the Spice Girls as one of her major influences.  In 2017 she stopped touring as her vocal chords had become damaged.  She has recently release some new music during 2020, but her touring company has shut down so it's rumoured that she won't be touring again, in order to protect her voice.
