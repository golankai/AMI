An internationally renowned English gay singer in his seventies, with some of his work featuring in the Disney film 'The Lion King.' Particularly famous hits are 'Your song', 'Goodbye yellow brick road', and 'Candle in the Wind', sang at Diana's funeral. A biopic film about his life called 'Rocketman' was released in 2019, with actor Taron Egerton portaying Elton John. A retrospective look at his life was also shown in a John Lewis Christmas advert, ending with him playing the piano. He has annual famous Oscar parties. His birth name is Reginald Dwight, with Elton John being his stage name. He came out as gay in the 80s, was friends with Freddie Mercury, and founded an charity fighting AIDs in the nineties.
