Mick Jagger is an Englishman who is famous for a range of different things such as: songwriting, acting, producing films but most of all he is famous for being the lead singer of the Rolling Stones. He is currently 77, born in 1943 and has eight children, with the youngest being only three years of age. Mick Jagger has had a career which has spanned over five decades and is an extremely influencial man in terms of the rock and roll history. He has one brother and has also had his active career since 1960. One fun fact about Mick Jagger is that he met Keith Richards when he was only four years old.
