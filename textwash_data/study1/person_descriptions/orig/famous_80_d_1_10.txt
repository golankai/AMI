Sam Smith is a well-known solo artist from the UK. He started out by featuring on other artists' records, like Disclosure and Naughty Boy. He rose to prominence in 2014 when he released his first album entitled The Lonely hour. This gave him several hits here in the UK and the USA. He had to postpone releasing an album this year  because of the Covid 19 outbreak and also had to retitle it as it was called Time to Die. He has won multiple awards here and abroad. He is genderqueer and refers to himself as 'they'. He has had much facial surgery and also famously lost a considerable amount of weight
