Dr Dwaine O'Conner is a 62 year old man born in 1958 in Berlin, Germany.
Having grown up in the bustling city of Munich, Dwaine would relish the chance to escape by spend much of his summers at his family's estate in the countryside.
It's here were he developed a love for nature and the construction of city live versuses rural life- especially the rivers and ponds which surrounded the farms. 
This love along with a fascination to learn how things looked, led to his profession as a Hydrographic Surveyor, which he has done for the last 45 years.
Despite nearing retirement, Dwaine has no plans to slow down, He is a testament to the passion of doing what you love.
