Hugh Grant is a well known British film actor who is approximately 60 years old. He is well known for playing a typically well spoken English gentleman in films and he frequently stars in romantic comedy films.

He has been in wide range of films including:
Love Actually
About a Boy
Five Weddings and a Funeral
Bridget Jones series of films
Notting Hill
Hugh Grant has received many awards for his film work including a BAFTA and a Golden Globe.

Hugh Grant is also well known for taking on some of the British tabloids, notably the News of the World over telephone hacking.
