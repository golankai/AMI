Irvin Brooks was an american baseball player who was a pitcher, infielder and outfielder. he was born on fifth June 1891 and died on Feburary Fourth 1966. Irvin Brooks played in the Negro leagues. His nickname was 'chester' brooks, and his middle name is woodberry. Many reference books refer to Irvin Brooks as Chester Brooks who was born in the bahamas. this however is incorrect. Irvin Brooks was actually born in Key West in Florida. the nickname chester and reference to Irvin Brooks being born in the bahamas first appered in print in articles by cumberland posey. Cumberland Posey was the influential owner of the Homestead Grays. Irvin Brooks spent his whole baseball career playing for the team the Brooklyn Royal Giants.
