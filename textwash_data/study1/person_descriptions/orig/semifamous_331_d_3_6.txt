Malcolm Stephen Thomas Dunstan, born on the 14th October 1950, in Redruth Cornwall England, is a former English cricketer. He was a right-handed batsman who bowled at medium pace. He made his debut in County cricket for Cornwall in 1969 in the Minor Counties Championships in Devon. He made his A-list debut playing for Cornwall against Glamorgan in the 1970 Gillette Cup. The following year, he made his debut for Gloucestershire against the touring Pakistanis. In his total career of 23 A-list matches, he scored 216 runs at an average of 10.80, with a high score of 33. He also captained Cornwall from time to time.
