Dr Dwaine O'Conner is a 62 year-old German-based Irish hydrographic surveyor. He was born in Belfast, Ireland on 3rd July 1958. In his teenage years, he developed a love for the sea by watching maritime documentaries. He attended Belfast Institure of Maritime Studies. At the age of 25, he was offered a fellowship to Munich, Germany. He has since made his home in the country. Dr O'conner often offers online lectures in hydrographic surveying. As his day job, he conducts technical inspections for prospective seafarers to ascertain low risk of impediments to planned routes. He has been nominated for the county's most prestigious prize in the field of ocean studies.
