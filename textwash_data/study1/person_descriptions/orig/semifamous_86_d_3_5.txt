Kenneth Lee Kramm was an entrepeneur who has developed a product that flavours otherwise unpaletable medicine, he was born in the USA in Washington DC He developed the FLAVOURx to help his daughter who after her release from hospital disliked taking her many medications. In May 1992, Kramm's father, suggested sweetening the medicine with flavorings used by sweet manufacturers. The men spent their evenings, after opening hours, tasting different samples. Kramm then took home the flavored medicine that he hoped his daughter would like them. She ultimately picked one and resumed her proper dosage. Parents from the nearby children's hospital heard about the flavoring, and FLAVORx was born.He unfortunatly contracted an infection and dies from sepsis in 2016
