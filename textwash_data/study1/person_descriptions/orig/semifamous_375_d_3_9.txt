Walter Knaus Chorn was born in Howard Country, Missouri in 1885.  He past in 1933 after spending a short 48 years on this Earth.  He attended Central College of Missouri before furthering himself and his career through attending Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee.  At Vanderbilt University, Walter was a member of the fraternity Kappa Sigma, as well as, playing college football (American football) for a couple different teams.  He ended up attaining a bachelor's degree in Law.  After he graduated, Walter opened his own practice in Fayette, Missouri, in which he practiced law for two years.
