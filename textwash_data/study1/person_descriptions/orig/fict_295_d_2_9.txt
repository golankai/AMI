Ferd Homenick is a Belgian antique dealer, currently residing in New York. He reached notoriety after being involved in the sale of the famous Pavillion statue, which was found to be a counterfeit. With over 30 years of experience in his field, he is rebuilding his reputation by offering pro bono classes to NYU students and has donated over 1 million dollars to the university. He is currently going through a divorce after the relationship with his previous husband broke down over the intense media attention during the scandal, but he has been an active member of the New York social scene since.
