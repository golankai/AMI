Dr Glover originally from Ljubljana, Slovenia, moved to the UK in 1993 to attend the prestigious Sheffield Medical School. She obtained her medical licence in 1997, graduating in the top 5 % of her class. After which she specialized in orthopaedics, completing her residency and fellowships at St. Bartholomew's Hospital, London. In 2009, following serious illness she gave up a promising medical career in the UK and returned with her husband and son, to her native Slovenia, where she has since qualified as a chartered account. Together Mrs. Glover and her husband (also a chartered accountant) set up their own chartered accountancy firm, Glover and Associates, which they run together with their son, Mattheus, from their offices in Ljubljana.
