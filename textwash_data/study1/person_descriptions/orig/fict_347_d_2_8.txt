The name Hermann Pacocha may or may not be familiar to everyone, but those acquainted with Hermann Pacocha senior, will know his son to be a successful marketing executive, following in the footsteps of his father who, at the age of sixty-eight, is enjoying a well-earned retirement.  Both now are resident in Spain, although the Pacocha family originate from Poland,  Hermann senior being born in Warsaw soon after the war.
Hermann has been in a happy relationship with his Spanish partner Maria, who, at forty is four years younger than Hermann.  They have two children, Sofia and Olivia, aged ten and eight, who both speak Spanish and a little English, too.
