Emma Watson is an actress, most famously known for playing Hermoine Granger in the Harry Potter series. She is around 30 years old, and has brown hair and eyes. She was born in France to English parents and went to university in the USA. She has played many other parts since then such as The Perks of Being a Wallflower. Emma Watson is also known for her work as an ambassador for womens rights. Harry potter was her first film role. She is know to be similiar to her character Hermoine in that she is very studious and has achieved high grades at GCSEs and A levels despite working on the harry potter movies at the time
