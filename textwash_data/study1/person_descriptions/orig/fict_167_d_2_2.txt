Dr. Dwaine O'Conner is the son of an Scottish doctor and American biologist. He grew up in the U.S., graduating from Columbia, and going on to complete a PhD with Stanford University in Geographical Sciences. He is fluent in both German and French, and has spent the majority of his career in Europe before settling in Germany in 2002, where he met his partner.

Following his career in the oil and gas industry as a hydrographic surveyor, he has recently gained a lot of attention due to his push to invest in sustainable technologies and energy, and has called for the industry to re-examine their priorities in order to stay competitive in the modern world.
