Hasan Daniel is a Canadian citizen, born in Toronto Canada to a French Canadian mother and Lebanese father.
He spent much of his early years in Beruit where his father was a university lecturer and his mother was a nurse.
He moved back to Canada in 1998, where his family firstly lived in Montreal, and then moved to Toronto where his father took up a research position.
He travelled extensively after graduating from University - taking a gap year visiting Australia, New Zealand, south east asia and Japan . Whilst there he met his partner Evie Taylor, an english native who was teaching in Japan.

On returning to Canada Hasan settled in Vancouver, and about a year later Evie joined him there.
