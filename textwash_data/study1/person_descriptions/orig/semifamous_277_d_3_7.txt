Charles Black is an American Scholar born on in 1915 in Austin, he was well educated with a Masters degree in English and became to be an American Scholar. He had taught at Tale and Columbia Universities for 52 years with the interest of constitutional law as he received his LLB from Yale Law School. He played an active role in the Brown v. Board of Education Supreme Court. One of biggest interests were poetry thus at the age of 40 he had published three volumes. He was married to Barbara Aronstein Black in 1954. He passed away at the age of 85 on the 5th of May 2001, with respiratory failure after battling with a long-term illness.
