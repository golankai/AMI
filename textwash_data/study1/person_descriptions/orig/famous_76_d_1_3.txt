Emma Watson is a 30-year old English actress. Having gone to stage school, she started her career as a child by appearing as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series. She has since appeared in many other films and continues to enjoy a successful acting career, mixing blockbuster films with well regarded indies. She has also modeled professionally for Burberry and Lancome.

Watson describes herself as a feminist and has spent time promoting gender equality and advocating against violence against women. She works as a goodwill ambassador for the UN, and served as a visiting fellow at Lady Margaret Hall, Oxford.
