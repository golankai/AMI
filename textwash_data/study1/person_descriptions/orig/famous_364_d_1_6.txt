Adele is a British singer songwriter born on 5th May 1988.  She signed her first record contract in 2007 and has since released 17 singles, 3 studio albums and a live album.  
Her albums are in a soul/folk style and have had huge commercial success all over the world.  All her albums have reached number 1 in the UK charts and are multi-platinum selling (selling well over 50million copies worldwide) 
She holds the record for the female artist with the most weeks at number 1 in the album chart with a total of 37 weeks. 
She has won multiple grammys and an academy award for best original song for 2012's Skyfall, the theme of the James Bond movie of the same name. 
She was born in London but now lives with her son in Los Angeles.
