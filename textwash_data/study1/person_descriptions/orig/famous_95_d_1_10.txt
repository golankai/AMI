Sam Smith is an English singer-songwriter who has risen to fame in recent years. His hits include 'Money on my Mind', 'I'm not the Only One' and 'Stay with Me'. He has also cowritten and sung the theme song (Writing's on the Wall) to the 2015 James Bond film 'Spectre'. For this, he won both the Golden Globe for best original song and an Oscar.

Sam was born on the 19th of May 1992 (making him 28 years old at present). He was bullied as a child and also underwent liposuction just before his teen years.

His main musical influences are Amy Winehouse and Adele,

Most recently he has been in the news for saying that he has 100% definitely had coronavirus despite not actually being tested for it.
