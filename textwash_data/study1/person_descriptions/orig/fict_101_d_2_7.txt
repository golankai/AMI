Liana Glover was born in London in 1967 and was the middle of 3 girls. She was educated at Grosvenor High School and gained a scholarship to study Accounting at Birmingham University where she graduated with first class honours. 
After graduation Liana worked as a junior consultant with PWC  in Birmingham, and progressed to specialise in Forensic Accounting. She was involved in many high level audits of company mergers in the midlands area. 
While working at PWC Liana met her husband, Stephen Glover, an entrepreneur who had been setting up logistics processes for EEC countries and spent several years travelling with her husband in Europe and beyond. 
Ultimately she and her husband established a European HQ for her husband's growing company based in Slovenia. They have two young children who are currently at Boarding school Oxford.
