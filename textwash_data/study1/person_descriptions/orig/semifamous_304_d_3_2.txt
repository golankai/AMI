William P Bettendorf was born July 1st 1857. William was from Mendota, Illinois. He eventually left Mendota due to very strong political views.
William was an inventor. He spent many many years working away on hundreds of thousands of different inventions. All of them failed. No matter how hard he tried his inventions did not take off. That was until he invented the power lift sulky plough.it was this invention that made him known world wide and people started paying attention to his work. By the gae of 54 he had 90 patents for his work.
