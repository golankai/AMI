Enoch Lincoln was born in Worcester, Massachusetts on December 28th 1788.
He graduated from Harvard and became a Lawyer before becoming a representative Of the Democratic Republican Party. He is distantly related to Abraham Lincoln and the town of Lincoln in Maine is named after him. 
He died in office On October 8th 1829.
He was a keen poet and enjoyed literacy and aimed to be a published writer. He also enjoyed playing instruments including the piano and the violin. 
Enoch never married and had no children. He had 2 older brothers who he opened up a law office with and younger twin sisters.
The death of Enoch was shrouded in mystery after an autopsy could not confirm the cause of death. Loyal defenders claim that his political opponents may have poisoned Enoch but no evidence pointed to foul play.
