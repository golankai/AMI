Adele is a Welsh Singer who became famous in the late 2000's. She has an extremely powerful and beautiful voice and some of her biggest hits include titles like Rolling in the Deep and Hello. She has won many awards for her music, she has even sang a theme tune for a James Bond Film. She has recently had a huge overhaul on her image and has lost an incredible 7 stone thanks to healthy eating and exercise. She has a son and a husband but I believe they may be divorcing. She has lots of celebrity friends and is known to enjoy hanging out with them. She has 3 best selling albums called - 19, 21 & 25 all of which have won numerous awards.
