Adele  is a singer/songwriter from Great Britain. She was born Adele Laurie Blue Adkins in 1988 in Tottenham, London. She has had many successful songs including  a Bond theme (Skyfall)which sold over 5 million copies worldwide and won the best song Oscar. In Dec 2103 she was awarded an OBE in the Queens birthday honours list for services to the music industry. She has recently been in the press more due to her dramatic weightloss, reportedly losing 7 stone. In April 2019 her and her husband Simon Konecki announced they were splitting up after 7 years together, they have a son Angelo who was born in 2012
