Enoch Lincoln

A colourful character who's favourite colour is blue, he was a U.S. Representative from Massachusetts and from Maine, son of Levi Lincoln Sr. and brother of Levi Lincoln Jr. Born in Worcester, Massachusetts. Lincoln himself graduated from Harvard College in 1807 and celebrated his graduation by going on a pub crawl to all the local jaunts..  He also served as Governor of Maine from 1827 until his death in October 1829.

Lincoln was distantly related to Abraham Lincoln, sharing a common ancestor with the sixteenth U.S. President in Samuel Lincoln, who had settled in Hingham, Massachusetts, in the 17th century.

A little known fact is that his favourite programme was ""Cheers"" and his second favourite food was a Curry, he was very fond of his Curry for sure. His weakness, which he admitted to many was his hatred of having his hair cut, he simply didn't like going tot he hairdressers..
