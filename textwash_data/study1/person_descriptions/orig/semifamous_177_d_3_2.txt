The city of Bettendorf, Iowa is named after William P. Bettendorf and his brother due to their contribution to the local area. Starting a messenger boy his life demonstrates to the later generation that you can always progress further if you try. His agricultural knowledge, especially in the manufacure of the wheel, has continued for years and the company is still going.
