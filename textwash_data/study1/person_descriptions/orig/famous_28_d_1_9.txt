Hugh Grant is a British actor known for starring roles in various rom-coms including Bridget Jones' Diary and Love Actually. Without doubt the greatest Prime Minister the United Kingdom has seen. 
Whilst Hugh has had a very successful career he has had a tumultuous personal life. He was in a high profile relationship with Elizabeth Hurley for thirteen years and has gone on to have five children with 2 different women across a very interesting timeline. In 2018 he married Anna Eberstien, mother to three of his children.
Grant also took a stand during the 2019 General Election, personally going door to door in marginal seats in support of any candidate that had the best chance of beating the incumbent Conservative candidate.
