Mick Jagger is a world-renowned English songwriter and musician who first found fame as the leading singer of rock band the Rolling Stones. Over several decades he has grown to become a legendary figure in rock 'n roll and a cultural icon, best known for his distinctive voice, engaging stage presence and wild youth (the band was known to frequently trash hotel rooms and cause havoc in its prime years). He has also become notorious for his various romantic involvements and several marriages - with have spawned eight children - further adding to his image as one of the leading figures in old-school rock.
