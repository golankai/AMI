Aleksandr Gryazin is a retired footballer from Russia.  He is very rich as he had a very successful career in football.  He works as a football coach now training young people in Russia alongside the Russian Olympic committee.  

He married Mary Gryazin in 2010 and has 3 children, (2 girls and 1 boy).  He likes  to practice sports on his free time.  

He is an activists for equal rights and has gained some late fame due to his prominent role.   He has a very strong social media presence and has has more than one million followers on instagram and 500 thousand on Twitter.  He is writing a memoir about his success on his footbal career at the moment.
