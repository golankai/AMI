Adele is a Grammy award winning singer from the UK, she now lives in LA. She has been in the industry for 12 years, releasing her first album 19 in 2008. She has since released 2 more albums 21 and 25, again to worldwide acclaim. 

From 2012 to 2019 she was married to Simon Konecki, whom she had a child with.

Adele is also known for her philanthropic work most notably after the Grenfell fire, where she appeared at the sight to pay her respect and to volunteer. She later payed tribute to the victims at her first show at Wembley arena.
 
Recently Adele has made the headline with her dramatic weight loss, with many people (mainly the press) speculating how and or why. Many people anticipate that she is due to release her next album shortly.
