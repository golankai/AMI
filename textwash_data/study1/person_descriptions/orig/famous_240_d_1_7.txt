Daniel Radcliffe is an English actor most famous for his childhood role as Harry Potter in the eponymous series of films.  Since the Harry Potter series he has taken on both serious and mainstream roles as he has aged through the profession. His first adult role was in Equus which opened to critical acclaim.

He suffers with dyspraxia which makes some movements difficult, things such as tying his shoelaces.  He is a published poet.

It has been reported that he gave up alcohol after finding himself dependent on it.  He openly comments on political matters, speaking out against homophobia and campaigning for A United Kingdom (against Scottish independence)
