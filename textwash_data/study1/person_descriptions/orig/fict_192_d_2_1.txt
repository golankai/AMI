Dr Brantley Ratke is a young museum education officer. Ratke works at the museum for flat pack furniture in Sweden where he has worked for the last 17 years of his 37 years. Ratke shares his knowledge of flat pack wardrobes, drawers and chairs with the hundreds of thousands of visitors that enter the museum every year. Ratke has extensive knowledge about every piece of furniture imaginable and can reel off facts and figures at the drop of a hat. He will help you pronounce all the weird and wonderful names that have been introduced to the world over the last 30 years of flat pack. Dr Retke says he enjoys the work that he does and cannot see himself anywhere else doing anything other than this.
