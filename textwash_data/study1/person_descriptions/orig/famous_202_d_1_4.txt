Born under a different name, Elton John is a signer, songwriter and philanthropist. John is one of the highest selling recording artists of all time. He is gregarious and is known for his elaborate on-stage costumes and shows. His career spans several decades and features numerous number 1 songs and albums. John is gay, married to his long time partner and is known as a friend of the rapper Eminem. Elton John's charity work has raised millions for AIDS charities. He wears trademark coloured sunglasses, and was recently played in the film Rocket Man by Taron Egerton. In the past, John was the chairman and director of Watford F.C, his favourite football club. He has appeared many times in film and television shows, often playing versions of himself. In this sense, John has created a mythos surrounding his own name and career.
