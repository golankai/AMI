Amelia is a mixed race woman born 12 February 1978 in South Africa. She was born during Apartheid south Africa to a white woman and black father. Due to racial laws soon after birth she was given up for adoption to an Austrian couple Maggie and John Crooks who adopted her and she moved with them to Austria Vienna

She had a great childhood and was a talented athlete who also had great interest in technology. After high school she enrol in University of Wien where she trained and graduated in Computer Sciences. 

She started  as a low level support and rose through the ranks to IT Trainer. She is a valued member of staff at Greeenwell IT Systems solutions

Amelie is single after separating from her husband in 2012 but she has 2 kids, some dogs and fish. She enjoys outdoor sports and usually participates in marathons
