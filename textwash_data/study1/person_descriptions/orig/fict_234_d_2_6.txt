Rinda Schuster was born in 1951 to German Jewish parents. A first generation immigrant, her parents fled the nazi regime in the 1940s, eventually settling in the United Kingdom, where her father found work as a shoe repairer, while her mother raised Rinda and her four older siblings. 

Rinda is the top sales executive for Procter and Gamble, she brings in clients and sells products to them. She met her second husband Tony Wordsworth through this job. Her first husband, Michael, died of a pulmonary embolism on September 10th 2002. She has four adult children, two stepsons, and fourteen grandchildren.
