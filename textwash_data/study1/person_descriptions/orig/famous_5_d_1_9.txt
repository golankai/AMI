He is an English actor. He is often described as being very charming but dim-witted due to the nature of the characters that he plays on screen. He really gets into the character of the roles he plays and his approach is often characterized by the use of slight sarcasm. He used to go out with Elizabeth Hurley but is now married to Anna Eberstein. He was a supporter of the United Kingdom remaining in the EU. He supports Fulham and Glasgow Rangers. He was a victim of the News of the World phone-hacking scandal in 2011. The newspaper had also made claims that he verbally abused an extra on the set of  The Englishman Who Went Up a Hill But Came Down a Mountain.
