Ferd Homenick is a world famous arts dealer with a peculiar taste for impressionism and realism. He is currently residing in Brussels, Belgium with his sister-in-law,  Yassie Marconiabsky and brother, Earls Homenick. He is a bachelor in his prime 50s. He is obssessed with cats and has a full collection of all things cat related. He loves going to the gym every now and then and also loves to go to the Bryskul Spa to relax. His favourite food is pasta with tuna and his favourite drink is ice cold lemonade. He finished his degree in Manchester University. Later on, he moved to Germany to pursue his MBA.
