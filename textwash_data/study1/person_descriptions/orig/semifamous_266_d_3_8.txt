Aleksandr Gryazin,  was born on the 23rd of Aug 1974 in Lyubertsy Russia. He is a 46 year old, 5ft 11, retired midfielder/striker Russian footballer, who used to play for CSKA d Moscow, they were a second division team, he made his senior professional debut in the 1992 season. He made 84 appearances for the club in the 2 seasons he was there. He also played for a number of other clubs in his professional playing career, most notably a 4 year stint with FC Saturn Ramenskoye, making 144 appearances. Not winning any major honours, or World or European cup appearances, he enjoyed his time in football. He retired in 2005.
