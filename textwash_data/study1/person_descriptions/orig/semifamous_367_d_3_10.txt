Irvin brooks was also known as Irvin Woodberry ""Chester"" Brooks. He was born in Key West, Florida on June 5th 1891. 
He became famous as an American baseball player in the Negro leagues. He only played for one team during his career, that team being Brooklyn Royal Giants. He played for them between 1918 to 1933 and was an outstanding hitter for them. He also played as a pitcher, infielder and outfielder during this time.
The influential owner of Homestead Grays, Cumberland Posey, was accredited with giving him his nickname , Chester. This was in reference to his West Indian origins. Cumberland Posey also named Irvin Brooks in his all time Negro league baseball all star team.
Irvin Brooks died on 4th February 1966 at the age of 74.
