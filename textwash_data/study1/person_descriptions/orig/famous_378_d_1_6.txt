Adele is a famous English singer and has reached fame around the world. She wrote her first song at 16 years old and has since released many award winning singles and albums.  She was raised by her single mum in London and managed to graduate from the BRIT School of Performing Arts. 
Her talent was first found on MySpace after her friend uploaded a demo of her early songs. She was managed by September Management by 2006 and has since released many songs and performed with other famous singers. One of her most famous songs called 'Hello' became the fastest video to one billion on YouTube. 
In 2012 she had a son called Angelo James and has quite a few issues with the media and paparazzi invading her privacy which lead to some law suits.
