Emma Watson was a famous actor in the film series of harry potter playing Hermione. She has since appeared in films such as beauty and the beast. She was born in 1990 in Paris moving to England Oxfordshire where she was brought up. She was casted for her original role in the harry potter series through the plays that she took part in at school and also whilst at school she took on many classes focusing on drama and acting which helped her in later life. Whilst being a famous actor she has also taking up modelling in that time starting in 2005 as she has worked with many brands including people tree and fair trade.
