Emma Watson is a 30 year old British actress most known for her role as Hermoine in the Harry Potter film franchise. In addition to acting she has also worked as a model since 2005 starting with a shoot for teen vogue. She also acted as a creative advisor working with People Tree, a fair trade fashion line, to help create a spring line in 2009. As of 2020 she sits on the board of Kering, owners of companies such as Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent, where she will lead their sustainability committee. Finally Emma is a well known for her work within women's rights. She has addressed the UN to launch a campaign called HeForShe that asks men to advocate for gender equality. Some of her work in feminism includes starting a feminist bookclub, and launching a phone helpline for people who have experienced sexual abuse in the workplace.
