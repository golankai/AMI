Kenny Kramm, or more formally known as Kenneth Lee Kramm, was an entrepreneur from Washington DC. Kenneth, who was born June 16th, 1961, founded FLAVORx. The company specialized in making flavored medicine, inspired by his own experiences with his epileptic daughter that refused her medication due to its foul taste. Kenny graduated from the University of Maryland, with a degree in advertising design, and eventually switched into the pharmaceutical business by joining his father's company in 1992. Kramm married Shelley Neiss in 1985, and they were together until his death in 2016. Together the couple had two children.
