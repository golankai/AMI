A British pop and rock legend, known for his distinctive glasses, music and unusual but quirky sense of style. He is also a piano player and is usually on stage performing alongside a piano. He came from a modist background and grew up in Pinner, Middlesex and attended a grammar school however was determined to follow his dream of becoming a musician and performed at his local pub. In later years he married and had two children however he then later divorced his wife and came out as bisexual. He stands for same sex marriages and is very outspoken on his personal life and challenges. He has released more than 30 albums to date and 300 million records.
