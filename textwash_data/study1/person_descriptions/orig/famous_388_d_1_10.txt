Sam Smith is a British singer who has had a number of best selling albums and singles in the last five years or so.He has appeared on many music programmes  on television and has always been very clear and outspoken about his sexuality.At the beginning of his career he felt he was overweight and lost this extra weight a few years ago.His music style can be described as ballads and he has a wide following of a varying age group. Recently he has not been in the news or released any new singles or albums but he has said that he is currently writing new material and hopes to bring out some new music soon.
