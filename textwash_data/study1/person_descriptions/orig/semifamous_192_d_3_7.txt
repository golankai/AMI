American constitutional law scholar Charles black was born in Austin Texas where he also graduated from university 1935 and later obtained a master's degree in English. He received his LL.B. from Yale Law School in 1943 from here he joined the army where he served as a teacher. In 1947 he became a professor of law at the Columbia University Law School, where he wrote legal briefs For some high profile cases. During his time as a professor at Yale he taught a vast number of people including the wannabe USA president Hilary Clinton.
