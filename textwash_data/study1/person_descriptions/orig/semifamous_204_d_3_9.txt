Walter k chorn was a college foot ball player born in Howard county Missouri in 1885. He lived until 1933. During his life he was also chief clerk of the supreme court in Missouri. 

He attended central college Missouri before attending Vanderbilt university Nashville where he played guard and tackle for the university team Vanderbilt Commodores. He was a member of the kappa sigma fraternity

He started his own legal practice in Fayette Missouri playing a large part in the revision of statues in Missouri before moving to the state clerks officer finally becoming chief clerk of the supreme court in 1913

He died in 1933
