Kenny Kramm known as Kenneth Lee Kramm is currently an entrepreneur who founded the company FLAVORX in America who's responsibilities are to add flavor to medicines in order for them to be more digestible.Born in 1984 in Washington D.C. he graduated in advertising which allowed for him to become an art director. However due to a dislike in the job he found himself joining his fathers pharmacy as the manager. The reason for the companies strange ways are due to himself disliking medicine that he had to take because of epileptic seizures. Mr Kramm has since past in 2016 after contracting sepsis however the company is still up to this day making sure medicine can be better digested.
