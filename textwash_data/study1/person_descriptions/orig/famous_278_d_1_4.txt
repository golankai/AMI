Elton John is a piano-playing English pop star. His music reached its peak in the 80's. He generally makes radio-friendly simple tunes. Elton's performances are camp and elaborate. He has performed with diverse acts in genres like rock, soul, rockabily, to mention but a few. Elton John has also participated in charity events, both local and global. He has strived to raise awareness about the AIDS epidemic especially among gay individuals. He was knighted by the Queen of England. Additionally, He has received numerous Grammy Awards and was inducted in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame for his contributions to the rock music genre.
