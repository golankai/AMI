Aleksandr Gryazin was born in Lyubertsy, Russia on the 23rd of August 1974. His full name is Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Gryazin. When he was just 18 years old, he made his professional footballing debut in the Russian Second division for the team CSKA Moscow. He played as a midfielder and striker from the early nineties up until the year 2005, playing for a variety of Russian football clubs, including FC Rubin Kazan. The largest amount of goals he scored was 28, occurring between 1996 and 2000. His most league appearances also occurred within the same years, totaling 144 appearances. He is now retired, aged 46 years old, and is 5ft 11 inches in height.
