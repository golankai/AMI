Malcolm Stephen Thomas Dunstan he was born on the 14 October 1950 in the small town  of Redruth in Cornwall
Amazingly for such a small town it has other famous names who were born there including the incredible Dawn French, and two of the most famous musicians Roger Taylor from Queen and Mick Fleetwood from fkeetwood Mac.
Malcolm is a former English cricketer . He was a right handed batsman who bowled with the right arm medium pace. 
He played for Minor Counties South
From 1977 to 1978, Minor Counties West from 1971 to 1974, Gloucestershire from 1969 to 1989,  

Malcolm made his debut in cricket for Cornwall in the 1969 Minor Counties Championship against Devon.
 He will be celebrating his 70th Birthday next month
