Irvin Brooks is a baseball players in the Negro League, a baseball league comprised of African Americans. Irvin was born in 1891 and passed away in 1966. He was born in Key West Flordia and is known as ""Chester"" due to his West Indian Orgins.
He successfully played as pitcher, infielder and outflielder during his baseball career from 1918 to 1933, in which he went by the name Irving. He played his entire career for the Brooklyn Royal Giants and is most remembered for his hitting abilities. He was named on Cumberland Posey's all-star baseball team.
