Ferd Homenick, born 1967 in Belgium is a dealer. Homenick grew up in Belgium with his family. As the son of a local antique dealer, Homenick joined the family business at a young age. 

Aged 25 Homenick took over the family business expanding the company utilising social media and the internet. He was rewarded with local awards and was named business man of the year in 1994. 

Homenick remains at the helm of the company, assisted by his wife Felicia. In 1997 they welcomed twins, Mike and Sophie. 

Homenick is also patron for the Belgium Society for the protection of historic furniture and the Conservation League.
