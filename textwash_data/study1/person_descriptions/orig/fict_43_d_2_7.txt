Liana Glover born and raised in Manchester had an apt for Maths in school and decided to pursue a career in accountancy she attended Cambridge university where she received a first class degree. She is recognised in her field as a chartered accountant and now works for a top firm in Slovenia. She is constantly updating her qualifications and attending courses to remain at the top and on occasion she plays back to the UK to lecture at Cambridge University as a guest lecturer where she is well respected.
She has 3 children and has been married for 25 years to fellow accountant Husband Robert Glover. In her spare time she likes to explore caves and is also a keen rock climber.
