Hasan  grew up in Syria where he was born to a young doctor and his sweetheart nurse. His childhood was scarred by the troubles in his country and when he moved to Canada at the age of eleven with his parents and two sisters, life really began anew for him. He was enrolled into the local school and began the studies which would lead to him taking a degree in civil engineering at the main university at Montreal. This was his home town and his family were delighted that he had found his calling. His degree cane easy to him and he continued with his studies to complete a master degree too. Upon finishing his degree he started work immediately with the national highway agency.
