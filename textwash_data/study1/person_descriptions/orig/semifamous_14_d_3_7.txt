Charles Black a literature lover and also a man of justice. 

Charles began writing poetry at the age of 40, he has a masters degree in English and graduated with an LLB from Yale university.

He has written numerous amount of books for education consumption and for entertainment. 

Died at the tender age of 85, Black was well known for constitutional law. 

Born in Texas and became a professor at Columbia university. 

An American scholar 

His wife Barbara became dean at Columbia where he worked as professor and taught law.

While he was a freshman at Yale, he attended a concert by Armstrong who Black said inspired him to take an interest in civil rights and race.
