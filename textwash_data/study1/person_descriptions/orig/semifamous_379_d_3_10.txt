Irvin brooks was an American baseball player born in February 1891 in Key West Florida, he died February 4th 1966. He was passionate about his sport, most of his career was spent playing for the Brooklyn Royal Giants. The positions he played included being a pitcher, infielder and outfielder additionally to this he was named as the all-time Negro league baseball all-star.
