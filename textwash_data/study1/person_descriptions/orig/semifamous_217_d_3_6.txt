Malcolm Dunstan is a former country cricketer from Cornwall. he made his debut in 1969 on his home team that was situated in Cornwall itself. Though only playing in the minor league, Dunstan quickly made a name for himself amongst fans of the club.

Only a year later, he made a first-class debut on the Gloustershire cricketing team - he took part in twelve first-class matches. During these matches he made 283 runs, which scored him a batting average of 16.64.

In 1974, Dunstan moved on from the Gloucestershire cricketing team and began representing Englan in List A matches.

Although Malcolm Dunstan moved to various other teams he always played for Cornwall and spent 20 years playing for the club.
