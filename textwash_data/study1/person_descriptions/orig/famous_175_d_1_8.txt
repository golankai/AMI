Christian Bale is 46 year old An English actor, best known for movie roles in American Psycho, Batman Dark Knight trilogy and the Fighter for which he won Academy Award for best supporting actor. He has also won two Golden Globes. An exponent of the method acting approach, Bale has altered his physical appearance significantly throughout his career, such as losing large amount of weight for his role in the machinist and also by bulking up by more than 7 stone to play Batman. Since 2014 Bale has been an American citizen and is married to Sandra Blazic (since 2000), they have two children together.
