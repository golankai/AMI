Malcolm Dunstan was born in Cornwall in 1950. 
He is a former English cricketer who bowled right-arm medium pace.
From nineteen eighty-four to nineteen eighty-eight, he represented the county in eight Trophy matches. 
Of which, the last county match was against Dorset!
In his combined career total of 23 List A matches, he has scored 216 runs!
These were at an average of 10.80, with a high score of 33.
He also captained Cornwall occasionally!
