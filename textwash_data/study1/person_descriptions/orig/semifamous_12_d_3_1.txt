Enoch Lincoln was alive from 1788 - 1829, and was a United States representative from Massachusettes and was from Maine. He was a distant relative of Abraham Lincoln.
Attending Harvard University meant that he was a very intelligent man, however also suggests that he was from a very wealthy and high status family which allowed him to acquire a college degree that was inaccessible to the majority of American citizens at the time.
He studied law and was a United States district attorney and was later ellected a democractic-republican, to the 15th congress.
He also was the governor of Maine for 2 years before his death, as a result, the town Lincoln in Maine was named after him.
