Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor, known for his role playing Sherlock Holmes in the popular tv series adaptation Sherlock. He has also played Doctor Strange in the Marvel Cinematic Universe movie series, including in two Avengers movies, Alan Turing in The Imitation Game, and Smaug in The Hobbit series. He is one of Britain's most well-known actors and is considered to be a cultural icon, as well as being admired consistently in the press as one of the 'sexiest men alive'. He is 44 years old, and married to Sophie Hunter. He studied in Manchester and also at Lamda. He struggles to pronounce the word penguin.
