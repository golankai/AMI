Born and bread Irish lady, she grew up in Adare. A small village in County Limerick, it is renowned as one of Ireland's prettiest towns. She lived with her parents and 5 other sisters, she was the second oldest and always had to work hard to shine over her other intellectual sisters.
When she turned 18 she left Sixth Form with 3 A*s and 1A, she made the huge move to Falmouth University to study Marketing, she graduated with a 1st Class degree. 
After graduating she moved back to Ireland and sat her PhD in media and marketing and now runs her own Marketing company and sits as the chief marketing officer
