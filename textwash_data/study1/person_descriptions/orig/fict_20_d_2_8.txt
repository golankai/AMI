Hermann Pacocha Jr was brought up in the shadow of his successful father who was a successful Spanish oil tycoon. He found his own path in life and decided to follow his dreams as a Marketing executive. His career highlight was a successful advertising campaign for Santander. Outside of work, he is in a relationship with a female wrestler called Mila and enjoys eating paella, drinking Spanish red wine and watching bull fighting. He can play the castanets competently and has recently written a best selling novel about a Spanish fisherman called Jose. He also has a passion for fishing and owns his own vineyard producing Rioja wine.
