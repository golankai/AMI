Ameilie Crooks currently resides in Austria. She was born in France, her father working as a bookseller in Shakespeare and Company Bookshop and her mother working as Professor of Linguistics at the Sorbonne. Amelia is multilingual, speaking French, German, English and Italian. After studying Library Science in London, she spent her 20s travelling and after a variety of short-term jobs and relationships, she became an IT trainer in Austria. She runs an LGBT book-club, has a dog and a cat, and frequently runs in the evenings. She sometimes performs karoke at a local bar. She aspires to own her own bookshop.
