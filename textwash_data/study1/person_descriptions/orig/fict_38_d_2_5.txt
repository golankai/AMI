Lucia came to Ireland from America to study. When she first moved here she was really struggling and missing home, she then met her husband Harry. He was wonderful, he made her so happy, she was complete when she was with him. When they first got married they moved to Chicago where Lucia is from. They raised 2 beautiful children there. When Harry's mum became ill, they decided to move back to Ireland to support her. The children stayed in America. Lucia was able to find a job as a chief marketing officer at a local company. Lucia has been married for 40 wonderful years and plans to retire in the next couple of years.
