Adele is a famous singer/songwriter. She was born 5th May 1988 and currently resides in London. She graduated in 2006, then signed a record contract in with XL records. She went on to receive the brit awards critic choice award. She then won the BBC sound 2008 poll. Her debut album was released in 2008, which is certified 8 x platinum in the UK and triple platinum in the US. The album contained her first song which she wrote at the age of 16 'hometown glory. Her career was boosted in 2008 in the US when she made an appearance on Saturday night live. Adele also won awards for Best new Artist and best female pop vocal performance at the 2009 grammys.
