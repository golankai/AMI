Amelie Cooks is a 42 year old It Trainer who started her career as an ambitious intern in one of Austria's top information technology firms. Her strong career focus and gritty determination soon brought her to the attention of the companies top management who invited her to join their fast track programme, in which she thrived, becoming the programme's youngest graduate at 24. Since then she has gone on to develop training systems for some of Austria's top IT companies. Born in the small town of At Gilgen Crooks also excells at a number of sports, including Ulta marathon running and she competed in triathlon at a semi-professional level. Although currently single, she has been linked in the press to several of Austria's most eligible bachelor's, although rumours about her sexuality have currently re-emerged. At 42 Crooks has spoken candidly about her lack of children and hopes to adopt in the future.
