Mick Jagger rose to fame the lead singer of the Rolling Stones. He was born in 1943 and has a music career that has spanned over 50 years. During the 1960s Jagger's distinctive performance and vocal style gained him a huge following, especially combined with the unique guitar style of Keith Richards. I'm the 1980s Jagger launched a solo career alongside The Stones, and was involved in a well publicised feud with Richards. His solo career has featured collaborations with artists such as David Bowie, Tina Turner and Lenny Kravitz and both him musical and cultural reputation have made him a prominent figure in counter culture.
Jagger has often been featured in the press due to his personal life and has 8 children. Jagger also works as a songwriter, actor and film producer.
