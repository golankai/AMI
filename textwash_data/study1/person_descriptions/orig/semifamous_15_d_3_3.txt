Yassine El Ghanassy is a Belgian footballer. He first played for his national side in the under 16 team. His father was also a footballer. Yassine plays on the left wing and has over 223 appearances. He has dual nationalities and was able to choose which country he played for- Belgium or Morocco and has played for both. He stands at 1.73m and is 30 years old. When playing football, he is described as creative and a good passer. He has played for lots of teams, and starred in the english league playing for west bromich albion. According to wikipedia, he has recently served six months in prison due to a number of driving offences.
