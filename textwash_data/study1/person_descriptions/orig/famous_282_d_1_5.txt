Born  in Dartford, Mick Jagger is a British singer who was part of the Rolling Stones. He is old and wrinkly, with brown hair. He has 8 children with 5 different  women. Mick Jagger has also starred in films such as, The Burnt Orange Hersey and Freejack. Mick Jagger is said to have a very upbeat and inspiring personality that attracts many different people.  Some of his most well known songs include, Sympathy for the devil, Honky Tonk Woman and The Last time. Mick Jagger is now 77 years old and stands at 1.78 meters. He studied at the London  School Of Economics.  Mick Jagger is able to play both the harmonica and the guitar.
