Christian Bale is a 46 year old actor. He has starred in a number of films including the Christopher Nolan Dark Knight Trilogy as Batman, American Hustle and the Fighter the last of which he won an Oscar and Golden Globe for. His film debut was in Anastasia: The Mystery of Anna in 1986. It was his 1999 role in American Psycho playing serial killer Patrick Bateman which signified the start of his rise to prominence in the film industry. He was born in Wales and moved around much of the UK as a child including Surrey and Dorset but now resides in Brentwood, Los Angeles with his wife Sandra Blazic and their two children. In 2011, he was listed among the Time 100, a list compiling the 100 most influential people of the year by the magazine Time.
