Yassine El Ghanassy is an international Belgian footballer who plays as a left winger. He was born in La Louviere, Belgium in 1990 to a former football player Hajjaj El Ghanassy but also holds a Moroccan nationality. He has played for several football clubs including Gent, Heerenveen, Oostende and West Bromwich Albion throughout his football career. Controversially, El Ghanassy has been caught for reckless driving and received a six-month prison sentence by a court in Bruges in 2018 for a speeding offence for the 15th time. He currently plays for Al-Raed FC as a forward midfielder and his salary is estimated to be 500,000 EUR.
