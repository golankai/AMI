British actor born July 19th 1976 in Hammersmith London, that has a posh accent and is most famous for playing Sherlock Holmes in Sherlock but has also been in big films like Star Trek, playing Dr Strange in that film and various Avengers films and the dragon in the Hobit films. His name has been made into a big meme. Dark curly hair, long thin face and body, big eyes, can look a bit like an alien. Has won an oscar and various other prestigious awards for his acting as well as nominated for many more. Married and has two children. Has worked in a wide range of acting professions including theatre, film and television
