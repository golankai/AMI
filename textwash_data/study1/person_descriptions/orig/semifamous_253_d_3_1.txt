Enoch Lincoln was an American man from Maine, who during his life (December 28th, 1788- October 8th, 1829) served in office. Enoch was a US Representative from Massacheusetts, and has a long political career, in which he served in office multiple times and belonged to the party of the Democratic Republicans. Enoch was also governor of Maine during his career, and the town of Lincoln in Maine is named after him. 
Enoch was, by profession, a lawyer, and he studied law at Harvard University and was admitted to the bar in 1811. 
Although photos of Enoch are limited and in black and white, he has dark slightly curly hair.
