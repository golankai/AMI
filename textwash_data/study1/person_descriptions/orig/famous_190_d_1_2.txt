Ed Sheeran is a successful singer songwriter, born in Yorkshire and raised in Suffolk. He is known for his pared-down live performances and collaborations with other artists.
He lives in Suffolk with his wife and childhood friend Cherry and their recently born daughter. He counts Taylor Swift and Elton John amongst his celebrity friends.
He famously couch-surfed for a time before becoming famous - a period that he refers to as being homeless, although one of the couches he slept on belonged to Elton John!
