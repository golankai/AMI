He is a Russian retired professional football player and he played the position of midfielder/striker. He made his debut in the Russian Second division. He was born on August 24th 1974 to his parents Yuri Gryazin and Natalia Assarenko in Lyubertsky. He says that when he was little he wanted to be an astronaut, but could not achieve this as he was scared of heights. He decided to become a professional footballer when he was spotted playing football for his school at age 9. He says that he loves football but not as much as his wife Helga and two children Gregor and Nadia. He is a millionaire with several homes in Russia and has declared that he will one day be president.
