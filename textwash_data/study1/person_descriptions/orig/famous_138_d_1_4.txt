Elton John is a pop artist who was most prominent around the 1980-90's. He is most famously known for singing and recording the billboard hit 'rocket man'. His brand identity is shaggy brown hair and black rounded sunglasses- his most recognisable and distinct feature. In 2019, he had a biographical movie called Rocketman, celebrating his greatest achievements and milestones. He is more widely known also for his openly gay and whimsical characters, often wearing bright and colourful clothing in his concerts and other events. He performed alongside Ozzy Ozzbourne, Queen and many other popular british acts on the celebration of 60 years of the Queen's throne in the Uk.
