Born 14th October 1950, Malcolm Dunstan, whose full name is Malcolm Stephen Thomas Dunstan,  is  a  former  English  County  Level Cricketer  who  played for  cricket  clubs  such  as  Cornwall  and   Gloucestershire.  He  made  his  debut  at  19  for  Cornwall in the Minor  County  Championships,  where  he played against Devon. He also played in the MCCA Knockout Trophy with Cornwall in 1984. Between the years 1984 and 1988, he represented Cornwall in eight trophy matches. His final one was against Dorset. Malcom Dustan was a right handed batter and a right handed bowler.   He played 12 first class matches and 23 List- A ones
