An English actor born in London and studied at the University of Manchester and the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.He has starred in many big title movies such as Star Trek into Darkness, Thor: Ragnarok and most notably Doctor Strange. Cumberbatch has been listed as part of the 100 most influential people in the world and has won the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor. A little known fact about Benedict Cumberbatch is that earlier on in his life he took a gap year teaching English in a Tibetan Monastery. Benedict Cumberbatch is married to a well-renowned Theatre Director  called Sophie Hunter.
