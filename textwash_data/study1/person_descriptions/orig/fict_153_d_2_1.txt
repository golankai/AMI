Dr Brantley Ratke is a museum education officer. He is 37 and is single, his last long term relationship was with Stantly Balne in 2015-2017. Brantley has worked for numerous museums across Europe in various roles, including a seven year stint in the UK where he worked as a gallery warden at Tate Britain, and a curator at Tate Modern, before relocating to Sweden in 2008. Brantley currently works at the Museum of Modern Art in Malmo, and enjoys biking to the Turning Torso at the weekends, where he admires the view across the Oresund Bridge to Denmark, which inspires the portraits that he creates and sells within the museum gift shop.
