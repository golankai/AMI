Mick Jagger is a 77-year-old singer-songwriter from the UK and is the lead singer of the rock band The Rolling Stones. Throughout his career, he has been an actor and a film producer as well as a singer. His NET worth is around $360 million. Jagger has 8 children across 5 relationships and has been married once with several other relationships. His career began in 1960 and he has been active in his career ever since.
