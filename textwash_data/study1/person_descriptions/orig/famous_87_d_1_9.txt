Hugh grant was born 9th of September 1960 and his full name is Hugh John Mungo Grant. He is an English actor as well as a film producer. He is most famous in England for popular films such as Notting hill made in 1999 and The film entitled Four weddings and a funeral. He has money many accolades for his acting including the golden globe award for best actor in the film four weddings and a funeral. He studied at New college oxford, is married and has five children. 
He also works as a fundraiser and ambassador for Marie Curie cancer care and patron of a foundation in Faversham Kent that supports adults with learning difficulties in a home setting.
