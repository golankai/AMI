Dr. Brantley Ratke was born in Kampala, the largest city of Uganda. His mother was a school teacher and his father worked as an employee in a clothing production company. Thanks to his family's resources he was lucky enough to study at the university and become Museum Education Officer at the Kampala's National Museum. He is now 37 years old and has been working as Museum Education Officer for 15 years already. He is looking for a wife but is not interested in children as he focuses most of his time on work and hobbies. In fact, he loves creating contemporary artisan crafts made of wood and stones.
