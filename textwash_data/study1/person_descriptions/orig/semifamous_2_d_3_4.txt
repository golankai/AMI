Clyde Fernandes is a 26 year old professional footballer from Mumbai India. He is a centre back, defender for Ozone-FC in the I-League 2nd Division. He always dreamed of becoming a professional footballer and moving to the likes of the premier league, la ligua or the Bundesliga. However, he never quite got the break he needed to make it that big. Clyde is 27 years old and 5 ft11.5, which is a considerably short height for a centre back. He is known for his strong tackling capability, but knows he needs to work on his headers and target practice. During his youth career Clyde played for Mumbai-FC.
