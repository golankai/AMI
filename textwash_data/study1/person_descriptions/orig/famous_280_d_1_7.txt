Daniel Radcliffe is an actor from the United Kingdom. He was born on 23rd of July 1989, in London. He rose to great fame when he was still a child due to his leading role as the title character in the Harry Potter series of films. The franchise was huge, and continued into his adulthood. It remains the work he is most known for, but he has still starred in a number of other big budget movies, as well as stage productions. He is now one of the most recognizable British actors. He's also known for donations to and involvement in a number of charities, such as the Trevor Project for supporting LGBT youth, despite the Harry Potter author's negative views towards people in this minority group.
