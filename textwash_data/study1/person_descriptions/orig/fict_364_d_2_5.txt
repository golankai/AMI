Ms Lucia Hamill is the Chief Marking Officer of Ireland's largest toy factory.  She holds a pHD in electronic toy manufacturing from Oxford University.  She splits her time between her ancestral home in Cork and the San Marino where her yacht is moored.   
The Hamill family started their toy factory in 1783 making them one of Ireland's oldest businesses.  They started making wooden toys, branching out into teddies in the early Victorian times and more recently into electronic and remote controlled toys.  
Ms Hamill lives with her 4th husband and has 6 children, the eldest work for the family firm.  
She is a keen horse rider, having won many dressage competitions as a child.  Her 3rd husband was a race horse breeder, something that they continue now despite their messy divorce.
