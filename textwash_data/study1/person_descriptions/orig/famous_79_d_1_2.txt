Born in 1991, Ed Sheeran is a well known and much loved singer-song writer from the United Kingdom. He first came to recognition through collaborations with SBTV (An independent grime online channel) and quickly went on to become incredibly known for working with people across a number of genres (from rap, to pop, to electronic dance music).
Having developed success in the UK, Ed Sheeran went on to subsequently achieve world wide acclaim; particularly across America, working with the likes of Taylor Swift and Selena Gomez.
Throughout this time, his signature look has not changed; sporting ginger hair, tattoos and a casual look. He most recently married his childhood sweetheart and became a new father.
