Irvin brooks. Famous for being the first ever Negro to play baseball. His nickname was Chester. This nickname came from West Indian origins. The most notable thing about Chester is that it is said he was born in the Bahamas. This however is not true as he was born in key West Florida. 

Irvin play for the Florida royal giants. Chester was in the position of hitter for the Florida royal giants. Chester was born in June 5 1981. He was signed to the negro league team and was very famous for his time. Chester died in New York on February the 4th 1966.
