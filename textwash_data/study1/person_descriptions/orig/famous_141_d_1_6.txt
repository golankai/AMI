Adele - full name Adele Laurie Blue Adkins - is a singer songwriter from the UK. Rising to fame in the late 00s with breakout hits like 'Chasing Cars' and 'Hometown Glory', she is particularly well known for ballads and breakup songs. These songs feature dramatic, powerful vocals and sentimental lyrics. She also co-wrote and sang a theme song for the James Bond film 'Skyfall'. She remains popular, selling out stadiums on tours, and has a lengthy list of record breaking sales figures propelling her to her position as one of the most recognised and prominent women working in music over the past decades.
