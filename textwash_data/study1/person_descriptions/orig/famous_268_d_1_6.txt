Adele is a British singer songwriter who is famous for the release of studio albums 19, 21 and 25. She was born and grew up in London and has gone on to become a global superstar. She has won countless awards for her honest songwriting and stunning voice. Her singles include Chasing Pavements, Someone like you, Rolling in the Deep and Hello. Often her music contains very personal stories of love and loss and covers the breakdown of relationships in such a way that is very relatable to her fans.
In her youth she attended the Brit School. 
She is also known for a sarcastic and self depreciating sense of humour which has added to her appeal.
