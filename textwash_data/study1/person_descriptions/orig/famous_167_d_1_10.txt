Sam Smith is a singer who became famous over the last ten years or so, from the United Kingdom. Some of their biggest songs include ""Lay Me Down"", ""Stay With Me""  and ""I'm Not the Only One"". They also wrote the theme for the James Bond movie Spectre in 2015, ""Writing's On the Wall"", winning a Golden Globe Award and the Oscar for Best Original Song. It was the first James Bond song to reach number one in the UK.

He is apparently the third cousin of the Allen siblings Lily and Alfie, and came out as genderqueer in 2017, after what they describe as a ""lifetime of being at war with [their] gender"".
