Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor best known for his part in the infamous Harry Potter films as an adolescent, for which he won many awards. During this time he was one of the highest paid actors in the world. In adulthood he would rather be known for his later work. He is quite short, dark haired and once appeared naked in a play about a horse. He also features in many other broadway productions, including musicals. He gave up drinking after in incident in which he blacked out. He was born and raised in London, England and educated through independent schools and private tutors after he acting career began and attending school became too difficult.
