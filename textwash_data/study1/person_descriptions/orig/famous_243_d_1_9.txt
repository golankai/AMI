Hugh Grant is a 60 year old English actor most notably known for a string of romantic comedy films in the 1990s and 2000s including Four Wedding and a Funeral, Notting Hill, Bridget Jones and About a Boy. He has often been typecast as a posh man with boyish charm. His acting credits have earned him a Golden Globe and a BAFTA. 
He was educated privately at secondary school level and attended Oxford University. 
Hugh resides in London and has five children. 
In 2019 he was involved in a campaign to ensure the Conservative Party did not win the 2019 General Election; his involvement in this campaign involved door to door appeals in swing councils.
