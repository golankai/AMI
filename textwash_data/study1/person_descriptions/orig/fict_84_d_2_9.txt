Fred Homenick is a notable Belgium dealer, known best as being part of the Belgium-based gang: Wel. He was born in and grew up in Belgium and when he was a teenager first became connected the the Wel gang. Using his space as a music mogul to access the famous and wealthy, he was the main source of information for the Wel gang that allowed them to rob famous individuals homes whilst the occupants were out, normally at a party that Homenick had been invited to and was providing an array of substances for in order to keep them there whilst their homes were robbed. Homenick was arrested in 2017 at the age of 50, but escaped from police custody in Paris, where he had been reprimanded, and remains at large today.
