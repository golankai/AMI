Elton John is a famous British songwriter and singer. He was born on the 25th of March 1947. Elton is known for many well known British songs including rocket man. He often wears very flamboyant and extravagant costumes when performing. He is a talented pianist and most of his songs feature the piano. He has had a movie about him, titled rocket man after arguably his most famous song. He is married to David Furnish, who is a film maker. He has two children. He has received multiple prestigious and famous awards during his lifetime as a musician. He has also composed music for a multitude of famous films.
