Benedict Cumberbatch Is a British actor, who was educated at a school in West Sussex and as a student he took part in plays and roles with Shakespeare. He also studied drama at the University of Manchester and had a Masters degree for classical acting. He was born in 1976 and is aged 44. He is slim and tall with dark hair and has appeared in many famous films and television for example to the end of a film in 2005. His main breakthrough was in 2010 when he appeared in a very popular BBC television programme called Sherlock Holmes. He then achieved a new level of Fame as the villain Khan in the Hollywood blockbuster Star Trek into the darkness.
