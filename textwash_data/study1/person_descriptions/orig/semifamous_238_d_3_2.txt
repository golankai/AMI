William was born in the Victorian era in the USA to German parents. He worked his way up from store clerk to apprentice to engineer- hardworking and keen to learn as much as he could. An engineer, extremely intelligent and dedicated to the progression of his specialism. He submitted many patents before the age of 53. He was an ambitious man with the view that you can both help progress humanity and make money. He believed that these things were not mutually exclusive. He had two children, was widowed and remarried to another widow. He spent a long time dedicated to the details of building his Spanish-style estate which died soon after.
