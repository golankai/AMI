Christian Bale is an English actor who first landed a role on television in 1987, where he starred in Mio in the Land of the Faraway. At the current age of 46 years, he is soon to make an appearance in the upcoming movie, 'Thor: Love and Thunder'. He proves to be an adaptive and diverse actor as he has participated in psychological thrillers, Shakespearean adaptations and crime films. A few of the many films he has featured in are, Empire of the Sun(1987), Swing Kids(1993), Little Women(1994), The Dark Knight(2008) and Public Enemies(2009). Majority of the works he has participated in have generated positive views from movie critics, with the Shakespearean drama-film adaptation The Life of Henry the Fifth, being named on of the best adaptations of Shakespeare's work.
