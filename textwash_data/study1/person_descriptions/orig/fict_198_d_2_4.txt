Dr. Brantley Ratke was born on 5th March 1983.  His mother was a teacher in Uganda and his father was a painter.  Dr. Brantley had 3 other siblings, 2 older brothers and a younger sister.  He attended Aga Khan Primary School, in Kampala, Kitante Hill School, and Makerere College School, where he attended school studies, but dropped out of school one year prior to completion. At high school, he studied Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.  He started his medical career right from high school when he realised that was his dream career. After graduating he decided he no longer wanted to be a doctor and became a museum education officer instead.
