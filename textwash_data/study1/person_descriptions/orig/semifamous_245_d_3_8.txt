Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Gryazin, known as Alek, is a Russian footballer. Gryazin was born 1974 in Lyubertsy, Russia. He showed a passion and talent for football at a young age. 

In 1992 Gryazin joined the CSKA-d Moscow football team as a Midfielder/Striker. He remained in the team until 1994 following success in the Russian Second Division. 

Between 1994 and 2004 he swapped clubs playing for Krylia Sovetov Samara, Torpedo-ZIL Moscow, Metallurg Lipetsk, Rubin Kazan, Salyut-Energiya Belgorod and Spartak-MZhK Ryazan. 

In 2005 he retired from the sport following a decline in success. He remains in Russia and continues to take an interest in football.
