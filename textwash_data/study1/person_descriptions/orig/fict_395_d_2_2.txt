This was due to Dwaine's father, Hansel. Hansel was a in the armed forces and was stationed in Germany.

Dwaine was always interested in archaeology and dreamed of becoming an explorer. This Passion led him to meet his girlfriend Beth at the archaeological convention of 1985 . Both Beth and Dwaine are atheist and so no reason to marry.     

Dr Dwaine is a hydrographic surveyor and has won 3 prices from this.

Dr Dwaine is close to retiring now and plans to travel the world, going to historic sites.
