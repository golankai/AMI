Elton John is a singer, songwriter and pianist.  He was born in England in 1947.  He grew up in London.  Over the years he has had many hit songs and has written songs for many other famous artists. He is a very talented artist and has received five Grammy awards and two golden globes.  He sang a famous song for Lady Diana,; Candle in the Wind which he performed at her funeral in Westminster Abbey in 1997.   His first hit single was Your Song in 1970.  He established the Elton John Aids Foundation 1992.  He has been openly gay since `988 and married his husband, David Furnish in 2005. They have 2 adapted children together.
