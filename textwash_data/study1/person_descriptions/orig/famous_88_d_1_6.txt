Adele is a female musician. Her first single to break through the charts was Chasing Pavements. Her first album was 19. She spent some time touring it through America to attempt to break the US market. Her next album, 21,  was full of hits: Someone Like You, Rolling in the Deep. It was written about an ex-boyfriend ad growing up in a London borough. This brought her global success and she went back to the US after this to play arenas.

She then released the album, 25, She came back with her song 'Hello' which was advertised on the commercials of X factor and debuted on Nick Grimshaw's breakfast show. In between she had a baby and went through a divorce. She has also performed many times on the Brits

Recently she lost a lot of weight and has been linked romantically to Harry Styles.
