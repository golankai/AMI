Dr. Brantley Elias Ratke was born in Gothenburg, Sweden on December 5th 1983 to parents Sven and Linnea Ratke. In the early days Brantley had a love of history, which led him going to Lund university in which sees him finishing with flying colours. He went onto getting his dream job as a Museum education officer at Swedish History Museum in Stockholm, Sweden, which sees him organising and encouraging people and children in disadvantaged in programmes such as numeracy, literacy and computers.
Personal life: Dr. Brantley Ratke has had a couple of serious relationships, with one to Elsa Larsson with whom he was engaged to was called off when his career took him to Stockholm.
