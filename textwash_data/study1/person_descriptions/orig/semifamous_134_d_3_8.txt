Aleksandr Gryazin is a retired Russian professional footballer. Aleksandr was born on 23 August 1974 so he is currently 46 years of age. 

His full name is Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Gryazin. He was born in the city of Lyubertsy which is in Russia. He is 1.80 metres tall. 

He played the position of midfielder/striker when he was a football player. 

He had made his debut as a professional football player in 1992 in the Russian Second Division for PFC CSKA-d Moscow.

He has played for team PFC CSKA-d Moscow from 1992 to 1994. He has played for team Hapoel Bat Yam F.C. from 1994 to 1995. He has played for team FC Krylia Sovetov Samara from 1995 to 1996. He has played for team FC Saturn Ramenskoye from 1996 to 2000.
