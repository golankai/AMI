Aleksandr Gryazin is a retired professional footballer.  His debut was in 1992 where he played for PFC CSKA-d Moscow which was in the Russian Division 2.  He was born on 23rd August 1974 in Lyubertsy in Russia and he is 5 feet 11 inches tall. His playing position is a midfielder and striker.  Whilst playing for his first team he played 84 games and scored 22 goals. After that he moved in 1994 to Hapoel Bat Yam FC but did not play a game with them before moving to FC Krilia Sovetov Samara where he played 24 matches and scored 2 goals in this period.  After this he went on to play for six other teams before his retirement.
