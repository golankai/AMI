Christian Bale is a male actor who is best known for playing the second most recent batman. He is in his late 40's, and over the course of his acting career has won two golden globes and an academy award. The British actor is now married to Sibi Blazic with who he has two children with. His first on-screen role was in the 1980's for a PacMan cereal commercial at the young age of 9. Along with some of the Batman films, he also starred in American Psycho (2000), Equilibrium (2002) and The Machinist (2004). Bale claims that his career choice was influenced by his childhood as his family moved around a lot including places like the United Kingdom, Portugal and the United states.
