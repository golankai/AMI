Emma watson is a female english actress most well known for her role as Hermoine Grainger in Harry Potter. She has become world famous and appeared in lot's of blockbuster films. She is also very well known for her support of womens right movements and is fashionable. She was born in Paris but brought up in England and went to school in oxford. She has brown hair and is 30 years old. As part of her acting career she has also taken part in modeling and has appeared on loads of magazine covers and fashion shoots. She won loads of awards for her acting including an NMA and lots of teen choice awards.
