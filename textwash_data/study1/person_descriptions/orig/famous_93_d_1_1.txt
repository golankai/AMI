Benedict Cumberbatch is a classically trained English actor who was born on 19 July 1976 in Hammersmith. After being educated at boarding schools, he trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, He has starred in both Television shows and films. He famous for playing Sherlock Holmes alongside Martin Freeman in the BBC TV show Sherlock. He is also famous for playing the lead role in the Marvel film Dr. Strange and also playing the same role in other Marvel films such as Avengers:Endgame. He has won many awards including the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor for Frankenstein. He is married to English theatre and opera director Sophie Hunter.
