Elton John is a British musician and song writer known for his ballads and piano music. He grew in popularity in the 70s and 80s with his fun and upbeat music. He is known for flamboyant dress and behaviour and was famously one of the first big stars to come out as gay. He has a husband and two adopted children. He is relatively short and likes to wear tinted glasses.He is known as someone who likes to shop and is particular about his appearance. He has won numerous awards and wrote the music for The Disney film the Lion King. He is in his late 60s. He recently had a film made about his life called Rocketman.
