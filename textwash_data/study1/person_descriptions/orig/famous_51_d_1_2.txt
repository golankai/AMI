Ed Sheeran was born in Halifax in 1991. Growing up, he lived in Hebden Bridge and Suffolk. As a child he sang in a local church choir and learnt guitar. He is now a 29 year old singer-songwriter and has been active since 2005. His music fits into the genres of pop, folk-pop and hip hop. He has sold 150 million records worldwide and has achieved 12 number ones, giving him the record for most number one hits in a decade. Ed Sheeran is well known for being one of the easiest ways to fill an arena full of straight people. His music are very often covered at weddings by disgruntled string quartets who couldn't get into orchestras and would much rather be playing Beethoven. He has ginger hair.
