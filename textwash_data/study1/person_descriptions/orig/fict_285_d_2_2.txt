Dr Dwaine O'Conner is a 62 year old man who was born in Dresden, Germany. His current occupation is Hydrographic enigineer which he has been doing for thirty years. He is a widower with two children and currently 3 grandchildren, he was married for 25 years before his wife passed. Recently he has met someone new and is happily settled again. Dwaine's job has taken him around the world many times, but enjoys meeting new people and immersing himself in new cultures. As he is approaching retirement he has decided his time to slow down and enjoy his time by teaching the next wave of hydrographic surveyors.
