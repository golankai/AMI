Dr Brantley Ratke - born and raised in Stockholm, Sweden and a strong advocate of Swedish culture - is an experienced museum education officer who has spend the last 15 years working in the cultural sector. He is a well-respected figure in the industry, having worked at some of Sweden's most renowned museums throughout his career. However, this has not left much time for other parts of his life. Dating has taken a back seat throughout his adult life, with his focus rarely moving away from the museum floor. He is passionate about history, geography and a firm believer in the educational power of museums for people of all ages.
