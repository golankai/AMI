Ronda Schuster is 69 years old and was born in October 1950 in a small town in North Yorkshire into a large family. She started working for a small printing company in Leeds in 1980s after having children and worked her way up the ladder to become a sales executive in 2001. She is married to John Schuster, an architect with his own successful company and they have 2 children. Ronda does a lot of charity work and has even climbed Mount Kilimanjaro to raise money for be local cancer charity after losing her best friend to the disease. Ronda enjoys walking and hill climbing and is involved her her local amateur dramatics society. She lives with her husband and 2 dogs in the outskirts of Leeds.
