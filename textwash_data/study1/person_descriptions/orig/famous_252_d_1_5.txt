Mick Jagger is the colourful lead singer with the rock band the Rolling Stones. Famous for his energetic performances, distinctive lips, pout and strutting-style dancing he first came to prominence with the band in the 1960s.

Forever young, Mick's career is now well past 50 years and he shows no sign of slowing down yet.

Mick has dated and indeed married many women but is probably most associated with his first wife Bianca and a long association with the Texan super-model Jeri Hall.

Mick love for the game of cricket is also enduring. Mick can often be spotted at Lords during England test matches.
