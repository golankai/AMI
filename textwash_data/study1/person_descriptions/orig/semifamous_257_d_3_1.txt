Enoch Lincoln was a u.s representative from Massachusetts and Maine he went to Harvard College in 1811, where he studied law. He was also a governor of Maine. He was a member of the American antiquarian society where some of his personal and business documents are still kept,  Abraham was distantly related to Abraham Lincoln through ancestor Samuel Lincoln. He was a democratic-republican to the seventeenth congress and died in 1819 in Augusta, Maine 
He served as Governor of Maine from 1827 until his death. He won three terms, with 90% and over of the vote. He did not run for a fourth  term and the town of Lincoln in Maine is named after him.
