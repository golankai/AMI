Daniel Radcliffe is an English actor, as well as a producer. He was born in 1989 on the 23rd of July in London, England. Although many people believe he started out his career by starring in the Harry Potter movies based on J. K. Rowling's books, the truth is, he made his acting debut in BBC One's David Copperfield movie, at the age of 10. However, in the same year, he starred in Harry Potter and the Philosopher Stone, the first movie of the Harry Potter series. He continued being the main character in the series for over 10 years, ending with Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows  - Part 2. Apart from the Harry Potter series, Daniel Radcliffe has starred in other famous movies, such as The Woman In Black, Kill Your Darlings and Now You See Me 2.
