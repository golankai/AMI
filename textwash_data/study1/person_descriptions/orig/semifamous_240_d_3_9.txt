Walter K Chorn was born in Howard County, Missouri in 1885 and died in 1933.  He played football in college and then became a lawyer, having graduated with an LLB from Missouri and Vanderbilt university in Nashville, Tennessee.

Whilst at university he was a member of Kappa Sigma and played football for the varsity team.  A dapper young man, he cut a chase with his sideswept hair and neat white tie with black suit.

He practiced law for 2 years before becoming chief clerk of the commisson that revised the Missouri statutes.  In 1913 he became chief clerk to the Missouri supreme court.

He died in 1933 aged 48 in Kansas City, Missouri.
