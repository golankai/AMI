A posh English actor, Tall, dark and handsome. 
He was born on 9/9/60 in London and married Anna Eberstein in 2018. He has 5 children from 2 women. He studied English at oxford university and graduated with a 2:1. 
He received a BAFTA and Golden Globe award for best actor in Four weddings and a Funeral.
In 1995 he was arrested in Los Angeles for receiving oral sex from Divine Brown. He was fined 1180 US dollars and was ordered to complete an AIDS education programme. 
He is a fundraiser and ambassador for Marie Curie Cancer Care after the death of his mother, Fynvola MacLean. His ancestors include Sir Walter Raleigh.
