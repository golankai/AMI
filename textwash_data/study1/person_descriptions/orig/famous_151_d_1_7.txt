Daniel Radcliffe is an actor/producer from England, born in 1989. He is most famous for his role as Harry Potter in the Harry Potter film series, involving 8 films released between 2001 and 2011. 

He has short brown hair, bushy eyebrows and a beard. While he can play serious roles in films, his personality is fun and comedic at times. He has a sense of humour and many people can relate to him.

When people think of Daniel Radcliffe, the instant thought would be Harry Potter as he played the main character. Other actors/actresses that come to mind when you think of Daniel are Emma Watson and Rupert Grint who are his co-stars in the Harry Potter series.
