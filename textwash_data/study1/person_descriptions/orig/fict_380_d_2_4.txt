Born in Kampala in Uganda on 26/8/1983 to Afiya and Damba Ratke. He has 2 brothers and one sister. 
He was educated at the local school then onto Makerere University where he graduated with a first class honours degree in  English. He then did a post graduate in Archaeology. 
He was previously married for 5 years and has one child but has recently become single again. He now lives alone in a luxury apartment near the shore of Lake Victoria.
He works at the Kampala International Museum and is responsible for encouraging children to visit the museum. He does this by visiting the local primary and secondary schools. He organises talks.
