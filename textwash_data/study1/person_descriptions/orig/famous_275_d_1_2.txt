Ed Sheeran is a Grammy award winning British musician. After being well known for performing live shows with his guitar, he eventually found fame after going viral with an online video. This video got the attention of the rapper Example who consequently invited him to open up for his show. This is hen led to the release of his first EP in 2010. Success followed and so did the music - ‘thinking out loud’ and ‘shape of you’ are just a few hits to name. As well as this he has song written for the likes of Taylor swift and one direction. Last month he welcomed his first child with his long term girlfriend, Cherry seaborn.
