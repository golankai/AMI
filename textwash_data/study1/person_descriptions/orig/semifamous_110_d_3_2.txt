
William, full name William P. Bettendorf is a inventor who invented lift sulky plow alongside with two other important and useful inventions. He was born in 1857 in the month of July. Bettendorf made a shop to manufacture the wheel. He brought together the bettendorf axle company. This factory was built on atleast 70 acres. William got married in 1879. He had two children. He died on june 13 1910. He is buried in Davenport in a cemetry there. He is of american nationality. He was working as a messenger boy in his early life. After that, he was working as a machinist.
