Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor, most known as Harry Potter in the series of movies of the same name franchise. His is regarded as successful actor and he has managed to move on from his famous role (which he starred in as a very young actor) to other things, other roles and even becoming a producer. He is still young (born in 1989); it is reported that he divides his time between USA and UK. 
He was critically acclaimed for a number of his works, on stage and on the screen; he participated in several TV series both as actor and voice actor. 
He is also performing on stage in both countries, and his is a supporter of the Labour party in the UK.
