Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor most known for starring as Sherlock Holmes and Dr Strange. According to Wikipedia, he has been married to his wife Sophie Hunter since 2015, has two children, and is 44 years old. He is currently working on the new Dr Strange film due to be released in May 2021. He is well educated and has won a BAFTA and has a CBE. He stands at 1.83m and has dark hair and pale skin. He is known for his deep voice and posh accent. Both his parents were actors and he is a grandson of a submarine commander. He seems to be highly involved in charity work and donates on his birthday each year.
