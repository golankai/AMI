Rinda Schuster is an English sales executive. She was born in England and has lived there her whole life. She was born in 1951 in a rural village in the North of England. She married her husband in 1971 who she met while at school before they both moved to London together to pursue her husband's dream of being a musician. Acting as her husband's manager, she starting working in the music industry and was integral to building her husband's career. Being familiar with selling and marketing music, in her early 30's she changed career to becoming a sales executive. The change in career was due to her becoming pregnant and feeling that the music industry would be too draining for a new mother and the flexibility of working from home as a sales executive appealed massively to Rinda.
