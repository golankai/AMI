Charles Black was really into Law. In fact he loved law so much that he became a professor of it. He wrote a really well-liked document on how to impeach people and was much involved in a court case in the highest court of the (US) land. His document was to prove useful during the Watergate scandal, which led to the impeachment of President Nixon. He studied at Yale, after a stint at the  University of Austin, Texas. He also did time in the army where he taught some stuff about law. He hated the death penalty and devoted much of his career to decrying it. He has written over a dozen books on law.
