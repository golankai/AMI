Lucia, 63 and based in Ireland, has had a long career and has now reached the acclaimed position of Chief Marketing Officer in a well-known international advertising agency. Previous to this, Lucia has held several high profile positions in similar companies. 
Previously divorced, Lucia is has been with her current partner for many years, sharing two children together. 
She has a love of the countryside, and her current location of Ireland affords her the opportunity to partake in the wonders of the Irish countryside and coast, but also allows her to get to work in Dublin quickly and easily.
As well as outdoor pursuits, Lucia loves to travel, and her position of CMO allow lots of international travel, with New York and Sydney being some of her favourite destinations.
