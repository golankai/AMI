Malcolm Stephen Thomas Dunstan was born in Redruth, Cornwall on the 14th of October 1950. He was a Former English Cricketer who played for a total of 4 teams throughout his playing career. These teams included Minor Countries South(1979), Minor Countries West(1977-1978), Gloucestershire(1971-1974) and Cornwall(1969-1989). He was best known for his Right-Handed Batting ability and also Slightly known for his Medium Pace right arm bowling ability. Dunstan played a total of 23 list A matches and scored 216 runs through his career, he then played 12 first class matches on top of this he has scored 283 runs.
