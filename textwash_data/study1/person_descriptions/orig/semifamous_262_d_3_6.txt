Malcolm Stephen Thomas Dunstan, now aged 69, is a former English Cricketer from Cornwall. 

Beginning his career in 1969, the right-handed batsman's career spanned a decade and saw him play for teams, including Minor Counties South, Minor Countries West, Gloucestershire and finally Cornwall.

A medium-pace right-handed bowler, Dunstan's professional career spanned 35 matches - 13 First-Class and 23 List A.

His First-Class career saw him score 283 runs and held a batting average of 16,64. In comparison, whilst playing at List A Dunstan held a 10,80 batting average and scored 216 runs. 

Whist never playing for the national team, he did play against Pakistan as part of their national tour in 1971.
