Charles Black was a scholar who studied constitutional law and taught the same . He was born in Texas and later when on to get a Masters degree in English. He began writing poetry at the age of 40 publishing three volumes .He was an outspoken critic of the death penalty and has also studied in the Yale University.He then served in the Army Air Corps as a teacher .Many of his works has been published related to law and poems. He died at the age of 85 in New York. He is most known for Brown v. Board of Education case. and his famous book is Impeachment: A Handbook
Not much is known about his personal life but it is believed that he married  Barbara Aronstein Black and she was a Dean in Columbia Law School.
