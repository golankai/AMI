Dr. Brantley Ratke was born and grew up in Uppsala in 1983, with three siblings. His parents were accountants and strongly encouraged him to take up the trade, but Ratke decided to study history at Stockholm University. While there, he spent a year as an exchange student in York where ho took part in various archaeological digs. Upon completion of his undergraduate degree, Ratke returned to Uppsala to do a masters in curation, after which he took up a graduate position as an education officer at Uppsala Art Museum. Ratke has had several serious relationships, although he separated amicably from his most recent boyfriend because he had to move for a work opportunity.
