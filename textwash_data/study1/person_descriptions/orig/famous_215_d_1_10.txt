Sam Smith is an English singer and songwriter. They are non-binary and prefer they/them pronouns. They originally came out as a gay man, later saying they were non-binary. They sang a James Bond theme song which won an Oscar for best original song. Smith was also the songwriter on this. Smith's style is primarily romantic ballads. They have been part of collaborations with other artists on dance tracks before coming to fame with their own ballad style of songwriting. They have been around for a number of years now. Their vocals are notable as being high and distinctive. They are white and originally from England although they have travelled around the world to record and perform. Notable songs of Smith include 'Stay with me' and 'too good at goodbyes'
