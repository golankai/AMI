Ms. Lucia Hamill PhD (age 63) is the Chief Marketing Officer for Industrial Components Inc. She holds a degree in Marketing from Goldsmith's, University of London, and a master's in Marketing from Durham University. Her PhD, exploring how knowledge and facts can be best communicating to the public, was awarded by UCL, and featured in numerous journals. After this, she was an Assistant Professor at UCL, then a Professor, serving as a professor for 20 years, before switching to the private sector to join Industrial Components Inc as an adviser, then as CMO, She has been married to Jamie Hamill PhD, a mathematics researcher at UCL and Field's Medal Winner, for 30 years, and has three children.
