Dr. Lucia Hamill is a sixty-three year old woman who currently resides in Ireland, with her partner of 30 years. She is employed as a chief marketing officer for a large textile design firm, before this she earned her doctorate in econometrics from Trinity College, Dublin and her BA in economics from the University of Manchester. In her spare time she enjoys gardening and tends to a large collection of root vegetables and some violets and peonies. She also has four cats named after various DC comic book characters and a fondness for collecting vintage Victorian era postcards depicting sunbathing frogs. She takes her coffee with cream and two sugars.
