Elton John was born Reginald Kenneth Dwight in Pinner, Middlesex, England, on March 25 1947. Elton John is a famous British singer, pianist and composer. He is one of the best selling music artist of his generation having won many music awards throughout his career. John's most famous song is Candle in the Wind and has sold over 33 million copies making it one of the highest selling singles of all time. Elton John has composed music for theatre shows such as The Lion King, Billy Elliot the Musical, Although John has had relationships with women and was married to a women for a very short amount of time, he is now openly gay and married to David Furnish with whom he shares two children with.
