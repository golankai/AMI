Walter Chorn was born in 1885 in Missouri. He loved the town he was born in and decided to attend college . During his college years Walter was heavily involved in football.
He later attended Vanderbuilt University in Nashville, Tennessee where his career win law started. Walter graduated with a LL.B.
Following completing his studies in Tennessee, Walter decided to move back to his home town of Missouri and opened up a law office in Fayette. Walter's career in law was successful and he later became the chief clerk of the commission in Missouri. Walter was responsible for the commission which revised the statues of Missouri. Walter later in his career became the chief clerk of the  supreme court of Missouri.
