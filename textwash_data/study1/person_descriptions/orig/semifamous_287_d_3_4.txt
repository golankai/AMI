Clyde Cyril Fernandes is a professional footballer from India. He has always played as a defender and represented India in their national Under 16's team. As he got older he continued his success and played for his home City, Mumbai. Whilst playing for them in 2016 he made notable appearances for the team. Yet this was enough, as he has progressed in his career to become and international player and since 2018 plays for the South African team, Ozoe. At aged just 26 and with a few more years left in him, who know's where his talent will take him? Will we hear more of his super defending abilities?
