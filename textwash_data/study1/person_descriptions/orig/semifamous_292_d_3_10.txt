Irvin Woodberry Brooks also know as Chester  was born June 5, 1891 and died February 4, 1966. Irvin was an American baseball player in the Negro leagues. Several books incorrectly list him as ""Chester"" Brooks born in the Bahamas, he was actually born in Key West, Florida. Brooks spent his entire playing career with the Brooklyn Royal Giants his career lasted 15years between 1918 to 1933. The nickname ""Chester"" was a reference to his West Indian origins.  During his playing career he was known as ""Irvin"" or occasionally as ""Irving. Cumberland Posey, the influential owner of the Homestead Grays, named Brooks to his all-time Negro league baseball all-star team.
