Walter K. Chorn was born in January 21 1885 in Howard County, Missouri. 
He attended college and university. One being Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee where he played on a football team as a guard and tackle.
After he graduated, he opened a law office in Fayette, Missouri, where he remained for 2 years until he became chief clerk of the commision that revised the Missouri states in 1909.
1910-1911 he served in the state auditor's office, until in 1913 he was then a chief clerk of the supreme court in Missouri. 
He passed away on February 26, 1933 aged only 48. He died in Kansas City, Missouri.
