Kenneth Lee Kramm (Kenny Kramm) was born on June 16, 1961 in Washington D.C and died on July 12, 2016. Kenny was an American entrepreneur who founded the company ""FLAVORx"". FLAVORx was known for adding flavouring to unpalatable medicines to make them easier to take. Kenny was married to Shelley Neiss in 1985 which was a year after he graduated from the University of Maryland. Kenny obtained a degree in in advertising design and shortly after graduating became an art director for an agency. In 1992, Kenny realised he didn't particularly enjoy his career and decided to join his father's pharmacy firm as a business manager back in Washington.
