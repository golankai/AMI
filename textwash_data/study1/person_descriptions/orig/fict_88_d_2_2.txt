Dwaine grew up in the UK with 10 brothers and sisters. He went to live with extended family for a summer in Germany and there he learning German. He went to university in Munich to study surveying. 

While at uni he met his now husband. They met at the uni volleyball club where they both played. They have since adopted 3 children, 2 girls and a boy, and have 5 grandchildren. Dwaine played volleyball for a local Munich team for many years and has several trophies on his mantel.

He really enjoys his job but is looking forward to retirement in the next few years. He has plans to travel more and see his grandchildren who live in France and New Zealand.
