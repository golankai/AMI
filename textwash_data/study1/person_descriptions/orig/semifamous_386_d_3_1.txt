Enoch Lincoln was an american harvard graduate and politician who lived in the late 18th to early 19th century. born in worcester, massachusetts, he studied law and began his career in salem (famous for the 1692 witch trials) massachusetts. He has a town named after him in Maine, which is place of death. As both his name and profession suggests, he was indeed (distantly) related to the famous president Abraham Lincoln. He spent 2 years in office as governor and 8 years in office as a u.s. representative before that, serving his country well. from what i can see on his wikipedia page he was a republican
