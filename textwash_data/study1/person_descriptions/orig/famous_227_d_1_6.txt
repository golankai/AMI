Adele is a famous singer, well known for her album ""21"" in which she sang the hit song ""Rolling in the Deep"". During her stint as a solo artists she has reached critical acclaim for her emotional, and powerful songs that tend to resonate during both live and recorded sessions. Recently Adele has been featured in the spotlight for her weight loss and ""glow-up"", a position of controversy for body positive peoples. 

Outside of her career, Adele can be seen as an enthusiastic and outgoing individual on show such as Carpool Karaoke with James Corden. Adele owns multiple houses, in both America and her home country of England.
