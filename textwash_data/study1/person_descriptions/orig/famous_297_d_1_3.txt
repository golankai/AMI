Emma Watson is an British Actress. She is famous for her role in Harry potter series, she reprised the role of Hermione. She was born on April 15, 1990, in paris. Watson debut film 'Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's stone' was an enormous success.  The actress appeared in all the eight harry potter movies from the year 2001 to 2011. 
In year 2017 she starred as the Disney character in Beauty and the Beast movie. She has also done some modelling work. Her modeling career includes her campaigns for Burberry and Lancome.

She is beautiful from the inside and out. She is a role model for youth. As an established movie star, she is also a true fashionista.
