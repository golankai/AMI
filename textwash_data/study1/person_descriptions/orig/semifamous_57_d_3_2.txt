William was an inventor who was born in July 1857. His most famous and well known inventions were power lift sulky plow, the Bettendorf metal wheel and the one-piece railroad truck fram. He was a very creative man and loved to imagine and create new things. He was often alone trying new things and building and making new inventions. He was a stern man and didn't get along with many people, despite that he was married twice to Mary and Elizabeth and had 2 children Etta and Henry. He grew up Kansas USA and started work as a messenger boy. He eventually built his own house in Bettendorf and the river valley below, the estate was built for more than 150,000! He sadly died aged 52 in his home in Iowa
