Mick Jagger is a famous British singer and song writer who is a member of the Rolling Stones as well as having hits as a solo artist. He is well known for his distinctive dance moves and energy during a performance.

Born on 26th July 1943 and grew up in Kent, he has been married and divorced once and has 8 children, 5 grand children and 1 great grandchild.

Mick was knighted in 2002 for services to pop music, and has sponsors a number of different music related charities.

Micks hits include Dancing in the Street, Sweet Thing and Out of Focus. His first Solo Album was 'She's the Boss' in 1985 and las was 'Goddess in the Doorway' in 2001
