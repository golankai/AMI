Elton John, knighted Sir Elton John in 1998, is an English musician who blends pop and rock into a style that he is renowned for. In his lifetime he has sold more than 300 million records, and for this makes him one of the best-selling music artists in history. 

John began his serious music career in 1969, with his debut album 'Goodbye Yellow Brick Road'. He then went on to have a successful music career and is still one of the most well-known artists of the 21st century.

Some of his most recent albums include: Wonderful Crazy Night (2016), The Diving Board (2013), The Captain & the Kid (2006) and Peachtree Road (2004).
