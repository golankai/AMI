Emma Watson is a British actress who started acting at a young age. She was cast in the Harry Potter movies along side Daniel Radcliffe. Emma played the part of another student called Hermione Granger. This really started her career off with a bang and Emma continued after the Harry Potter film series to star in many other movies. She has proved herself to be an excellent actress, she even featured in the Disney remake of Beauty and the Beast proving she could dance and sing.
Emma also has a modelling career and is a feminist. She is a UN Women Goodwill Embassador and has done so work work in this field helping so many women all over the world.
