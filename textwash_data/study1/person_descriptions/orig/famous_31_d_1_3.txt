Emma Watson is an English actress who was born in Paris to English parents but brought up in Oxfordshire. She lived near Paris until the age of 5. Her parents divorced when she was young and she moved England to live with her mother. She trained at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach Theatre Arts. She is 30 years old. In 2001 aged 11 she started starting in the Harry Potter films as Hermione Granger. She studied English Literature at Brown University after her fame in Harry Potter and graduated in 2014. She has been in several other films including Beauty and the Beast and Little Women. She is also known as a feminist and activist and in 2014 was appointed as a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador and helped start HeforShe, a UN Women campaign which advocates gender equality. She has also worked as a model. She is currently single.
