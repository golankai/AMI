Sir Elton John is a British singer song-writer and is one of biggest selling artists of all time. His song “candle in the wind” was a tribute to Princess Diana and is the highest selling single in UK history. He has won multiple awards in the music, stage and film industries including Tony and Grammy awards. He has had multiple number 1 singles and albums and is often ranked as one of the most influential musicians of all time. He is also one of the wealthiest Britons. 
He is married to David Furnish who he has 2 sons with and is an honorary president of Watford football club who he previously owned.
He has held a few controversial views in the past and has had issues with eating disorders and drugs. This was a consequence of stardom.
