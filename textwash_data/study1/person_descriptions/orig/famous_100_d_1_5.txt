Mick Jagger is the lead singer of British rock band The Rolling Stones. He was born in 1943 in Dartford, Kent. He formed the band in the early 1960s with Brian Jones, Keith Richards, Bill Wyman, Charlie Watts and Ian Stewart. Keith Richards and Mick Jagger knew eachother from their early childhood, having been born in the same area and attending the same school. Ronnie Wood joined in 1975.
Mick Jagger was knighted for his services to music in the early 2000s.
Mr Jagger is a known ladies man, having been married on two occasions first with with Bianca Jagger and then with supermodel Jerry Hall. He's got a total of 8 children with five different women.
