Christian Bale is a famous male actor known for playing one of the roles of Batman and the one of the main characters in American Psycho. He is 46 years old at the time of writing this and will turn 47 on 30th January.
He is 5'11 and he was born in Pembrokeshire, Wales, UK.
His mother was a circus perfomer and his father was born in South Africa and his job was a commercial pilot.
Growing up, he moved around a lot. His first acting job was for a cereal advert in 1983. He also appeared on the West End perfoming in ""The Nerd"". He thought about quitting acting very early on due to struggling to adjust to fame and the attention.
