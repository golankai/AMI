Christian Bale is an English actor who played Batman in the Dark Knight trilogy. He continues to act in some very good films.
He is married with 2 children. He is 46 and was born in Wales.
Christian will will play main villain in the Marvel film Thor: Love and Thunder, release dates is supposed to be on 18 February 2022, this will be his second comic-book role since he played Batman.
He made his film debut in the television film Anastasia: The Mystery of Anna in 1986.
Christian Bale has received several awards and nominations for his film and television performances. His biggest awards include one Academy Awards, four British Academy Film Awards, six Critics' Choice Movie Awards, two Golden Globe Awards and two Screen Actors Guild Awards.
