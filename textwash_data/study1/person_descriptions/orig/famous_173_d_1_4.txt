This person is a world famous, award winning, male musician with an eccentric dress sense. He has a huge range of well known and hit songs. One of which he titled “candle in the wind” dedicated to Princess Diana after her death, he is often linked with the royal family. An eccentric dresser, he is known for his tinted glasses and flamboyant outfits. He is gay and married with children.
As well as singing, he also plays piano and is a song writer. He is British. He also wrote ""circle of life"", the famous theme song to The lion king and songs for the film Moulin Rouge. He is a household name and easily recognisable to many people.
