Mick Jagger (Sir Michael Philip Jagger) is an English singer. He is also a songwriter, an actor and a film producer. He is most famously known for being the lead singer and one of the founding members of the rolling stones. He was born on the 26th of July 1943 and grew up in Dartford, Kent . He was married to Bianca Perez-Mora Macias for 7 years in 1971 and was unofficially married from 1990 -1999 to model, Jerry Hall. He has 8 children with five different women. He is a Grand Father to five and a Great Grandfather to one. His last child was born in 2016 when he was already a Grandfather and Great Grandfather!!
