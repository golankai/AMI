Clyde Fernandes is a professional footballer. Born in Mumbai India he W was a gifted footballer from a very young age and by the age of 10 was well known locally for his incredible talent. His heroes were the likes of Marodonna and Pele who he studied by watching television. He famously used to practice his skills out in the streets of Mumbai using a ball of rags as a football as his family were too poor to afford anything else. He was discovered by David Beecham who met him while visiting India on a charity trip with Save the children. This chance meeting lead to Clyde being given a place in the UK at a football academy where his career went from strength o strength.
