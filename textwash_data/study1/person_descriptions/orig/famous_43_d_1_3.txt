English actress who played Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter films alongside Danielle Radcliffe. More recently she played Belle in Beauty and the Beast and appeared in Little Women. She appeared in all 8 Harry Potter films. 
As a child she trained at Stagecoach in singing dancing and acting. She has also done some modeling work for Lancomme and Burbery. On Beauty and the Beast she was required to sing. She attended an all girls school in Oxfordshire. 
She is of slight build with blonde hair.
She is one of the highest paid Actresses in the world thanks to her role in Harry Potter. 
She was born on the 5th April 1990 in Paris.
She studied at university alongside her acting career and got a degree in English Literature. She has also won awards for her acting.
