Ferd Homenick is a Belgian dealer. Born in 1967, he lives in Antwerp where he is based as a dealer of miscellaneous things. Ferd speaks Flemish and French, and has spent time in other countries doing his deals. Ferd is notable for dealing in exotic chocolates, which he smuggled in his bushy Belgian beard during the confectionery embargo of the early 2000s. Ferd is a tall white man. He sports a mighty beard which is both blond and red, which Ferd attributes to his viking ancestry. Ferd identifies as aromantic  because he just loves doing deals. He was once engaged to a Brazilian supermodel but later said it was a ploy to do a deal for some famous sculpture. A deal which he closed.
