Sam Smith is an English singer and song writer. He was born  in London on 19th May 1992. Before coming known as a singer in his own right he featured on popular dance tracks such as Latch by Disclosure and La La La by Naughty Boy. He then released his hit album In the Lonely Hour in May 2014, which spawned the hit song Stay With Me, which also became an international success. He released a second album in 2017 and also sang the James Bond song for Spectre. He has won many awards such as Grammys and Brit awards. He is also known for losing a lot of weight and coming out as gay. He is distantly related to singer Lily Allen and actor Alfie Allen.
