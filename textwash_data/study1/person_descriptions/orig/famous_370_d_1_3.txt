A 30 year old British actor who is famous for playing Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter films. Started acting as a young child at 10 and continues to do so, however has appeared in fewer films since Harry Potter (which was her first professional acting role). She was born in Paris, grew up in Oxford and now lives in the USA. Alongside acting she has also done modelling work and has worked for various advertising campaigns. She has one BAFTA award from 2014 for British Artist of the Year. She also engages in charity and activist work, and has been appointed as a ""UN Women Goodwill ambassador"" for work toward gender equality.
