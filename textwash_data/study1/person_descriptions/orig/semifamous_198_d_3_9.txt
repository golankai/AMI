Walter K. Chorn (full name Walter Knaus Chorn) was a college football player, lawyer, and one time insurance superintendent of Missouri.  He was born on January 21st 1885 and sadly passed away at the age of 48 on February 26th 1933.  He weighed 77kg.  In high school Chorn loved football so much.  He attended Central College in Fayette, Missouri and Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee.  Chorn was a guard and tackle for Vanderbilt Commodores football teams.  At Vanderbilt he was a member of Kappa Sigma.  He graduated from the latter with a batchelor of law.  After graduation, he opened his own law firm in Missouri, practicing for two years.
