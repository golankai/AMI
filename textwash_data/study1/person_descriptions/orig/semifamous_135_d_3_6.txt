Malcolm Dunstan was born in 1950. He was born in Redruth in Cornwall and played Cricket for Cornwall for the first time in 1969. His first match was against Devon.  He continued to play for Cornwall throughout his career.  In 1971 he has his debut of first-class cricket as a professional player with Gloucester against the Pakistani cricket team on tour.  He played from 1971-1974 he played in twelve first-class cricket matches where he made 283 runs in totall. His batting average was 16.64.  His top score was 52.  Despite playing with Glouster he still played for Cornwall in the minor Counties from 1969-1989.
