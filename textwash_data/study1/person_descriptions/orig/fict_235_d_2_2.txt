Doctor Dwaine O'Conner is living in Germany after moving from New York (USA) for work, as one of the World's leading Hydrographic Surveyors. He is in Germany to lead a massive project to redevelop the countries ports in the north of the country, allowing for a larger capacity of Cargo and passenger travel to countries such as Denmark, Norway and Sweden in particular. Dwaine met his true love in Germany aged 60, Emma, and never wishes to return to the US so he can spend the rest of his life with her. They both share a passion for the Ocean and have recently moved in together into Dwaine's houseboat on the river Rhine.
