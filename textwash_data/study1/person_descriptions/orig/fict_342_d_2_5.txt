Ms Lucia Hamill works as a chief marketing officer. She started working as a marketing officer when she was 30 and was promoted to the chief marketing officer 10 years ago. She has a degree in marketing and a PhD which helped her get her job. She did her degree and PhD in University College Dublin where she was born but before starting her PhD she spent a year in France as a sales executive for a clothes shop in Paris. Hamill never got married but is happily in a relationship with a man who she met on a work trip 30 years ago when their companies teamed up. They have 2 sons and 5 grandchildren. Hamill is planning on retiring in 4 years when she will be 67 and moving into a small house in Italy where she has always dreaming of living.
