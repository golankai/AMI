Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor who is best known for playing the titled character in the Harry Potter films. Born in Fulham; England, he soon found stardom at a young age following his big screen debut in the first Harry Potter film. Since his final outing as Harry, Daniel Radcliffe has gone on to star in numerous other films and continues to find work into his early adulthood. His personal life has been under scrutiny from the media for all of his teens and formative years, similar to his Harry Potter co-stars Rupert Grint and Emma Watson. Daniel has also had outings as a producer and remains a ‘child star’ in the eyes of many. He enjoys lifelong fane from his childhood successes.
