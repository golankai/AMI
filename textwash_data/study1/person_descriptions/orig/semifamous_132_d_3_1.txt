Enoch Lincoln lived December 8th 1788 to 8th October 1829. Enoch Lincoln lived in Maine, America. He graduated Harvard College in 1807. He served as governor of Maine from 3rd January 1827 until he died 8th October 1829. Enoch Lincoln was son of Levi Lincoln. Enoch Lincoln studied law and was admitted to the bar and commenced in profession in law in 1811. The town of Lincoln in Maine is named after Enoch Lincoln. Enoch Lincoln was elected as democratic-republican. Enoch Lincoln was also elected as a member to the American Anquitairian Society in 1819. Enoch Lincoln won three terms all with over ninety percent vote, he did not run for a fourth term.
