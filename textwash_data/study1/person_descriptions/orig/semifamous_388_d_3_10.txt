Irvin Brooks also known  as Irvin chester brooks was a black american baseball player who played in the american  negro baseball leagues.Many books have mentioned him as been born in the Bahamas, but in fact he was born in 1891 in key west, Florida.He played  in his career for the Brooklyn Royal Giants and was an outstanding hitter.Many expert have included him in their all time negro league badeball allstar team.He died in february 1966 at the age of 74. Many people who know a lot about the history of baseball have commented that if he had been allowed to take part in the national baseball league he would have become one of the top players in america.
