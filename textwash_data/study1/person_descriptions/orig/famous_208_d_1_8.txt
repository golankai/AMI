Christian Bale is a British actor who debuted in the movie 'Empire of the Sun'. But actually Bale made his stage debut at the age of ten, playing opposite British comedian Rowan Atkinson in The Nerd. He has been recognized for his acting and been awarded Academy awards as well as a couple of Golden Globe awards. He has often portrayed psychologically tormented characters. His net worth is more than $60 million dollars. One of his highest rated movies is  Henry V. He is 45 years old and was born in Pembrokeshire, Wales.
Bale was raised in England, Portugal, and the U.S. His mother was a dancer and both of his grandfathers were part-time actors.
