Hugh Grant is a British actor who is most known for staring in romcoms such as Notting Hill and his floppy hair and posh accent. He was born in 1960 in London. He has also stared in comedies such as About a Boy and as the Prime Minister in Love Actually with a very famous dance scene around No. 10. He was in a relationship with Elizabeth Hurley early on his career. He was involved in exposing the phone hacking scandel in 2011 as he was a victim of this. He received great notoriety for his role as a womaniser in Bridget Jones. He married Swedish producer Anna Eberstine in 2018 and they have children together.
