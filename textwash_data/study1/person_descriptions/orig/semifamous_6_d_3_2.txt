William was a German-American inventor most known for the invention of the power lift. By the age of 53 he had 94 patents. He was family oriented and had a wife and kids who unfortunately died before him. He founded the Bettendorf Axle company with his brother who he was very close to. He was popular and well known in his town. The city of Bettendorf was named after him and his brother and the local community knew them well and he was popular. He married more than once due to his first wife dying before him. His company was fast growing and very successful. He had 2 children called Etta and Henry. He died at the age of 52 and was American
