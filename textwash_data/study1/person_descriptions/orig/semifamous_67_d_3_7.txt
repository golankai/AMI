Board of Education Supreme Court case, as well as for his Impeachment: A Handbook, which provided a trustworthy analysis of the law of impeachment during the Watergate scandal.

A graduate of the University of Texas in Austin in 1935 he went on to obtain a Masters in English and received his LLB from Yale.  Whilst working at Yale as a Professor of Law he taught Hilary Clinton.  

He was against the death penalty and a book on the subject. He was interested in race and the civil rights movement and attended a performance by Louis Armstrong whilst still a freshman in college.
