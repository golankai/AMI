Mick Jagger is a British rock star from the 1980's from the band The Rolling Stones. He is aging now but looks like he still lives  the life of a rock star. He is famous for his big lips. He has developed other interests including acting and film production. He was knighted for his services to music.
Mick Jagger went to university in London and studied at LSE. His most recognisable hit song is Angie. He dated Marianne Faithful and married Bianca but later divorced and now has a new long term partner. He has had a life long friendship with his also famous guitarist Keith Richards.
Mick Jagger has been prolific in producing music and children - fathering eight of them.
