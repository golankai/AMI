Malcolm Dunstan, born 14th October 1950 was a cricketer in English Minor Counties Championships throughout the late 1960s, 1970s amd 1980s. He was born in Cornwall and started playing county cricket for Cornwall in 1969. Dunstan batted right handed and had a right arm medium bowl. He played in the 1970 Gillette Cup which led him to play for Gloucestershire in twelve first class matches. While with Gloucestershire he continue to represent Cornwall in minor counties championships.
