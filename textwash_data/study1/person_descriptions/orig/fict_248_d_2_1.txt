Dr Brantley Ratke was born in Sweden on Christmas Day in 1983. He read history at University of Gothenberg, graduating in 2006, before travelling to Princeton to complete a PhD in Classical Civilizations. Specialising in Ancient Egypian artifacts, Ratke accepted a job at The Museum of Mediterranean and Near Eastern Antiquities in his native Stockholm after graduating. He was promoted to Museum Education Officer in 2015 and has reinvigorated the department leading to a large increase in bookings from local schools. Ratke is currently single, though has taken part in Sweden's version of the TV dating show, 'Take Me Out', in the past; he was unsuccessful in being selected for a date. He currently resides with a close friend in one of Stockholm's trendier suburbs.
