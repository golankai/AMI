A British actress who played a major role in the Harry Potter film series as Hermione Granger. She was born in Paris, France and attended Brown University in the United States before completing her education at the University of Oxford. As well as appearing in the Harry Potter series, she has played major roles in many films such as The Perks of Being A Wallflower (2014), The Bling Ring (2013) and Little Women (2019). She is a feminist activist who was an ambassador for 'heforshe', a UN-led initiative for greater male solidarity with women worldwide. She describes herself as 'self-partnered', being a single woman who doesn't ascribe her relationship status with her self worth.
