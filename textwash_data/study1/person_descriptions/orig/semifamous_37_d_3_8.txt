Aleksandr Gryazin was born on 23 August 1974 and is currently 46 years of age. His hometown is Lyubertsy Russia. Aleksandr is a retired footballer (midfielder and striker), and he used to play for PFC CSKA-d Moscow, Hapoel Bat Yam F.C., FC Krylia Sovetov Samara, FC Saturn Ramenskoy, FC Torpedo-ZIL Moscow, FC Metallurg Lipetsk, FC Rubin Kazan, FC Salyut-Energiya Belgorod and FC Spartak-MZhK Ryazan. Gryazin started his professional career back in 1992, when he started playing for Professional Football Club CSKA (which was the official team of the Soviet Army during the communist era). His height is 1.80 m (5 ft 11 in) and his full name is Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Gryazin.
