Pacocha Was inspired by his mum spirit as she is encouraged him for life following the death of her husband and he wanted to be just like his mum and make her proud so he studied hard at school and went to college and university but to make himself successful, he decided to travel to London England to do a Masters in marketing and law. Whilst at university in London he met his current partner and through this relationship together they developed a business partnership they now both reside in Spain.
