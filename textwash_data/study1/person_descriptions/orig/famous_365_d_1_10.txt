Sam Smith is an English singer/songwriter who rose to fame in 2012 and his songs have been used in a few movies. He is medium height, could be smaller or larger (depending on the year!) with dark blond hair. He has a cute lisp and seems really sweet. He's won 4 grammy awards, 3 Brit awards, 3 billboard music awards, an American music award, a Golden Globe and an Academy Award! Sam Smith identifies as Genderqueer and uses the singular they/them pronoun.
His third album has been delayed because of Covid-19 and is still due to be out sometime this year. 
He quotes his primary influences as Amy Winehouse and Adele, and secondary as Britney Spears, Chaka Khan, Christina Aguilera, Beyonce, Brandy and Lady Gaga. He has also quoted Whitney Houston and Mariah Carey as the greatest R&B voices.
