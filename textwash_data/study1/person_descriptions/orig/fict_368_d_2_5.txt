Ms. Lucia Hamill is currently a Chief Marketing Officer. She was born in 1957 in Dublin. She is married with four children. She went to school in Dublin and then on to University in London, England, where she studied business and economics. After university she travelled the world in the search of adventure, joining in many new hobbies, including water skiing, scuba diving and sky diving. She worked in Australia and New Zealand before returning to Ireland to settle down with her partner.
Lucia has in the past been able to further her other passion in life of scuba diving. She has been to many places in the world to scuba dive, her two favorites being Thailand and Cuba.
