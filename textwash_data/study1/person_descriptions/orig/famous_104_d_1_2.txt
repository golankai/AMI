A ginger-haired English pop star, famous for his folksy-style and hits including Shape of You and Perfect. He is around thirty and recently had a child with his wife, Sherry Seaborn. He has a strange pattern of naming albums after arithmetic operators. His net worth is estimated at a ridiculous $200 million. He is a slightly podgy, bespectacled, bearded man who stands around 5' 11"".
Sheeran grew up in Framlingham, Suffolk, England and was awarded an MBE at some point in his twenties. He ran into some controversy a while back about claims he was couch surfing in his teenage years but was instead believed to have been living relatively comfortably with his parents.
