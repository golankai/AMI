Clyde Fernandes is an Indian soccer player. He plays in the second division of the Indian Football league.  Clyde is 26 years old. He was born in Mumbai, Maharahtra. Clyde is 1.82 metres tall. He professionally debuted at Mumbai Football Club. He went on to play for his current team Ozone FC. He made seven appearances for Mumbai Football Club and scored no goal. This partly owes to his position on the field as a defense enforcer. He has been marked as a potential stalwart on the Indian foot ball scene. He is stable in the first quarter and often pulls forward in midfield pursuits and fourth quarter advances, under suitable circumstances.
