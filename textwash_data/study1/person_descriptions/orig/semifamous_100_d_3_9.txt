Walter Knauss Chorn or also known as Walter K. Chorn was born in the midwest state of Missouri on the 21st January 1885. Walter was a very talented and multifaceted individual who played college football throughout his education, then became a lawyer and was also an insurance superintendent.
Walter started his sporting career as a college football player playing as linebacker first in Central College (now Central Methodist University) in Fayette, Missouri. He then moved on with his football career to Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee where he played as a guard and tackle.
Walter K. Chorn passed away on the 26th February 1933.
