Irvin Woodberry ""Chester"" Brooks lived from June 5, 1891 â€“ February 4, 1966. He played American baseball player. He was mistakenly referred to as Chester Brooks in some reference books which also incorrectly had him listed as coming form the Bahamas. In reality, he came from Key West, Florida. He was an all-rounder and played many positions in his career including pitcher, infielder and outfielder. He devoted all his career to playing with the Brooklyn Royal Giants, and was renowned for his hitting ability. Cumberland Posey who owned the Homestead Grays stated that Brooks was his favourite player of all time.
