Mick Jagger is the lead singer of the Rolling Stones.  He grew up in London and wrote many songs with the Stones guitarist Keith Richards.  Following global stardom he went on to have a string of relationships and marriages with a variety of women and became almost as notorious for this as for his music.  His relationships include Carly Simon, Jerry Hall and many other famous women of the period. Mick Jagger is now in his 70's and still tours with the Rolling Stones regularly, filling stadiums across the globe.  He enjoys the status of rock legend and is often idolised by contemporary performers. He was also famous for his dance moves, which were quite bold during the 1960's and maintains these dance moves when performing live today
