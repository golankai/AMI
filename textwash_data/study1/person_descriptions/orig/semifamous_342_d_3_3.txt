Yassine El Ghanassy is a Belgian footballer who plays left wing. He was born on 12 July 1990 and started playing football when he was 15. El Ghanassy was signed by the youth team La Louviere and spent som etime playing but left for Gent soon after. Although initially being in the youth team he played some games in the Jupiler Pro League. His first goal was scored in the Belgian league playoffs in 2009 against Zulte Waregem. Since that goal he has become more popular. On 11 July 2012 West Bromwich Albion confirmed the loan signing of El Ghanassy from Gent. He signed a season long deal with an option to make the move permanent. He scored his first goal for West Bromwich Albion against Yeovil in the league cup on 28 August 2012.
