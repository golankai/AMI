Daniel Radcliffe is a British actor born in London. His most famous role is as Harry Potter(the main character) in the Harry Potter franchise. A beloved star adored by many. After completing Harry Potter franchise he tried diversifying his career in order to be known for other movies, not only Harry Potter. Those movies include The Woman in Black(2012), What if(2013), Tv series A Young Doctor's Notebook & Other Stories,, Viktor Frankenstein, Trainwreck, The Gamechangers  and Guns Akimbo.(For full filmography, visit imdb.com). Another interesting fact about him is that he keeps close friendship with his co-stars from Harry Potter movies Rupert Grint and Emma Watson.
