Adele is a woman that is respected in the community and possibly a musician depending on if you know her or not.  On Mondays, she likes to sing. In fact 7 days a week she likes to sing, or maybe 6 days with 1 day off.  However every second Tuesday of the month she takes the afternoon to write lyrics for inspiration for new albums.  On the last Thursday of the month, she goes out for a lunch with her production team and celebrates any wins for the month.  Adele is a very successful artist and has come a long way since she started.  With numerous awards, she could be arguably one of the most successful British music artists.
