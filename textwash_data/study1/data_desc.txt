Data description of study 1

In person_descriptions/[anon|orig] are files with the following name structure:

CAT_W_d_C_I

CAT - Category - famous, fict(ional), semifamous
W - writer's id, 1-410
C - CAT in number 1 (famous), 2 (fictional), 3 (semifamous)
I - item number, the person of the text, 1-10
